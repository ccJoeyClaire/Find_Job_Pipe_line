We are currently seeking an experienced professional to join our team in the role of Lead Business Analyst.

Business: Enterprise Technology L3

Principal Responsibilities

The ServiceNow Technical Analyst will:

  • Provide Business Analysis for Servco initiatives.
  • Asses the viability of new requests and provide proposals and solutions to the business that meet business requirements, align with IT strategy, and maximise reuse opportunities.
  • Translate business requirements into system requirements using the appropriate terminology to facilitate the progression of a project through elaboration and definition.
  • Demonstrate good analytical skills to ensure business problems are questioned and understood and the solutions are cost effective and timely.
  • Manage IT as a business, articulate and identify non-functional requirements.
  • Ensure delivery of assigned project tasks on time and to budget.
  • Ensure commercial benefits of change are delivered to the organisation.
  • Be a subject matter expert in a technology function and would be required to manage multiple small scale, moderately complex projects.
  • Drive sustainable growth, add sustainable value by looking at opportunities to reduce the complexity of the application where possible.
  • Promote good practice, promote importance of following risks, recognize inefficiencies, and adheres to policies.
  • Support processes for service enhancement that result in improved service, customer satisfaction, and process improvement.
  • Report progress and identify and raise any issues/risks, escalating as appropriate to enable satisfactory resolution.
  • Have relationships across various functions on a peer-to-peer level and will also be required to interact with senior management in the organisation. Also interact with Regional/Global teams primarily within the GCOO function.
  • Establish and maintain good communication and relationships, both internal and external to the Bank as a representative of the analysis domain.
  • This role will report to the ServiceNow Delivery Lead – Colleague Workflow.

Knowledge & Experience/Qualifications
  • Experience of working on ServiceNow is required.
  • Understanding of Agile methodologies and working practices is required.
  • Good knowledge of the core concepts of ServiceNow to include its OOTB functions and customisation capabilities.
  • Experience of system testing with a focus on end-user testing and ensuring traceability between requirements and testcases.
  • Experience in functional or performance testing, writing, and executing test cases.
  • Experience of business process, data and functional process mapping preferred.
  • Able to work under pressure and demonstrate initiative, enthusiasm, and a rapid learning capability.
  • Proven results-driven approach with the ability to take initiatives, handle multiple tasks and shifting priorities and meet deadlines.
  • Experience of forming and maintaining network relationships - solid partner/stakeholder interaction skills
  • Both spoken and written communication skills with experience of adapting style and approach to the audience and message to be delivered.
  • Experience of working knowledge on ServiceNow tool preferred.
  • ServiceNow Certification preferred.
