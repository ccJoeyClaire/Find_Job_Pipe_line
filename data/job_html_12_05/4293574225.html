WHO WE ARE

lululemon is a yoga-inspired technical apparel company up to big things. The practice and philosophy of yoga informs our overall purpose to elevate the world through the power of practice. We are proud to be a growing global company with locations all around the world, from Vancouver to Shanghai, and places in between. We owe our success to our innovative product, our emphasis on our stores, our commitment to our people, and the incredible connections we get to make in every community we are in.

About This Team

The Team is responsible for building up data, planning and analytics capabilities within Retail Operations team, and collaborate within Retail Operations and Retail Performance team, as well as with cross functional partners to turn insights into actions to support business growth in China.

 A DAY IN THE LIFE

  • Passionate about turning numbers into insights that will inform how we run our business
  • Turn complex analysis into simple conclusions and clearly communicate finding in reports and presentations
  • Ability to present findings into a format that is visually appealing and easily digestible to the target audience
  • Strong Excel skills and proficiency in other Office products to present findings into a format that is visually appealing and easily digestible to the target audience
  • Work closely with Global Retail Planning & Optimization team and local FP&A team for retail labor efficiency, take charge in the labor program incl. system optimization, program upgrade and labor hours usage and efficieny
  • Work closely with FP&A as well as Omni Sales Team (EC/Wechat mini program, etc.) to track sales plans
  • Support to build financial modelling to forecast the quantitative impact of pilots and strategic projects, including projected return on investment
  • Support the team and cross-functional partners with store & guest insights/analysis
  • Work closely as a community with Business Planning & Operations, Tech, and other counterparts in various function to build up data & analytics awareness across the company
  • Participate in cross-functional initiatives to provide financial analysis, decision and business case support

Qualifications
  • 5+ years experience in retail and relevant roles
  • University graduates preferably in statistics, business, operations, or related discipline
  • Advanced level Excel skills (pivot tables, v-lookups, macros)
  • Strong ability to work independently and cross-functionally
  • Excellent written and verbal communications skill in both Mandarin and English
  • Proficient in Windows based software (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)

 MUST HAVES

  • Leads with courage, knowing the possibility of greatness is bigger than the fear of failure
