职位简介

The Company

Phibro Animal Health Corporation (PAHC) is a publicly traded, global organization with a rich history in animal health and nutrition as a manufacturer and marketer of medicated feed additives, vaccines, nutritional specialty products, and mineral nutrition. We strive to be a trusted partner with livestock producers, farmers, veterinarians, and consumers who raise or care for farm and companion animals by providing solutions to help them maintain and enhance the health of their animals.


Phibro is a diversified company. In addition to animal nutrition and health, we manufacture and market products for ethanol performance, microbials for plants, home and industrial applications, and specialty chemicals for various industrial markets.


Phibro’s revenues are in excess of $1 billion and are supported by over 2,300 employees worldwide.


Asia Pacific is a strategic area of focus and growth for PAHC.



The Challenge

PAHC seeks to hire a strong Business Intelligence Manager to lead the development, implementation, and optimization of CRM and marketing analytics projects across the Asia Pacific Region covering four sub-regions: Greater China, South East Asia, Australia and New Zealand, and South Central African Region. The role will focus on leveraging data-driven insights to enhance customer engagement, improve sales forecasting, optimize marketing campaigns, and support strategic business decisions.


Major responsibilities:

CRM Project Management:
  • Lead the end-to-end implementation of the CRM project, from requirements gathering and system configuration to user training and post-implementation support.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including IT, Sales, Marketing, Finance, HR and Customer Service, to ensure seamless integration and alignment of the CRM system with business needs.
  • Define and document CRM project scope, goals, deliverables, and timelines.
  • Manage vendor relationships and ensure that project milestones and deliverables are met.


Data Analysis & Reporting:
  • Develop and maintain dashboards and reports that provide insights into customer behavior, sales performance, and marketing effectiveness.
  • Analyze large datasets to identify trends, opportunities, and areas for improvement in CRM and marketing activities.
  • Provide actionable recommendations based on data analysis to support business growth and customer engagement strategies.


Marketing Analytics & Strategy:
  • Work closely with the marketing team to design and implement data-driven marketing strategies, including segmentation, targeting, and campaign performance analysis.
  • Collaborate with marketing teams to achieve successful execution and optimization of marketing projects across the region.
  • Measure the ROI of marketing campaigns and provide insights to optimize future initiatives.
  • Support the development of regional marketing strategies by providing data-driven insights into market trends and customer behavior.


Stakeholder Management:
  • Serve as the primary point of contact for CRM and marketing analytics in the APAC region.
  • Communicate project status, insights, and recommendations to senior management and key stakeholders.
  • Ensure alignment of BI and CRM initiatives with overall business goals and objectives


Technical Expertise and Leadership:
  • Utilize advanced BI tools (e.g., Power BI) to analyze and visualize data.
  • Ensure data integrity and accuracy across all CRM and BI systems.
  • Collaborate with IT to ensure the BI infrastructure supports business requirements and scalability.
  • Provide functional guidance and support to internal customers on using CRM and BI tools to continuously improve work effectiveness and productivity.
  • Provide training and development opportunities to enhance individual and collective capabilities in CRM and marketing analytics.
  • Foster a culture of continuous improvement and innovation within APAC Region.


Continuous Learning & Passion for Information Systems:
  • Stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in business intelligence, CRM, and marketing analytics.
  • Demonstrate a proactive approach to learning new tools, methodologies, and systems to improve business outcomes.
  • Share knowledge and insights with the APAC team to drive innovation and best practices.


This position will be based in China or South East Asia, reporting directly to the Marketing Director Asia Pacific Region.


The Profile

The right candidate shall have a strong background in business intelligence, CRM systems, data analytics, and project management, along with a passion for continuous learning and information systems.
  • Education: Bachelor’s degree in Business, Marketing, Data Science, Information Systems, or a related field. A master’s degree or relevant certification is a plus.

Experience:
  • Minimum 5 years’ experience in CRM management, business intelligence, or data analytics
  • Proven experience in managing CRM implementation projects and marketing analytics in a multi-country environment, preferably within the APAC region.

Skills:
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to interpret complex data and translate it into actionable business insights.
  • Proficiency in BI tools (Power BI), CRM systems (Microsoft Dynamics 365 or Salesforce), Excel
  • Strong communication and presentation skills, with the ability to engage and influence stakeholders at all levels.
  • Fluency in both English and Chinese is required.
  • Ability to work effectively and manage multiple priorities.

Personal Attributes:
  • Passion for information systems and continuous learning
  • Enjoys problem-solving and has a proactive approach to finding solutions.
  • Strong team player who thrives in a collaborative, dynamic, and fast-paced work environment
  • Excellent interpersonal skills with the ability to build positive relationships across departments, countries and regions.
  • Flexible and adaptable to changing business needs and priorities.
  • Proficiency in additional languages relevant to the APAC region is a plus.
