职位简介

Job Title: Strategic Operations

Core responsibilities

1. Responsible for the daily operation of the strategy trading module, and formulate and implement user growth strategies, including new user acquisition, retention, and re-engagement;

2. Collect user feedback, cooperate with the product and technical teams, and promote the optimization and iteration of product functions to increase the product usage rate and Customer Satisfaction Score;

3. Formulate a complete strategic marketing plan, transform complex strategic products into marketing content that users can easily understand, and improve the conversion rate through cases and data lake visualization;

4. Explore potential strategic partners, establish a strategic supplier database, introduce high-quality third-party strategies to enrich the platform ecosystem, design a strategy sharing mechanism, and enhance the cooperation stickiness of strategy providers;

5. Monitor the data related to strategy transactions, regularly output analysis reports and propose optimization suggestions, track the industry product trends and the movements of competing products, and timely adjust the operation strategies to maintain a competitive advantage

6. Explore user behavior characteristics through Data Analysis and optimize the user stratification operation strategy;

Job requirements

1. More than 3 years of experience in the cryptocurrency industry, and more than 2 years of experience in operations related to contract trading and strategic trading;

2. Have excellent experience in user growth and product optimization, and be familiar with operation methods such as user acquisition, retention, and re-engagement;

3. Possessing strong communication skills and teamwork spirit, and being able to efficiently promote XFN cooperation;

4. Have excellent marketing thinking, and be proficient in packaging and marketing strategy;5. Result-oriented, with strong stress resistance and execution ability;Contract strategy - for example, grid strategy, CTA strategy
