  • Be responsible for establishing good communication with airlines, collecting and sorting information on airlines latest flights, freight rates, and capacity changes to customer service colleagues, and properly handling cargo position matching;

  • Work independently , passionate attitude to work;
  • Good at English writing and speaking;
  • Excellent communication skill.
