职位简介

We are looking for a motivated and ambitious intern to join our team in Shanghai.


This internship offers hands-on experience in business operations, e-commerce, project coordination and digital marketing, supporting our Spanish leadership in China and collaborating with cross-border teams.


It is an excellent opportunity for students or recent graduates who want to build solid experience in the digital and international business field.


Tareas

  • Assist in day-to-day business operations, coordination and follow-up of ongoing projects.

  • Support e-commerce tasks across platforms such as Tmall, JD_com, Xiaohongshu and Douyin (training provided).

  • Conduct basic market research, competitor checks, and prepare simple analytical briefs.

  • Help prepare presentations, reports and documentation for internal and client meetings.

  • Contribute to communication and coordination between our China and Europe teams.

  • Assist with basic data checks, content reviews and operational tasks required to keep projects running smoothly.

  • Handle occasional logistics and administrative support tasks linked to project execution.


Requisitos

  • University student or recent graduate (Business, Marketing, International Trade, Communications, or similar).

  • Strong English communication skills (spoken and written).

  • High motivation, curiosity and willingness to learn.

  • Organised, reliable and comfortable managing multiple small tasks.

  • Good attention to detail and a proactive mindset.

  • Interest in e-commerce, digital marketing, China market, or cross-border business.

  • Ability to work in an international, fast-moving environment.


Nice to Have

  • Familiarity with Chinese digital platforms (Tmall, JD, Xiaohongshu, Douyin), even at a basic level.

  • Experience in student projects, part-time roles or internships related to marketing, operations or digital business.

  • Basic PowerPoint and Excel skills.


Beneficios

  • A high-learning-curve internship with exposure to real, high-impact projects.

  • Direct collaboration with international teams and senior professionals.

  • Hands-on experience in e-commerce operations, digital marketing and business coordination.

  • Training and mentorship from experienced team members.

  • Possibility of transitioning to a full-time position depending on performance and business needs.

  • Internship compensation aligned with local market standards for Shanghai.
