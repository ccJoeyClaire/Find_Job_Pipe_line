Database Administrator

BIT Exchange · 中国 (远程办公)

职位简介

ABOUT US


BIT is a full-featured cryptocurrency exchange that is designed for use by professionals, with advanced risk management and fund efficiency supported by Portfolio Margin and Unified Margin.


BIT was launched in August 2020 as a spinoff from Matrixport, a prominent crypto financial service company. BIT is one of the top crypto options trading platforms which offers innovative USD-margined trading pairs.


BIT is always striving to widen its selection of tokens, enhance its trading tools, and inspire the creation of innovative financial products.



ABOUT THE ROLE


Responsibilities:
  • Responsible for the operation and maintenance of databases such as MySQL, Redis, and ClickHouse, including performance tuning, troubleshooting, and general support.
  • Collaborate with development teams to design and implement database storage solutions that meet business needs.
  • Continuously learn and introduce new technologies and tools to support business growth.
  • Optimize cloud resource utilization to reduce operational costs.

Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree or above in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • 2+ years of experience as a MySQL DBA.
  • Familiar with MySQL and Redis operations and internal principles.
  • Experience with AWS cloud platform is a plus.
  • Experience in maintaining data warehouse products such as ClickHouse, Doris, or Kafka is a plus.
  • Proficiency in Python or Shell scripting is a plus.
