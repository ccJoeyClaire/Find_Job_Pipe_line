 Data Specialist

Responsibilities

  • Develop globally standardized and automated Energy Efficiency data analysis tools for all relevant as well as new applications and provide them to sales colleagues, e.g. Heavy Industry. Promote Energy Efficiency as a Klüber Product.
  • Evaluate and analyze metrological data, derive conclusions and find value selling arguments for the use of Klüber specialty lubricants.
  • Formulate internal information sheets, Energy Efficiency data analysis leaflets and Value Case Studies.
  • Develop and establish our Beyond Lubrication activities towards sensors and related data analysis. Support sales/Application Engineering/Product Management to create a product for promotion.
  • Interface with the internal structure to understand and apply necessary technical adaptations and upgrades for statistical data analysis.
  • Intensive collaboration with internal (especially IT and Analytics) and external partners as well as customers.
  • Travel to customers to support projects regarding technical knowledge for data analysis.
  • Prepare and hold analysis trainings in all relevant specialist areas e.g. internal and ex-ternal customer trainings, specialist forums, etc.
  • Evaluate ongoing market and competitive analyses to adapt internal processes.
  • Operational planning for the area of responsibility

Qualifications

  • Master Degree in the field of statistics, data science, engineering, natural science or related fields;
  • Working experience preferably with a focus on Sustainability Studies, Environmental Engineering and Renewable Energies;
  • Advanced working experience in multivariate data analysis (e.g. regression analysis) and code based statistical software (e.g. R, Python);
  • Profound experience in lubricant sensors and analysis of the relevant data is a plus;
  • High level of problem-solving skills.
  • Good communication skills both in English and Chinese, excellent verbal English;
  • Sales-oriented mindset, Adaptable, and highly analytic thinking;
  • Willing to travel (approx. 20% of working time), enjoy international and intercultural communication as well as cross functional cooperation with other departments in the company;
  • Ability to work in a team.
