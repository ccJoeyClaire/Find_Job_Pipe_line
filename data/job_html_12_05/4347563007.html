职位简介

Sandvik Mining Rock Tools PU Wuxi is seeking a Financial Analyst.

The position would directly report to the Finance & Admin Manager, below please refer to the job description:

Key performance areas:

  • Adhere to all Sandvik EHS policies and procedures.
  • Be responsible for internal control, execute the biannual test, monitor the actual process in the entity to meet group internal control requirements.
  • Financial analysis through budget / forecast/ actuals by product account, division, or business unit, to dig out the cost variability and improve the cost efficiency.
  • As the owner of Compliance activities, to follow up Enterprise Risk Management, Business Contingency Plan and entity compliance test.
  • Company execution plan coordinator and follow up, monthly update and present the result to management team.
  • Responsible for investment financial evaluation, fixed assets management, CIP follow up, tangible asset impairment.
  • Month closing coordinator, to ensure the closing activities meet the timeline, review monthly TB, PRC report, local statutory and regulatory reporting.
  • Assist external and internal audit, follow up and close all the findings and deviations.
  • Assist quarterly forecast and yearly budget, input for global reporting.
  • Back up of cost accountant.
  • Other tasks assigned by manager.

Your profile:
  • Gender/Age: No limit.
  • Language Skill: Fluent Mandarin and English.
  • Diploma / Degree: Bachelor or above.
  • Major: Accounting or equivalent.
  • PC skills, good presentation and advanced excel skills.
  • Strong communication skills and teamwork spirit.
  • Must understand business process for the manufacturing company.
  • At least 5 years' working experience in a multi-national enterprise with manufacturing environment, min. 3+ years costing and finance analysis experience.

As a person you are proactive, self-motivated and one with lot of initiative, courage and integrity and a business-minded, result-oriented attitude. You also have strong communication skills and can work in an English environment.
