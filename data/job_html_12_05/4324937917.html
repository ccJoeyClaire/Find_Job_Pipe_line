AI Product Manager

深信服科技 · 中国 广东省 深圳 (现场办公)

职位简介

Job Responsibilities

  • Lead the strategy and product planning for Cloud BG’s AI products, driving the product lifecycle from incubation to maturity to ensure business success and competitive differentiation.
  • Track and research the latest technologies and trends in AI cloud infrastructure and large model application development; continuously improve and optimize products to enhance user experience and strengthen competitiveness.
  • Support the planning teams (including product management and delivery teams) in producing scenario-based solutions, go-to-market materials, and standardized delivery assets.
  • Assist the operations team in formulating market promotion strategies, empower the sales and channel ecosystem, and proactively follow up on market and customer feedback to close the loop in product planning.

Job Requirements
  • Background in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, or related fields is preferred.
  • Over 5 years of experience in the AI domain, with more than 3 years leading AI product lines in large cloud computing companies/AI firms/industry software vendors, with proven commercial-scale delivery.
  • Deep understanding and hands-on experience in large model training, inference, and application development; experience with large-model applications, related platforms, or AI cloud infrastructure is a strong plus. Experience in leading or working at high-growth AI startups is also preferred.
  • Long-term experience in market intelligence and strategic planning, with strong technical insight, market awareness, and business acumen; able to form deep perspectives and judgments on the future development of the AI industry.
  • Strong teamwork and communication skills; proactive, fast learner, innovative, accountable, and driven by a strong desire to build impactful products.

Final job level and compensation will be determined based on interview performance.
