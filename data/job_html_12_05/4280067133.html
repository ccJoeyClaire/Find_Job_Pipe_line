Manager, Business Analysis and Insight, Enablement(跨境电商广告分析方向)

Walmart Marketplace · 中国 广东省 深圳 (现场办公)

职位简介

Key Responsibilities:

1. Analyzes and ensures accuracy of information by creating, maintaining, and evaluating of data; comparing data with third-party information, budgets, or actual results; analyzing and researching risks or compliance concerns; analyzing financial data, key performance indicators, and metrics; evaluating trends and reviewing results for accuracy; utilizing financial models, plans, and tools; and conducting analyses for special and recurring projects.

2. Coordinates, completes, and oversees job-related activities and assignments by developing and maintaining relationships with key stakeholders; supporting plans and initiatives to meet customer and business needs; identifying and communicating goals and objectives; building accountability for and measuring progress in achieving results; identifying and addressing improvement opportunities; and demonstrating adaptability and promoting continuous learning.

3. Be with the business teams to develop an in-depth understanding of the business. Build trust and partnership with key stakeholders within the business teams especially team leaders (Senior Directors and Directors). Be as an industrial BI expert for this business segment with quick access and clear understanding of using both internal and external data.

4. Understand what’s available from 3rd party data sources in the market (i.e., SimilarWeb, eMarketer) and develop strategic perspectives on where to build data/BI capabilities.

5. Provides data-driven analysis and insights to help support business strategies by evaluating ongoing analytical needs; reporting data-based insights in a uniform manner; reporting on business strategies; collaborating with internal and external business partners to build a consistent and accurate strategy for using data-driven insights; and providing expert advice and guidance on the application of best practices.

6. Provides supervision and development opportunities for associates by hiring and training; mentoring; assigning duties; providing recognition; and ensuring diversity awareness.

7. Provides market insights to key stakeholders based on research gathered and business knowledge by communicating data insights and global marketplace trends affecting the business using various communication tools; providing guidance and advising on next steps based on insights, objectives, and research; proposing improvements; and promoting the application of insights to support other departmental and leadership decisions and goals.

8. Provides customer service and reporting in area of responsibility by providing reports and communicating results, preparing information and supporting documentation; supporting key business processes; serving as an information resource; providing guidance and recommendations based on defined policies and practices; supporting relationships with key stakeholders to deliver solutions and support; and resolving issues, answering questions, obtaining information, and providing documentation.

9. Manages customer requests and needs by utilizing analytical tools; extracting and consolidating relevant data for reporting; determining layout and formatting; analyzing best delivery methods; identifying root cause and solutions in all supported systems.

Qualifications:

1.Bachelor's degree business, Analytics, Statistics, or related field and 2 years’ experience in data analytics, project management, business, or related area OR 4 years' experience in data analytics, project management, business, or related area.

2.Proven experience in data analysis, preferably within the e-Commerce or related industry. 3.Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

4.Proficiency in data analysis tools, language and software (e.g., Excel, SQL, Python, R, or similar). 5.Excellent communication and presentation skills.

6.Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

7.Attention to detail and strong organizational skills.

8.Knowledge of industry-specific regulations and compliance requirements.
