职位简介

Job Responsibilities:

  • Analyze industry talent trends and develop targeted talent attraction strategies.
  • Build and maintain a high-end talent network within the quantitative finance industry.
  • Collaborate with quantitative teams to precisely match candidates with role requirements.
  • Participate in recruitment strategy analysis and process optimization to enhance hiring efficiency.


Qualifications & Requirements:
  • 1-3 years of recruiting experience (agency or in-house), preferably in quantitative finance. Experience leading hiring for AI/Algorithm roles is a plus.
  • Basic understanding of or strong interest in quantitative trading, algorithmic strategies, etc. Background in Finance/Mathematics/Computer Science preferred.
  • Strong market insight and network-building capabilities.
  • Excellent logical thinking, communication, and coordination skills.


Preferred Attributes:
  • Familiarity with the competitive landscape of quant talent (e.g., hedge funds, HFT firms).
  • Ability to articulate complex technical roles to candidates (e.g., alpha research, HFT system development).
