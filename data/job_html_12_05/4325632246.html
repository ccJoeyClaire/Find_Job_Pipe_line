Mission:

We are looking for a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Test & Support Engineer to join our technical team. The role focuses on executing testing and debugging activities, supporting product qualification and verification, and ensuring smooth communication with customers.


ACTIVITIES


  • Execute testing and debugging tasks using test devices and tools.
  • Prepare clear and accurate test reports for internal and customer use.
  • Perform product qualification and verification activities according to defined standards.
  • Provide technical support and identifying and resolving issues efficiently.
  • Manage customer communications related to technical matters, ensuring timely updates and problem resolution.
  • Collaborate with internal teams to support continuous improvement of testing processes and tools.


JOB EXPERIENCE


  • 1–2 years of experience in software or hardware testing, technical support, or related IT/Software roles.
  • Experience with debugging, test tools, or technical troubleshooting is a plus but not mandatory.


KNOWLEDGE & SKILLS


  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Information Systems, Electronics, or related field.
  • Ability to write clear, structured technical reports.
  • Strong communication skills to interact with customers and internal teams.
  • Proactive, analytical, and detail-oriented mindset.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision.
  • Languages: Mandarin and English (spoken and written).


Contract: Fixed term contract
