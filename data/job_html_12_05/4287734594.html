职位简介

Position Description

  • Oversee the end-to-end project lifecycle for national business center initiatives, proactively resolve bottlenecks, and ensure delivery objectives are met.
  • Drive the implementation of key project processes, continuously optimize workflows, and improve overall efficiency.
  • Establish mechanisms to enhance collaboration among project stakeholders, and institutionalize lessons learned and best practices.
  • Participate in designing and executing project plans to ensure successful implementation.

Job Requirements
  • Familiarity with the cross-border e-commerce industry; at least 2 years of project management experience.
  • Proficient in at least one project management tool (e.g., Jira, Asana, Trello) with proven case studies of practical application; strong data-driven analysis and insight capabilities.
  • Excellent time management, prioritization skills, and ability to efficiently organize meetings.
  • Strong initiative, learning agility, and communication/collaboration skills; proactive in identifying collaboration issues, quick to adapt to new knowledge and environmental changes; prior experience in project mechanism & culture operations is a plus.
  • Project management professional certification (e.g., PMP, ACP) is preferred.
  • Fluent in English, capable of simultaneous interpretation in meetings, and proficient in writing/translation of work documents; overseas study or work experience is a plus.
