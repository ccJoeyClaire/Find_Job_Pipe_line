职位简介

Location: Hong Kong / Shenzhen

About Look Capital

Look Capital is a top-tier multi-family office serving ultra-high-net-worth technology founders and family enterprises across Asia.

We run a research-driven global allocation platform with a strong focus on hedge fund selection, manager due diligence, and multi-asset portfolio construction.

Role Overview

We are seeking an experienced Hedge Fund Due Diligence Analyst / Senior Analyst to join our Alternatives team.

The role focuses on manager research, investment due diligence (IDD), operational due diligence (ODD), and continuous monitoring of global hedge fund managers.

You will work closely with the CIO and Investment Committee, contribute to investment memos, and help shape our global hedge fund portfolio.

Key Responsibilities
  • Conduct investment due diligence (IDD) on hedge fund managers across strategies (Equity L/S, Macro, CTA, RV, Multi-Strategy, Credit, etc.)
  • Perform operational due diligence (ODD) including governance, risk, compliance, and operational infrastructure review
  • Build and maintain the hedge fund manager pipeline, including sourcing new managers
  • Analyze track records, portfolio exposures, attribution, risk metrics, and factor sensitivities
  • Prepare investment memos and present findings to the Investment Committee
  • Conduct ongoing monitoring of approved managers, including regular calls, onsite meetings, and risk review
  • Collaborate with global fund managers, prime brokers, administrators, and service providers
  • Support multi-asset portfolio construction from a hedge fund sleeve perspective

Qualifications
  • 3–7+ years of experience in Hedge Fund Research / IDD / ODD / Fund Selection / FoF / Allocator roles
  • Strong understanding of hedge fund strategies and risk frameworks
  • Experience interacting with hedge fund managers and operational teams
  • Solid analytical skills: performance attribution, exposure analysis, factor models, return decomposition
  • Excellent communication skills and the ability to write high-quality investment memos
  • Fluent English (Mandarin preferred)

Why Join Us
  • Exposure to top-tier global hedge fund managers
  • Work directly with CIO and Investment Committee
  • True multi-asset family office environment, not a product-driven platform
  • High degree of ownership and autonomy
  • Flat culture and fast decision-making
  • Opportunity to shape a world-class hedge fund portfolio for UHNW families
