职位简介

岗位职责:


The offshore analyst will support a bulge-bracket international investment bank's equity research team with specific sector focus:

1. Gathering and analyzing financial data on covered companies under specific sector from a variety of sources, including company reports, the internet, online databases and proprietary databases e.g. WIND, Bloomberg, etc.

2. Updating and maintaining industry/sector databases using proprietary databases and the internet research (industry association websites, govt. websites, third-party industry data providers, etc.)

3. Working on daily/ weekly/ monthly news highlights and reports (updating key charts/graphs, preparing news summary and insights)

4. Performing research on companies with significant price movement, try to get key news/events leading to this movement, call company IR, if required, etc.

5. Following the earning calendar of the companies, listening, and summarizing earnings conference calls, and updating the financial models and reports accordingly

6. Building, maintaining, and analyzing financial models of companies in the given industry/sector but for different markets e.g. A-share, H-share, TW, etc. Additionally, doing earnings adjustments, etc. and helping client team with publishing models.

7. Writing factual sections and update content (text, tables, and charts) for company and industry reports

8. Helping client team on some basic admin tasks related to updating client contact details and NDR requests


任职要求:


1. Bachelors/Masters with a major of accounting, finance or related

2. Good understanding of finance and accounting (financial statements, its inter-linkages, ratios, etc.)

3. Good financial modeling and valuation skills

4. High attention to details

5. Good written and spoken English, Chinese skills; should be able to express thoughts in a logical and clear manner

6. Relevant equity research internship or working experience

7. Needs to have positive mindset, able to take complete ownership and responsibility for allocated work, willing to accept challenges, self-motivator and should have strong passion for equity research, and learning and growth
