Operations Specialist – Trade Support

中金公司 · 中国 香港特别行政区 (现场办公)

职位简介

Job Descriptions


Responsibilities
  • Monitor trade processing and ensure an efficient and smooth flow
  • Liaise with business units for trade allocations and process trade confirmation
  • Handle client statements and relevant enquiries
  • Work with compliance and regulatory parties on regulatory reporting, searching and auditing
  • Perform reconciliations and clear breaks
  • Maintain close coordination with relevant parties such as traders, RMs, Finance, external brokers and counterparties for operation update and transaction follow up
  • Follow established procedures and perform appropriate controls while exercising good judgment in managing risk and following escalation procedures
  • Participate in system enhancement and ad-hoc projects related to operations environment and internal control
  • Provide basic trouble shooting solutions and issues identification
  • Fulfill other assigned responsibilities and duties as required by supervisors to meet operational needs


Products Covered
  • Products Covered: Cash Equities (including but not limited to Hong Kong, China Stock Connect and United States), P-notes, Bonds, Fund, Structured Products (e.g., Linked Note, OTC-Warrant), ETF, Private Equity etc.

Knowledges
  • Previous exposure to DTCC CTM/ALERT is preferred
  • Market knowledge on cash equities in Hong Kong, China Stock Connect, United States, Singapore, and other global markets. Product knowledge in equity derivative products and/or fixed income is an advantage


Skills and Abilities
  • Fluent in spoken and written English and Mandarin
  • Resourceful, attention to detail and able to multitask in a fast-paced environment
  • Must be detail-oriented and able to work under pressure
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Good time management skills
  • Strong in Microsoft Excel, Word and PowerPoint


Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree - concentration in Accounting, Finance, Business or Economics is preferred
  • 2 - 3 years relevant work experience
