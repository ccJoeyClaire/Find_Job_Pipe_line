Marketing Science Specialist

Snap Inc. · 中国 北京市 北京市

职位简介

Snap Inc is a technology company. We believe the camera presents the greatest opportunity to improve the way people live and communicate. Snap contributes to human progress by empowering people to express themselves, live in the moment, learn about the world, and have fun together. The Company’s three core products are Snapchat, a visual messaging app that enhances your relationships with friends, family, and the world; Lens Studio, an augmented reality platform that powers AR across Snapchat and other services; and its AR glasses, Spectacles.

We’re looking for a Marketing Science Specialist for E-commerce based in China to join our Measurement Team at Snap Inc. We are a team of scientists and researchers who work in a highly collaborative environment, using our suite of tools and ad products to improve the performance of Snap's advertising platform and drive increased value for our customers.

What you’ll do:

  • As an individual contributor, you will be responsible to provide strategic measurement advice to internal and external stakeholders while being hands on on measurement study set up and execution
  • Partner with the China Sales team to develop and execute the market growth strategy along with quarterly goals rooted in sound consumer research, advertiser performance, and industry knowledge
  • Be the primary driver of performance improvement across our top advertisers leveraging a mix of learning-agenda strategies, experimental design, causal analytics, and ads efficacy solution testing in the China market
  • Lead measurement, research, and learning plans for key clients in support of their media objectives and the long-term growth of the partnership
  • Develop a measurement strategy, prioritization, and roadmap for ads measurement and insights products for the E-commerce vertical
  • Work closely with sales and marketing teams to identify, test, and scale new ad products, features and solutions that have a positive impact on advertiser value
  • Lead the scoping and development of custom multi-study meta analysis in partnership with Data Science and Product teams to understand the relative impact of different marketing strategies across digital platforms and media
  • Build measurement capability and advocacy across your vertical and with cross-functional partners

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities:
  • Ability to structure and conduct analyses to generate insight and recommendations
  • Ability to influence senior leadership through presentation of clear and concise insights and recommendations
  • Understanding of measurement concepts, solutions, and underlying statistical fundamentals leveraged in the ads efficacy and measurement ecosystem
  • Subject matter expertise in at least one of the following areas and familiarity with both; experimental design (sig testing, power calculation, sample design) or econometrics modeling
  • Expertise with at least one of the advanced analytical tools (e.g. SQL, R, Python)
  • Access, analyze, interpret, and communicate ads performance insights using a wide range of standard data science tooling
  • Understanding of the market needs and ability to creatively apply measurement solutions and insights in a way that improves advertiser performance and the value-proposition of Snap
  • A strong understanding of the digital advertising and measurement ecosystem

Minimum Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in a quantitative or business field or equivalent years of experience
  • 3-5 years of advanced analytics and measurement experience within a technology company, media agency, consulting firm, advertiser, or research company
  • Experience with ads measurement in user growth and acquisition or eCommerce

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Advanced degree in business, math, statistics, economics, engineering or a related quantitative field
  • Direct work experience with top advertisers, tech publishers within the China market
  • Experience building and influencing client or partner relationships
