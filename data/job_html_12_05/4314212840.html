职位简介

About the Company

Noah Holdings is Asia's leading independent wealth management institution, listed on both Hong Kong and US stock exchanges. The company specializes in providing global financial portfolio management and comprehensive wealth planning services to high-net-worth individuals. We have accumulated over 20 years of industry experience and a substantial client base.

Mission Statement

Drive Noah Holdings' digital transformation in the AI era. Through technology enablement, we will reshape our customer acquisition, operations, and service systems, building a new wealth management paradigm centered on "data-driven insights and user experience excellence." This will drive simultaneous growth in business scale and customer loyalty.

Background & Opportunity

As we expand globally, we are fundamentally reimagining how we serve clients. This requires leveraging AI technology to scale services while maintaining the personalized, high-touch experience that defines our brand. The ideal candidate is an analytical team player who will lead the AI product strategy and execution across the organization. You will own the entire product lifecycle from strategy to evaluation, work cross-functionally to complete product roadmaps, and discover new AI opportunities that drive business value.

Core Responsibilities
  • Online Customer Journey Design - Lead the design of end-to-end customer journeys based on behavioral data and financial scenarios. Build a closed-loop product system spanning "prospect identification → personalized engagement → long-term relationship management."
  • AI Application Implementation - Spearhead deployment of AI solutions including intelligent investment advisory, customer segmentation & profiling, and automated marketing tools to improve lead conversion and customer engagement.
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration - Bridge business and technology teams, translating financial expertise into scalable digital solutions. Drive alignment between product vision and technical execution.
  • Agile Product Development - Lead or participate in agile development cycles, ensuring rapid iteration and data-driven decision-making. Establish key performance indicators (customer lifetime value, conversion funnels, retention rates) and use data insights to optimize operations.
  • Business Model Innovation - Explore new online engagement models to reduce reliance on traditional offline channels, improve operational efficiency, and optimize resource utilization.
  • Product Metrics & Analytics - Design and monitor core metrics throughout the customer lifecycle, using data-driven insights to continuously optimize operational strategies and product performance.

Experience & Background
  • 3-8 years of product management or operations experience at leading internet companies or fintech firms, with hands-on experience building or restructuring consumer-facing products from scratch or through major overhauls
  • Finance industry background (wealth management, securities) is a plus; understanding of compliance frameworks and high-net-worth customer dynamics is valuable
  • Global mindset and cross-cultural collaboration capability

Core Competencies
  • User Insight & Design - Proficiency with user research methodologies and customer journey mapping. Ability to precisely identify pain points and design solutions.
  • Data Acumen - SQL/Python proficiency. Capable of independently extracting, analyzing data, and deriving actionable insights.
  • AI & Technology Fluency - Understanding of AI application principles and machine learning fundamentals. Ability to communicate effectively with technical teams about technical approaches.
  • Commercial Mindset - High business acumen focused on ROI and scale. Ability to balance user experience with business growth objectives.
  • Adaptability & Innovation - Willingness to challenge status quo. Comfortable operating in ambiguity and driving change in a traditional industry.

Why Join Noah Holdings

Pioneering Opportunity - Participate in a strategic transformation of a traditional finance company. Direct involvement in company-defining projects with significant business impact.

Strong Support System - Access to integrated customer data, research & investment expertise, dedicated AI technology teams, and committed budget allocation.

Growth Trajectory - Develop horizontally across finance domains while deepening expertise in product and technology innovation. Build a rare combination of financial acumen and digital product excellence.
