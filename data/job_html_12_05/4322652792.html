Reporting to the Sr. Commodity Manager, the Commodity Master Data Clerk role involves…

What a typical day looks like:

  • Individual will be responsible for helping Global Commodity Teams on data maintenance
  • Bann system BI (Buy Information) maintenance/Supplier Code, VMF submit/SRS(supplier rating system) data collection etc.
  • SCA (Standard price maintenance)
  • Assist GCMs in data preparation for QBR meetings / as needed

The experience we’re looking to add to our team:
  • Good at Microsoft Office excel data analysis
  • Good at Bann system, ERP system. SCA system
  • Can read and write English.
  • Spoken English preferred
  • Take total ownership on master data management
