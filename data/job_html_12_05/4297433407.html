职位简介

Your Job

MOLEX is seeking a detail-oriented and contribution minded Accounts Receivable Analyst to join our global Collections Team. This position will engage, challenge, and provide fulfilment to a candidate with initiative and a sense of urgency. If you enjoy teamwork, embrace challenges, and seek mutually beneficial opportunities to drive our business forward, we want to hear from you!

What You Will Do

  • Ensure that customers adhere to established payment terms by monitoring and tracking payment schedules for timely processing.
  • Proactively manage and take ownership of your portfolio of customers, collaborating with internal stakeholders in resolving payment issues.
  • Timely report and escalate when material issues or impacts.
  • Be a teammate. We are a global team of likeminded contribution driven Collectors.
  • Lead or coordinate negotiations related to payments and claims with cross-functions.
  • Coordinate with Invoice team to ensure issue VAT per customer requirements.
  • Continuously seek opportunities through innovation for process improvement and system optimization.


Who You Are (Basic Qualifications)
  • Strong communication skills (both written and verbal) necessary for professional interactions with both external and internal contacts.
  • Ability to proactively engage in a rapidly changing business environment while demonstrating a high level of transparency, accountability, and collaboration.
  • Detail oriented problem solver, quick learner with ability to multi-task.
  • Good English communication skill is a must.


What Will Put You Ahead
  • 1+ years as a collection specialist in an international environment.
  • Possess hands on knowledge of MS office, SAP System and Getpaid tool.
  • Experience with Power BI or other data visualization software/tools.
  • Process Transformation / Process Automation / Process Implementation experience.
