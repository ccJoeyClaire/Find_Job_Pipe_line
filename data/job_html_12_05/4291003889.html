Company Description

Garena is a leading global developer and publisher of online games, known for its popular titles such as Free Fire, which has been the most downloaded mobile battle royale game for six consecutive years. Garena also exclusively licenses and publishes hit titles like Arena of Valor and Call of Duty: Mobile in various global markets. The company is committed to creating social and entertainment experiences through games and is a prominent organizer of esports events. Garena is part of Sea Limited (NYSE:SE), a global consumer internet company whose other key businesses include Shopee and Monee.


Role Description

We are building a next-generation AI digital avatar product that helps users understand themselves and connect with kindred spirits. We’re looking for a Product Manager (based in Shanghai) who can drive the product from 0 → 1, combining psychology, AI, and gamified experiences.
  • Simplify, gamify, and personalize ways of collecting user personality input
  • Lead internal and external validation of personality modeling and AI features
  • Conduct user research and competitor analysis to define audience personas and core needs.
  • Capture user insights from AI interactions and translate them into product improvements.
  • Define and track key metrics (DAU/MAU, retention, engagement, completion rates).
  • Collaborate with psychologists, engineers, and designers to deliver milestones efficiently


Qualifications
  • 3+ years product management experience, ideally 0 → 1.
  • Experience in AI companion, social, content, educational or psychological app.
  • Overseas study or work experience preferred.
  • Strong independent thinking, creativity, and UX intuition.
  • Data-driven, familiar with A/B testing and analytics.
  • BONUS: Psychology knowledge (MBTI, Big Five)
