Senior Planning Analyst

Tate & Lyle · 中国 山东省

职位简介

  • 了解工厂的能力，熟悉材料/劳动力/设备等约束条件；Work out the capacity of the plant, know well of the constraint like from Material /Labour/equipment.
  • 长期产能分析，针对未来12个月-5年的预测和生产情况，进行产能规划和相应的库存准备. Work out the long term 12 months to 5 years capacity analysing and planning, and make estimation for inventory outlook.
  • 根据生产能力,客户需求和库存水平要求制定生产计划；Work out the production planning according to capacity &Customer demand & stock level requirement.
  • 把月生产计划转化成合理的周生产计划和日生产计划， 需要考虑到实际生产的可执行性和有效性，减少不必要的切换和浪费. Manage monthly production to weekly and daily plan and minimize the waste in capacity and cost.
  • 跟踪每日，每周和月生产计划的执行情况，记录差异并分析原因. Keep tracking on production execution and analyse the variance between execution and plan.
  • 确认成品的有货日期，并配合订单处理部门完成客户订单交期确认。Confirm material available date and work with customer service team to make the customer order commitment.
  • 制定物料计划,进行生产相关的所有物料的采购申请及跟踪物料准时入库。Work out the material planning and purchasing requisition, and to follow up the on time good receipt of all production related material
  • 每月分析呆滞库存产生的原因，并召集相关部门商定解决方案，协助财务部门准备风险计提 Work out the inventory monitoring and analysing to reduce the abnormal and slow moving inventory, and be a coordinator in action of slow moving and support finance in inventory provision.
  • 协助财务部门进行库存计提

Inventory provision proposal to support financial control
  • 作为数据处理中心，与销售，质量，采购，制造部门等不同部门协调处理特殊情况或达到目标；As a data process centre, coordinate with different department like sales, Quality, purchase, manufacturing department to deal with special case or achieve the target.
  • 平衡供应需求并组织相关供应信息会议；Balance supply and demand, and lead supply related meetings to with other teams.
  • 其他临时安排的任务；Other task may assigned .
  • EHS (环境健康安全)，维护和改进公司设定的EHS标准和要求。参加公司安排的公司EHS计划，包括培训，审核等。EHS. Perform, Maintain and improve the EHS standard and requirements as set by the Company. Participate in the company’s EHS program including training, audit etc., arranged by the company.
