We aim to be where the growth is, enabling businesses to thrive and economies to prosper, and, ultimately, helping people to fulfil their hopes and realise their ambitions.

We are currently seeking an experienced professional to join our team in the role of SALES QUALITY REVIEW ANALYST.

Principal responsibilities

The tasks for this role may include some or all of the following:

  • Responsible for enforcing sales guidelines and sales compliance, monitoring of sales process, and conducting quality control checking
  • Managing various sales quality reports
  • Developing, maintaining and executing quality assurance programme/procedures for the frontline sales to ensure the Bank's standards are fully complied with
  • Cultivating the enforcement of sales compliance fulfilment and sales best practices in the appropriate Global Business Function
  • Promoting, fostering and monitoring sales compliance and sales integrity
  • Providing recommendations to improve and streamline the operation flow of the front sales office and other relevant business units
  • Where necessary, carrying out team administrative roles
  • Providing the team management with required management information, in order to inform decision making
  • Delivering fair outcomes for our customers and ensure own conduct maintains the orderly and transparent operation of financial markets

Requirements
  • Knowledge of Financial Services/industry/products/regulations
  • Good working knowledge and understanding of HSBC Sales Procedures and applications
  • Risk mindful and Strong analytical ability
  • Communicate at multiple levels with confidence (written and verbal)
  • Good command in English and Chinese
  • Good organisational and influencing skills
  • Ability to understand urgency and prioritisation
  • Proficient in Word, Excel, Powerpoint and Chinese typing is preferable

Candidate with less relevant experience or skills may be offered a lower Global Career Band than stated above.

Due to the urgent hiring need, candidates with immediate right to work locally and no relocation need will be prioritised.
