职位简介

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Manage a team of 3 other analysts and plan their weekly output.
  • Conduct in-depth quantitative analyses to evaluate marketing effectiveness, develop sophisticated statistical models, and segment customer populations to enhance upselling, deepen customer engagement, and improve profitability through optimized customer channel behaviors.
  • Implement cutting-edge marketing analytics solutions, including predictive statistical modeling, digital user behavior segmentation, data mining, and external data enhancement.
  • Develop and test experimental designs, sampling techniques, and analytical methods to drive continuous improvement in internal tools and processes.
  • Perform ad hoc analyses of business scenarios, systems, and challenges to identify opportunities for growth and optimization.
  • Analyse online user behaviours and digital marketing performance across multiple channels to uncover new business opportunities and improve campaign effectiveness.


QUALIFICATION PROFILE
  • Bachelor's degree in quantitative discipline such as Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research, Engineering, or a related field.
  • Minimum of 5 years of hands-on experience in marketing analytics, digital analytics, or CRM analytics within a fast-paced industry.
  • Proficiency in Power BI, SAS, R, SPSS, and SQL programming. Strong experience in statistical modeling is essential.
  • Exceptional problem-solving skills, strong business acumen, and the ability to communicate complex analytical insights effectively to stakeholders.
  • Proven ability to prioritize and resolve data-related issues with a high level of accuracy and attention to detail.
  • Proficient written and oral English is a must.
  • Familiarity with digital analytics platforms such as Adobe/Omniture, Google Analytics, IBM/Core Metrics, Web Trends, DoubleClick, AdWords, Ad server, DSP, or DMP is highly desirable.
