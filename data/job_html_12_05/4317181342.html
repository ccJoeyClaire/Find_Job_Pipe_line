职位简介

We are seeking a Assistant Data Program Manager.

As an Assistant Data Program Manager your main responsibilities will involve:

  • Act as the local owner and coordinator of data/AI initiatives.
  • Implement and localize data solutions, tools, and systems developed by the regional or group data teams.
  • Identify opportunities to leverage global/regional data products to solve local business challenges and drive adoption.
  • Partner with business stakeholders to translate needs into actionable data initiatives.
  • Manage project plans, timelines, and communication between local business units and regional data teams.
  • Collaborate closely with regional and global stakeholders to ensure alignment and smooth integration of group data/AI tools.
  • Track program KPIs, prepare executive updates, and ensure timely, high-quality delivery of data initiatives.
  • Serve as a data ambassador, building enthusiasm for data-driven decision-making within teams. Promote group data solutions, champion data use cases, and increase data literacy and awareness across business units.
  • Foster collaboration between data engineering, analytics, and business teams to ensure seamless integration of solutions.
  • Maintain and enhance existing Python-based tools and automations developed in previous projects.

Your Skills And Experience

As an Assistant Data Program Manager your skills and qualifications will ideally include:
  • Master degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, or a related field, and 6+ years of experience in data project or program management, preferably in a multinational or regional context.
  • Strong understanding of data ecosystems, including data pipelines, warehousing, and analytics platforms.
  • Proficiency in Python for maintaining and enhancing existing tools.
  • Experience with data visualization and BI tools (Power BI, Tableau, or similar).
  • Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills, with the ability to collaborate across regional and group structures.
  • Fluent in English; knowledge of Cantonese or Mandarin is an advantage.
