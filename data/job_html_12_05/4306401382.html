Reinsurance Actuarial Analyst

Guy Carpenter · 中国 上海市 (现场办公)

职位简介

We are seeking a talented individual to join our Reinsurance Actuarial team at Guy Carpenter. This role will be based in Shanghai. This is a hybrid role that has a requirement of working at least three days a week in the office.

We will count on you to:

  • Organizes client data, checks data for reasonability, loads data into predictive models, runs the models and shares output with senior actuaries to help determine client's degree of risk
  • Performs basic calculations with client data to assess a range of risk exposures and predict outcomes for clients in support of reinsurance transaction process
  • Collects and analyzes industry data and updates the trending process in the predictive models to support advisory work
  • Conducts research on industry/regulatory developments that affect loss liabilities to determine the impact on clients
  • Keeps teams updated on industry-specific and speciality related trends that may affect reinsurance programs/calculations
  • Performs simple calculations using the MetaRisk model to help assess outcomes for clients

What you need to have:

  • Master degree in Actuarial Science/Finance/Economics/Statistics or related discipline (for HK candidate, miminum degree can be Bachelor)
  • 1-2 year work experience in actuarial field would be a plus. Open to recent graduates.
  • Desire to learn and further develop in the broader risk management field.
  • Strong analytical and data skills with the ability to multitask and balance priorities.
  • Outstanding computer and technical skills. Proficiency with Microsoft Office especially working with Excel, PowerPoint and Word.
  • Strong communication and presentation skills, independent and diligent.
  • Proficiency in spoken and written English and Chinese.
  • Open to fresh graduates with relevant internship experience.

Why join our team:
  • We help you be your best through professional development opportunities, interesting work and supportive leaders.
  • We foster a vibrant and inclusive culture where you can work with talented colleagues to create new solutions and have impact for colleagues, clients and communities.
  • Our scale enables us to provide a range of career opportunities, as well as benefits and rewards to enhance your well-being.

At Guy Carpenter, you can be your best. We work on challenges that matter with colleagues who help bring out our best.
