Financial Planning and Analysis Manager

Sony Music Entertainment · 中国 北京市 (现场办公)

职位简介

We are seeking a highly analytical and business-savvy FP&A Manager to lead our overall P&L forecasting and business planning (BP) processes.

This role will play a critical part in supporting strategic deal evaluations, partnering with cross-functional teams, and driving data-driven decision-making across the organization.



What you'll do:


• Lead annual budgeting, align with business teams to decompose strategic goals and set revenue/cost/cash flow benchmarks.

• Establish monthly/quarterly rolling forecast mechanism covering P&L and core KPIs.

• Track budget execution, analyze variances and issue risk early warning with optimization proposals.

• Build customized financial models for diversified revenue and cost control.

• Conduct feasibility analysis for project evaluation with financial calculation and risk assessment.

• Develop and maintain dashboards and reporting tools to monitor performance and identify trends.

• Respond to ad-hoc management requests for decision support.



Who you are:


• Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or related field; MBA or CPA preferred.

• 5+ years of progressive experience in FP&A or corporate finance.

• Strong in data analytics

• Strong command of financial modeling, data analysis, and accounting principles.

• Proven ability to work cross-functionally and influence stakeholders at all levels.

• Excellent communication skills with the ability to simplify complex financial concepts.

• Proficiency in Excel, financial planning tools (e.g., Anaplan, Adaptive), and BI platforms (e.g., Power BI, Tableau).
