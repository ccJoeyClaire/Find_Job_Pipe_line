Digital Marketing Expert (GEO/SEO)

Wynn Resorts Macau · 中国 澳门特别行政区 (现场办公)

职位简介

Job Purpose: We are seeking a strategic and analytical GEO Expert to pioneer and lead our Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) strategy. This pivotal role is designed to enhance our brand's visibility and information accuracy across key AI platforms - including China AI agents (DeepSeek, Doubao etc.) and leading international platforms like ChatGPT and Gemini - while providing end-to-end oversight of GEO initiatives. The ideal candidate will possess a unique blend of solid GEO/SEO expertise, deep understanding of AI platform mechanisms, fundamental knowledge of social media platforms, and strong data analysis and insight capabilities. This combination will ensure optimal online visibility, consistent brand representation, and efficient cross-channel synergy.


Key Responsibilities

GEO Strategy Development & Agency Management
  • Develop and lead the execution of the brand's overall Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) strategy, defining core objectives and KPIs.
  • Act as the primary liaison, providing comprehensive management of the external GEO agency to ensure all deliverables meet expectations, timelines, and quality standards.
  • Lead key project milestone reviews, ensuring diagnostic insights and optimization strategies are effectively implemented.

Paid Media Strategy & Performance (with Douyin Focus)
  • Develop and oversee the integrated paid media strategy.
  • Manage and optimize paid advertising campaigns across key social platforms, with a specialized focus on Douyin, from budget allocation and targeting to creative direction and performance analysis.
  • Leverage deep understanding of the social media ecosystem (especially short video platforms) to inform both paid and organic content strategy.

Content Ecosystem & Technical Architecture Optimization
  • Guide and approve AI-friendly content strategies, optimizing the information architecture and textual expression of the official website and core digital assets.
  • Collaborate with the tech team to oversee and drive technical optimizations of the website's code and structure for search engines and AI crawlers.

User Insight, Prompt Strategy & Data-Driven Execution
  • Guide the development of core user personas and content topics based on brand and market data, driving their continuous iteration across both GEO and paid media strategies.
  • Lead the design, testing, and refinement of effective prompt frameworks and libraries tailored to various AI platforms to accurately guide AI outputs.
  • Establish a unified performance monitoring system for GEO and paid media, regularly analyzing data to extract actionable insights and drive strategic optimization.
  • Champion the adoption of internal AI-friendliness diagnostic tools, enabling the content team to perform self-service diagnostics and optimizations.


Competencies and Requirements:

Core Competencies
  • Strategic Thinking & Business Acumen: Ability to align GEO and paid media strategies with overall business objectives and quantify the impact of initiatives on business growth.
  • Exceptional Project & Agency Management: Proven capability to manage multiple complex projects and external partnerships simultaneously while maintaining high standards and meeting deadlines.
  • Data-Driven & Analytical Mindset: High proficiency in data analysis with the ability to distill key insights from complex information to drive strategic optimization.
  • Outstanding Communication & Influence: Excellent ability to articulate complex concepts clearly to team members at all levels and drive cross-functional collaboration.
  • Proactivity & Growth Mindset: Maintains strong curiosity about the AI and marketing technology landscape, proactively learning and applying emerging trends and best practices.
  • Resilience & Execution Excellence: A team player who thrives in a high-pressure environment, responding quickly to demands. Combines patience, meticulous attention to detail, and the ability to work independently to drive projects from concept to completion.

Experience/ Knowledge/Certificates:

  • 7+ years of experience in digital marketing, with at least 3 years specifically focused on SEO/GEO strategy, coupled with proven success in managing social media and paid media budgets and strategy.
  • Deep understanding of major AI platforms and hands-on experience with prompt optimization.
  • High proficiency with data analytics tools and the ability to independently extract, analyze, and report on data.
  • Solid knowledge of the social media ecosystem, including content distribution logic and paid advertising mechanics, with a strong focus on Douyin.


Education: Bachelor’s degree or above, preferably in Marketing, Computer Science, Statistics, or a related field.

Language Ability: Good written and spoken English and Chinese
