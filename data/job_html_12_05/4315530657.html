职位简介

【职位描述】：

1、负责美洲用户产品线的规划与设计，深度理解用户需求，制定并执行产品策略，提升用户满意度和活跃度。

2、与美洲本地团队，杭州的研发、设计、运营团队紧密合作，推动产品功能迭代，确保产品的高质量上线和持续优化。

3、进行市场分析和竞品研究，洞察行业趋势，为产品创新提供决策支持。

4、负责项目管理，协调资源，确保产品开发进度和质量，有效控制风险。

5、持续跟踪产品数据，通过数据分析优化产品，提升用户体验和业务指标。

1、Responsible for planning and designing user product lines, deeply understanding user needs, formulating and executing product strategies to enhance user satisfaction and engagement.

2、Collaborate closely with R&D, design, and operation teams to drive product feature iterations, ensuring high-quality product launches and continuous optimization.

3、Conduct market analysis and competitor research，insight industry trends, providing decision support for product innovation.

4、Manage projects, coordinate resources, ensure product development timelines and quality, effectively controlling risks.

5、Continuously track product data, optimize products through data analysis, and improve user experience and business metrics.


【职位要求】：

1、本科及以上学历，从事过跨境电商业务，或者有北美/拉美工作或学习经验。

2、具备出色的产品思维和用户洞察力，能够从用户角度出发设计产品。

3、英语或西语熟练，能够跨时差、跨文化沟通，了解不同文化背景，有效推动跨部门协作，确保项目顺利进行。

4、具备数据分析能力，熟练使用数据分析工具，能从数据中发现问题并提出解决方案。

1、Bachelor's degree or above in Computer Science, Information Management, or related fields, with more than 3 years of experience in user product-related roles.

2、Familiarity with internet product development processes, with a preference for those with a track record of successfully launched user products with significant impact.

3、Excellent project management skills, able to effectively drive cross-departmental collaboration to ensure project smoothness.

4、Proficient in data analysis, skilled in using data analysis tools, able to identify issues and propose solutions from data.
