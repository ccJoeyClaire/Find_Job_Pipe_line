Strategic Transportation Analyst

aramco · 中国 上海市 (现场办公)

职位简介

PRINCIPAL DUTIES:

  • Assist to foster professional relationships with government, local and international research institutions and industrial partners.
  • Track the forthcoming policies/regulations/standards; assist to analyze their impacts on fuel and petroleum demand.
  • Participate in various transportation strategic studies aimed at understanding the interplay between future engine and fuel technologies and global energy demand / supply landscape.
  • Participate in applied research studies related to fuel requirements of advanced and future transportation engines.
  • Assist to prepare materials for patents, make presentations, and publish technical papers or articles in peer-reviewed journals and other public media.
  • Perform other miscellaneous duties as directed by Line Manager.


MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:

  • Bachelor degree in fuel or other related science with 3 years of relevant research experience. Alternatively, fresh Master graduate in fuel related major.
  • Experience in building consensus with governmental, regulatory, and standards organizations is a plus.
  • Knowledge of the impact of fuels on transportation engine performance and emissions.
  • Knowledge of engine technology development trends.
  • Knowledge of fuel formulation, fuel characterization methods, fuel blending techniques, refining, and chemical processes.
