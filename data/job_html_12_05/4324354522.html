About This Position

  • Support financial activities at the plant, including budgeting, forecasting, and reporting to support both short-term and long-term goals.

  • Prepare annual product cost standards and oversee cost monitoring for ongoing and new products, including updating allocation keys.

  • Identify opportunities for cost reduction and process improvements and initiates respective actions.
  • Sharing best practices with other controllers across the organization.
  • Drive financial education and awareness initiatives for plant employees to enhance financial literacy and engagement.
