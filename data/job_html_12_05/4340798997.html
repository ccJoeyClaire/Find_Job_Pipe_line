Job Description

Primary Responsibility
  • Be responsible for areas including but not limited to sample selection, data quality control, data validation/simulation and client enquiries in FMCG industry based on data analytical skill and fundamental statistical knowledge
  • Implement the projects/tasks on schedule with highly quality
  • Serves as expertise for operation function, ensure goals assigned are met
  • Cooperate with cross team peers under Operation, Data Science and Tech team, identifies the key risks or potential issues for the projects
  • Serves as the initiator to solve the issues, prepare the backup plan and monitor it to ensure on time delivery

Qualifications

Qualification
  • Bachelor/Master degree in statistics, mathematics, computer science, social science, market research, operation, science, engineering is preferred
  • 2-3 years market analytical/modeling/operation experience



Hard Skill and knowledge
  • Good understanding with a programming language like SQL, Python, R, VBA or SAS and/ or experience in one or more areas in market database setup, data extraction, data cleaning and validation
  • Familiar with MS office (Excel, PPT)
  • Language - Fluent written and spoken English
  • Good understanding in FMCG market
  • Good judgment based on market facts and translating statistical concept into data application



Soft Skill
  • Multi task Management and anti-pressure ability
  • Strong Analytic skill based on data support and clear logic
  • Communication – clarify needs, excellent written and verbal communication skills, to present clear messages from complex information/data to all peers internally and externally
  • Continuous improvement mindset and proactive attitude
  • Leadership – a clear understanding and commitment to Nielsen goals and objectives, and the ability to build professional relationships partners and team member. Collaboration and Teamwork
