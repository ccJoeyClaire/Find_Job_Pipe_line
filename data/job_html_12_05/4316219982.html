What You Will Be Doing

Your activity domain will exist of:

  • Lead and execute small (weeks) and medium (months) sized projects, actively participating in all phases. Defines and documents project management materials: project definitions, assumptions, risks, and dependencies
  • Report to a Project Steering Committee, manage the customer relationship, at project level and with senior level when required
  • Manage the SharePoint roadmap of a business unit. Coordinates and plans activities to manage a pipeline of business requests and changes from definition to realization. Manage the economics of this pipeline against the overall roadmap (operational plan) of the BU.
  • Analyse business processes, establish requirements for implementation of changes in the business process, re-think to fit on standard platforms exploiting well known building blocks and best practices
  • Promote and ensure standard tools, design patterns and best practices are applied in the business community
  • participate in specific business adoption and related change handling
  • keep up to date with the latest Microsoft technologies and the use cases applied


The technologies involved in the first years will involve the broad Microsoft

Technology Stack
  • M365 collaboration solutions & services, in particular SharePoint and the Power platform, Power-Apps and Power-Automate
  • In support of application development: Azure DevOps, Database options like SQL and Dataverse
