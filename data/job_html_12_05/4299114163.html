Data Analyst, Greater China

职位简介

What You'll Do:

We are seeking a highly motivated Data Analyst to join our team and support key businesses across APAC, including the growth of key China clients. In this role, you will leverage your technical skills and proactive mindset to transform data analytics into actionable insights.

Your role includes expanding optimization and development for key agency related clients and accounts, accelerating growth for strategic China clients, and driving efficiency of support by collaborating with different teams and utilizing tools and resources. You will utilize your technical expertise, a client-focused approach, strategic consulting skills, and a deep understanding of market dynamics to deliver impactful solutions.

Working closely with various teams, you will deliver automation tools and solution to optimize campaigns, address key challenges, and achieve measurable results. Your work will directly contribute to client/agency success and business growth, particularly by unlocking new opportunities for China clients through analytics and tailored solutions.

As part of a collaborative and dynamic team, you will play a key role in driving efficiency and delivering impactful solutions that meet client and agency needs and contribute to business growth.

  • Analyze and Identify Opportunities: Understand business needs and challenges by analyzing features and market needs. Develop innovative and effective ideas that address these challenges and deliver value.
  • Support various China Client Growth: Partner with China teams to deliver insights and scalable solutions that drive measurable growth, optimize performance, and deepen strategic partnerships.
  • Scale Impact and Recommend Solutions: Translate ideas into actionable recommendations, supporting teams with optimization strategies and development of a tool. Scale proven solutions across clients and markets to maximize business value and deliver impactful outcomes.


Who You Are:

You possess a unique blend of technical expertise, analytical acumen, and a client-focused mindset. You thrive in collaborative environments, are detail-oriented, and demonstrate strong leadership and communication skills.

Technical Skills:
  • A solid grasp of SQL and Python to manage complex data scenarios and create meaningful tools
  • Hands-on experience with data techniques, such as automation, deep-dive analysis, and report
  • Familiarity with methods to create automation and assess business impacts with tools


Analytical Skills:
  • Ability to identify patterns and understand data relationships, turning them into actionable features and tools that drive impact
  • Ability to translate complex analytics into clear, strategic recommendations
  • Strong skills in creating clear and compelling visualizations to communicate insights to diverse stakeholders


Client-Centric Mindset:
  • Deep understanding of client challenges; designing tailored optimization strategies that meet business unique needs
  • Ability to communicate data-driven insights in a clear and actionable way, ensuring alignment with market objectives
  • A business-oriented mindset to anticipate potential challenges and adapt strategies accordingly would set you apart


Communication Skills:
  • Proficiency in English and Chinese is mandatory for working with both local and global teams
  • Ability to simplify complex technical concepts into clear, concise language that resonates with clients and commercial stakeholders


Leadership & Proactivity:
  • Ability to lead initiatives, facilitate cross-functional collaboration and alignment of efforts across teams
  • Experience managing projects with defined objectives, timelines and deliverables
  • Taking ownership of challenges and identifying opportunities for innovation


Market Understanding:
  • Ability to adapt solutions to client contexts industry-specific challenges, and market dynamics, ensuring relevance and impact in every situation


Desired Background:
  • Bachelor’s degree or higher (Master’s degree or above preferred) in Statistics, Data Science, Applied Mathematics, Operations Research, Industrial Engineering, Computer Science, Data Analytics, or a related quantitative field
  • 3+ years of relevant experience in analytics, consulting, or digital advertising
  • Familiarity with Adtech technologies (e.g., cookies, DSP, SSP, RTB) and experience in Agency landscape would be beneficial


We acknowledge that many candidates may not meet every single role requirement listed above. If your experience looks a little different from our requirements but you believe that you can still bring value to the role, we’d love to see your application!
