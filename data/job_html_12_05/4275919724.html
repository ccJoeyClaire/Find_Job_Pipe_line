1、About JD.com

JD.com (NASDAQ: JD and HKEX: 9618), also known as JINGDONG, has evolved from a pioneering e commerce platform into a leading technology and service provider with supply chain at its core. Renowned for its supply chain innovation and excellence, JD.com has expanded into sectors including retail, technology, logistics, healthcare, and more, aiming to transform traditional business models with cutting edge digital solutions. Ranked 47th on the Fortune Global 500, JD.com is China’s largest retailer by revenue. In 2023, JD.com reported revenues of US$152.8 billion and served approximately 600 million active customers.


2、TGT (Tech Genius Team）Program

Tech Genius Team (TGT) Program is a global technical talent initiative. We seek to collaborate with visionary technologists to explore cutting edge innovations through JD’s vast business ecosystems, leveraging technology to create meaningful societal impact.


TGT focuses on the most cutting edge topics with societal value. Our research areas include:

1）AI Infra

Responsible for the architecture design and optimization of JD.com's AI infrastructure, promoting the efficient implementation of deep learning frameworks in training and inference stages, and customizing development to meet business scenarios to enhance model performance and resource utilization, providing robust underlying support for JD.com's intelligent business operations.

2）Security AI

Focus on building a comprehensive security protection system for JD.com's platform, employing advanced algorithms and AI models to monitor and guard against various security threats in real-time, ensuring user data privacy and transaction security, and safeguarding the stable operation of the business, thereby fortifying the security line of defense for JD.com's e-commerce operations.

3）Big Data Platform Development and Optimization

Deeply involved in the construction and optimization of JD.com's big data platform, responsible for the collection, storage, processing, and analysis of massive amounts of data, mining data value to provide data support for business scenarios such as precision marketing, supply chain optimization, and user profiling, assisting JD.com in achieving data-driven decision-making and operations.

4）Multi-modal Large Model Development and Applications

Explore innovative applications of multi-modal large models in JD.com's business, such as product image recognition, video content understanding, and multi-modal recommendations. By integrating text, image, and voice data, enhance the model's perception and understanding capabilities to deliver a more intelligent and enriched shopping experience for users.

5）High-Performance Computing and Cloud Innovation

Dedicated to performance optimization and technological innovation of JD.com's cloud computing platform, building an efficient and reliable cloud infrastructure to support large-scale distributed computing and storage, meeting the growing business demand for computing resources while reducing operational costs, and driving the development of JD.com's business towards cloud-native.

6）Machine Learning and Sys Analytics

Conduct in-depth research and application of machine learning algorithms, targeting business scenarios such as user growth, risk control, and supply chain optimization at JD.com. Design and develop efficient predictive models and decision-making systems, providing precise insights and strategic recommendations through data mining and model optimization to enhance business efficiency and competitiveness.

7）World Foundation Models and Embodied AI

Investigate the application of spatial intelligence technologies in JD.com's logistics and warehousing scenarios, such as robot path planning and intelligent warehouse management. By constructing spatial perception and decision-making models, achieve automated and intelligent spatial operations to improve logistics efficiency and warehouse utilization, advancing the development of JD.com's smart logistics.

8）Search, Recommendation, and Advertising Optimization

Focus on algorithm optimization and innovation of JD.com's search, recommendation, and advertising systems, employing advanced machine learning, reinforcement learning, and deep learning techniques to enhance the accuracy of search results, relevance of recommendations, and effectiveness of ad placements, optimizing user experience and increasing the commercial value and user stickiness of the platform.
