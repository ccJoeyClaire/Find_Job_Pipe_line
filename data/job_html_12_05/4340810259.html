职位简介

Objectives

Responsible for transforming complex, multi-source data into clean, structured formats and actionable insights to support AI development and data-driven decision-making

Main Task

  • Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and correlations
  • Create reports, dashboards, and visualizations to support business decisions
  • Collaborate with various departments (e.g., Marketing, Sales, Production) to develop data-driven solutions
  • Clean, validate, and transform data
  • Support the development of KPIs and performance metrics

Job Requirements
  • University degree in Computer Science or a related discipline, e.g. Statistics, Business Informatics, Mathematics, Data Science, or a related field
  • Minimum 5 years of work experience in relevant fields
  • Strong project management experience highly preferred
  • Experience with data analysis tools such as Excel, SQL, or Python
  • Knowledge of BI tools like Power BI and MS Azure components
  • Understanding of databases and ETL processes
  • Ability to communicate complex data in an understandable way
  • Fluent in written and spoken English (German skills is a plus)
  • Analytical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Teamwork and strong communication skills
  • Good presentation skills
  • Initiative and structured working style
  • Business acumen to interpret data in context

Nice to have:
  • Experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
  • Knowledge of machine learning or statistical modeling
  • Experience with data governance and data protection regulations
