Performance and Technology Senior Analyst

AlixPartners · 中国 上海市 上海市

职位简介

At AlixPartners, we solve the most complex and critical challenges by moving quickly from analysis to action when it really matters; creating value that has a lasting impact on companies, their people, and the communities they serve. By understanding, respecting, and honouring the needs of our employees, clients, and communities, AlixPartners actively promotes an inclusive environment. We strongly believe in the value that diversity brings to our experiences and are committed to the perpetual enhancements of initiatives, policies, and practices. We hold ourselves accountable by providing the space for authenticity, growth, and equity for everyone.

AlixPartners has embraced a hybrid work model to provide flexibility and support our employees’ work-life integration. Our hybrid model combines a mix of in-person (at client site or AlixPartners office) and remote working. Travel is part of this position, but the frequency may vary based on client, team, and individual circumstances.

This role is located in Shanghai, China.

What You’ll Do

As a Senior Analyst, you will be responsible for supporting:

  • Structure and execute analysis involving our clients’ most complex and ambiguous challenges (working closely with our VPs and SVPs on client projects and supporting them by designing and building quality models, drafting PowerPoint presentations, and performing financial and operational analysis).
  • Translate analysis into tangible implementation plans and performance commitments (building insights into value creation areas, collaborating with engagement team and stakeholders, building relationships to influence and provide advice to key decision-makers)
  • Utilize AlixPartners proprietary tools to analyse data and derive actionable insights (such as our leading-edge tools for revenue growth, spend analysis, and organizational transformation). Conduct research using external tools and databases (including Capital IQ, Bloomberg, Factiva, Statista, and Euromonitor), and use interactive data visualization tools (such as Tableau and Power BI) to present results in a compelling manner.
  • Support practice development, marketing & pitches, and account management.

This description is not designed to encompass a comprehensive listing of required activities, duties or responsibilities.

What You’ll Need
  • 2 - 4 years of business consulting experience
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English and Mandarin is a must
  • Hands-on operational mindset is required
  • Analytical, capable of developing and managing related analytics, reports and metrics
  • High energy style, flexible and adaptive, with the ability to work well in a very fast paced environment
  • Independently structures problem solving approaches and adapts quickly to changing direction
  • Innovative with the development of new tools, methodologies and approaches
  • Exceptional customer service and interpersonal skills. Builds counselling relationships with clients and peers
  • Works effectively with others. Capable of ‘flexing’ between big picture strategy and detailed focus
  • Advanced proficiency with Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Access and Excel applications
  • Openness for occasional travel
  • Bachelor's degree with evidence of strong academic success. Degree(s) in Business Administration, Finance, Economics, Statistics, Engineering, Computer Science, Data Science, or another quantitative field is preferred. Master’s/ MBA degree and/or certification in related specialty area(s) preferred
  • Willingness to work outside of normal business hours, and in particular as unique projects/needs arise.
  • Ability to work full time in an office and remote environment; physically able to sit/stand at a computer and work in front of a computer screen for significant portions of the workday
  • Must become familiar with, and promote and abide by, our Core Values as defined by the AlixPartners’ Code of Conduct and foster an inclusive environment with people at all levels of an organization
