Process vendor, employee, and intercompany payments in accordance with company policies and payment schedules.

根据公司政策和付款计划处理供应商、员工及公司间的付款。

Ensure accuracy and completeness of payment requests, including proper documentation and approvals.

确保付款申请的准确性和完整性，包括适当的文件和审批。

Monitor payment runs and resolve any issues related to rejected or failed transactions.

监控付款执行，并解决与被拒绝或失败交易相关的问题。

Coordinate with banks and treasury teams for payment execution and cash flow alignment.

与银行和财务团队协调，确保付款执行和现金流匹配。

Maintain accurate records of all payment transactions and support audit requirements.

维护所有付款交易的准确记录，并支持审计要求。

Respond to inquiries from vendors and internal teams regarding payment status.

回应供应商和内部团队关于付款状态的询问。

Support month-end closing activities and payment reconciliations.

支持月末结账活动和付款对账工作。
