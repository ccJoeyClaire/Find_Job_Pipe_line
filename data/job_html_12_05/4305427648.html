职位简介

ROLE SUMMARY

The objective of this packaging development manager role is to lead end-to-end packaging development projects, providing advice and solutions regarding all technical requirements to our Modern Oral (MO) Category.

An excellent working knowledge of different packaging substrates such as rigids plastics, printed flexibles, labels, and cartons is required. Working closely with our product innovation, marketing, procurement, supply chain, manufacturing, compliance, legal and quality, the Packaging development manager will own packaging specifications and implementation process to MO new and existing product portfolio.

Strong communication, innovation and influencing skills will be critical to this role to build strong relationships with both internal and external stakeholders.

ACCOUNTABILITIES

  • Manage complex projects from design concept, prototype, build, and commercialization of rigids (plastics, metals), printed flexibles, labels, and carton structures.
  • Lead early-stage discovery activities (tech blocks) to identify emerging packaging technologies, materials, and design trends that align with consumer needs, sustainability goals, and business strategy.
  • Conduct feasibility assessments and material evaluations during the concept phase to inform packaging direction and innovation pipeline.
  • Provide technical support and collaborate with other functions of the business in resolving technical issues during design and development.
  • Provide technical support to procurement on new and existing packaging sourcing requirements, identifying and actualizing opportunities for cost and quality improvement.

  • Participate in the development of Quality Control Plans with the BAT Quality team.
  • Travel to suppliers/plants to support development, fine-tuning, and/or specification release.
  • Attend material qualification trials and first-off production runs at manufacturing sites and suppliers as required.
  • Establish strong technical relationships with internal and external stakeholders.

Rigids Plastics
  • Lead projects through all stages of the injection moulding process, from concept evaluation through commercialization.
  • Lead development of specifications and acceptance protocols to enable the purchase and production of injection moulds and moulded components.
  • Lead pilot and commercial mould trials, ensuring new project launches are on schedule and successful.
  • Manage suppliers to ensure timely and standard-compliant design, development, and implementation of injection moulds and component conversion.
