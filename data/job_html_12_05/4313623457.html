职位简介

About The Business

Deloitte China established its Global Delivery Center (GDC) in Chongqing, a key growth engine of China, in 2015. The Center provides innovative, multi-lingual offerings that support compliance and advisory services. GDC helps drive maximum efficiency and provides centralized "plug and play" internal services, enabling a scope of work that serves global clients and the Deloitte network better, making a real impact that matters. The Deloitte GDC has established offices in Chongqing, Dalian, and Guangzhou.

The Consulting Intelligence Center is the center of excellence for the Strategy and Business Design(S&BD) team, as well as the wider consulting practice, focusing on research and data analytical work, acting as a hub to knowledge management and intelligence gathering to future market eminence. We are the bridge to connect clients with global resources and professional expertise as well as apply leading innovative technologies to solve business problems. We focus on research-oriented activities, allowing accumulation of industry knowledge and insights. The Consulting Intelligence Center will provide a platform for services such as market research, knowledge management, market eminence, enabling a foundation for tangible, long-term value delivery for our clients.

Work you'll do

Work you'll do：

  • Support the project team on strategizing and delivering solutions to clients to promote growth and address the evolving needs of their businesses
  • Conduct analysis in your vertical knowledge domain, serve as a point of contact for the project team to support clients making decisions and implementing enhancements to their organizations' strategy, productivity, performance, and long-term profitability
  • Analyze, and synthesize the market data into targeted end deliverables, such as single exhibits, written summaries, financial models, basic fact packs, spread sheets, to help fulfill client's needs
  • Support the project team with your vertical expertise to help them tackle some of the ad-hoc client requests, which includes but not limited to project follow-up, proposal preparation, etc.
  • Maintain an accurate and up-to-date knowledge base on your responsible knowledge domain; manage the data quality of the database, if there's any accumulated from the project
  • Recommend improvements in work processes of the Intelligence Center within area of responsibility
  • In some cases, you're expected to create and maintain some tools to bring vertical knowledge and capabilities to our project teams, including benchmarking databases, simulations, and skill-building training sessions, etc.
  • Along the way, you'll participate in a comprehensive training programs that will help you continuously grow and develop.

During your tenure with us, you will demonstrate and develop your leadership and professional capabilities in the following areas: Inspiring, Creating purpose, Driving agility, Building diverse capability, Influencing, Collaborating, Delivering value, Building the business, Analytical acumen, Effective communication, Engagement management/delivery excellence, Managing change, Managing quality & risk, Sales excellence, Strategic thinking and problem solving, and Tech savviness.

We are looking for someone with
  • Bachelor's degree and above, preferably from majors such as science, finance, management, engineering, automotive, healthcare, chemistry, etc.
  • Prior experience 1-2 years in consulting, research, or related professional services firms, preferably with experience focus on the consumer goods and/or life science sectors
  • Strong analytical, problem solving and request handling skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment with people at all levels in an organization
  • Passion for research and analytics works
  • Persuasive communication skills in written and verbal Mandarin and Japanese; proficient communication capability in English
  • Travel might be required depending on business needs
