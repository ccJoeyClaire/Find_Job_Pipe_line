ABOUT NIELSENIQ

NielsenIQ is a global measurement and data analytics company that provides the most complete and trusted view available of consumers and markets worldwide. We provide consumer packaged goods manufacturers/fast-moving consumer goods and retailers with accurate, actionable information and insights and a complete picture of the complex and changing marketplace that companies need to innovate and grow. Our approach marries proprietary NielsenIQ data with other data sources to help clients around the world understand what’s happening now, what’s happening next, and how to best act on this knowledge. We like to be in the middle of the action. That’s why you can find us at work in over 90 countries, covering more than 90% of the world’s population. For more information, visit www.niq.com.

NielsenIQ is committed to hiring and retaining a diverse workforce. Job Description

Job Description

Scope:

The DC team is at the intersection between Client Team and the Data Science Team. Together, you will actively work with both stakeholders.

Markets : Greater China and possibly some projects in Asia Pacific Region

Global/regional/ local: Sub-Regional/Local

Purpose of the role:

Being the key contact point between data science and commercial teams, helping commercial team to understand the retail products and the methodology behind it.

Supporting the clients’ business needs and driving internal product enhancement at the same time.

Analyzing large amounts of data captured by NielsenIQ’s research team to arrive at insights regarding trends as well as opportunities for further study/drilldown.

Working hand in hand with the client to explain to them the specific aspects of the studies being undertaken and help the client better understand the data in a more intelligible way through a simple, but effective methodology.

Respond to client questions regarding data irregularities and help the client understand the methodology being employed by NielsenIQ’s statistical tools.

Create clear insights and examples, either through visualizations or through raw data analysis in order to answer the main question of WHY the data is behaving a certain way.

Engage and align with multiple stakeholders on data requirements as well as in design for new studies.

Testing new methods, softwares and workflows constantly and checking if they can improve productivity.

Main Accountabilities:

Work closely with key account clients to ensure that their data requirements are satisfied and that they are satisfied with NielsenIQ’s retail analysis methodology.

Conduct various studies within departments in NielsenIQ for updating, improving and creating new data requirements for the client.

Understand client requirements and translate them into intelligent insights by creatively using NielsenIQ’s data.

Contribute to creating best practices for the country/ cluster/ region and sharing them with other data departments

Improve the product through active inputs and engagement with the Data Science team.

About NIQ

NIQ is the world’s leading consumer intelligence company, delivering the most complete understanding of consumer buying behavior and revealing new pathways to growth. In 2023, NIQ combined with GfK, bringing together the two industry leaders with unparalleled global reach. With a holistic retail read and the most comprehensive consumer insights—delivered with advanced analytics through state-of-the-art platforms—NIQ delivers the Full View™. NIQ is an Advent International portfolio company with operations in 100+ markets, covering more than 90% of the world’s population.
