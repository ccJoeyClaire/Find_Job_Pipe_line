Molecular Devices · 中国 上海市 上海市 (现场办公)

职位简介

The Cost analyst is responsible for costing Analysis and accounting work for the global manufacturing organizations (US and AUT).

This position reports to the finance controller and is part of the Global Ops finance team located in Shanghai and will be an on-site role.

In This Role, You Will Have The Opportunity To

  • Drive and on time deliver US month-end closing in inventory and cost functions. Prepare inventory and costing related entries, complete full list of journal entries in time and save supporting doc. appropriately. Prepare and on time deliver balance sheet reconciliation on inventory accounts in compliance with DHR requirements.
  • Apply SOX for inventory and costing function, documentation saved appropriated ensure SOX compliance.
  • Analysis on monthly cost variances such as PPV, E&O, work order variance, and absorption/labor efficiency for US and AUT.
  • Monthly fcst on working capital- inventory balance, production hours, cost element in OCOGS. (AUT)
  • Cycle count and cell count management, support counting data preparation, verify counting result. Prepare cycle count report on weekly/monthly basis. Partner with US OPS&AWS team member on counting to meet cooperate requirement.
  • Other tasks assigned.

The essential requirements of the job include: (NOTE For Hiring Manager: Please reference the 3-5-7 Job Requirements Model)
  • 2+ years of finance related experience. Factory finance and costing related experience is preferred. Practical experience in costing, financial planning, reporting and control.
  • Bachelor’s degree or above in finance or accounting.
  • Ability to work with high self-motivation, be sensitive to numbers and the capacity to work in a changeable and stressful environment.
  • Well-developed inter-personal skills and good communication skills. Strong business partnering and teamwork mindset.

It would be a plus if you also possess previous experience in:
  • Accounting certificates would be a plus.
  • JP or KR language would be a plus.
  • Oracle and HFM experience preferred.
