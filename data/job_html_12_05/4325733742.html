Job Responsibilities:

  • IMS Product Management: Responsible for the planning and commercial success of OPPO’s mid-to-high-end products in India, integrating and aligning product, marketing, and sales resources within the region, establishing and managing the IMS team, and developing and executing overall IMS strategies for the project.

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher, with 7+ years of experience in the mobile phone industry.

  • Results-driven, highly motivated, strong execution ability, skilled in multi-project management, resource coordination, communication, and driving initiatives forward.
  • Fluent in English, willing to travel overseas for business.
