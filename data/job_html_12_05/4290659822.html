职位简介

Jardine Restaurant Group (JRG), a member of the Jardine Matheson Group, is one of the leading food and beverage groups in Asia. Headquartered in Hong Kong, JRG employs more than 25,000 people who share the passion for food, bringing simple joys of food with delightful experience to customers. Our passion, agility and innovation have helped us successfully broaden our portfolio and geographical footprint. The Group operates over 1,000 outlets under the KFC, Pizza Hut and PHD brands in Hong Kong, Macao, Myanmar, Taiwan, and Vietnam.


We are guided by our three core values agility, customer-centricity, and empathy (ACE), they are the guiding principles that help us navigate through the rapidly changing and hyper competitive market environment: We cultivate an adaptive and resilient spirit, foster empowerment and innovation that creates growth; We strive to bring the joy of food and beverages for people to love, with the best possible customer experience; We endeavour to be people-centric, respect differences while we collaborate, and aim to bring a positive impact to our world, sustainably.


We are seeking a talented and driven Commercial Analyst (Marketing Focus) to join the Pizza Hut team. In this role, you will be responsible for conducting in-depth market research and analysis to support strategic decision-making and drive the continued growth and success of our restaurant operations


Job Responsibilities:
  • Analyze data and integrate market trends to provide actionable insights supporting business and marketing strategies.
  • Deliver data-driven recommendations for specialized projects, including CRM and partnership initiatives.
  • Conduct comprehensive market and competitor analysis to refine product positioning, pricing, and promotions.
  • Perform market research to identify business opportunities and support the business plan for sustainable growth
  • Collaborate effectively with internal teams to ensure seamless execution of marketing campaigns and initiatives.
  • Provide full administrative support to the Commercial & Business Partnership team to optimize operational efficiency
  • Participate in ad-hoc projects and assignments, showcasing adaptability and adept problem-solving skills


Requirements:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or related disciplines
  • Minimum 1 year of experience in business analytics or CRM analytics within the F&B, Retail, or FMCG industry
  • Experience in PowerBI / Tableau is advantageous
  • Demonstrate numerical proficiency, coupled with strong business acumen and adept project management skills
  • Self-driven individual capable of working independently and thriving in a dynamic, fast-paced environment
  • Proficiency in written and spoken English, Cantonese, and Mandarin
  • Skilled in MS PowerPoint and Excel (e.g. vlookup and pivot tables)
  • Prior internship experience in marketing, data analysis, or related fields is a plus
