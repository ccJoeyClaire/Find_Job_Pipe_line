职位简介

【Important】English is required as a working language.

Base location: Hangzhou, China.

Work location / Contract signing / Payroll city: Hangzhou.


Job Responsibilities
  • Lead the team in building the offline data warehouse using Alibaba Cloud DataWorks / MaxCompute, with strong familiarity with AWS.
  • Lead data modeling design (ODS, DWD, DWS, DWT, ADS) and independently own the development of corresponding model layers.
  • Lead the planning, development, and maintenance of the enterprise KPI / metrics system.
  • Participate in data governance initiatives to improve data usability and data quality.
  • Work closely with business teams to deeply understand business scenarios; participate in big data application planning and provide guidance to data product and data mining teams.

Requirements
  • English can be used as a working language; fluent spoken English is required.
  • 10+ years of experience in the data warehouse domain; experience leading big data teams preferred. Experience in e-commerce or channel data architecture, and large-scale data processing is a strong plus.
  • Proficient in Alibaba Cloud DataWorks / MaxCompute; plugin development experience is a plus.
  • Familiar with Hadoop, Hive, and proficient in SQL and Python; experience with machine learning is preferred.
  • Familiar with the full implementation lifecycle of data warehouse projects; deep understanding of DW architecture and proven ability to support real business scenarios, ensuring data accuracy and completeness.
  • Excellent communication skills, strong business acumen, ability to quickly understand business context, and strong capability to bridge technology with business needs.

Nice-to-Haves
  • Experience with FineReport or Power BI.
  • Familiarity with cross-border e-commerce businesses, including mainstream platforms such as Lazada, Shopee, TikTok, Tmall Global, JD, Pinduoduo, etc.
