Job Descriptions

- Design and implement advanced data models and algorithms to extract insights from large and complex datasets, focusing on business growth and optimization in the Korea market.

- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business needs, define key performance indicators (KPIs), and develop data-driven solutions to address strategic challenges.

- Analyze and interpret data to identify trends, patterns, and correlations, providing actionable recommendations for product development, marketing strategies, and operational improvements.

- Present findings and recommendations in a clear and actionable manner and support major business decisions



Qualifications:

- 3+ year experience as a data scientist

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field with a strong emphasis on data analysis

- Proficiency at SQL, as well as experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI

- Experience in consumer/retail/internet companies or international strategy/operations firm is a big plus

- Fluent in Korean and Chinese, with the ability to work in a multicultural environment

- Strong logical and critical thinking skills to highlight complex problems; intellectual curiosity with excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.

- Dynamic personality with quick reaction to changing environments and ability to work under pressure.

- Ability to communicate in easy words to non-technical stakeholders

Note: This position is based in Hangzhou, China
