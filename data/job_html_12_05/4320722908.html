Business Analyst

Rotork · 中国 上海市 上海市 (现场办公)

职位简介

This is a unique opportunity to be part of Rotork's transformation journey, partnering with key stakeholders to harmonize processes across geographies and business units, ensuring alignment with Rotork's core values and strategic objectives.

The Business Transformation function in Rotork is responsible for driving customer value, designing excellent company-wide business processes to support effective customer delivery and our Growth+ strategy.

Our Business Analysts play a critical role in bridging the gap between business needs and technical, strategic or operational solutions whilst delivering comprehensive analysis and insights across technological, strategic and operational programmes. Shaping the way Rotork operates by supporting the design, documentation, optimisation, and governance of business processes across the organisation.

We are looking for a Business Analyst with the ability to effectively communicate and build relationships with stakeholders across the enterprise to gather requirements for ongoing improvements within our core systems, processes and workflows whilst delivering Rotork’s overall transformation strategy.



Job Description

Key Responsibilities

  • Translate business requirements into functional specifications, user stories and process flows to drive improvements across the business.
  • Define and document business processes, data flows, RACI models and system requirements to support business operation and transformation.
  • Facilitate workshops, interviews and working sessions to gather, document and validate business requirements.
  • Conduct gap analysis between current state and future state processes.
  • Designing, leading and implementing new processes and solution updates.
  • Partners closely with Product Owners, Process Owners and project teams to ensure alignment between business requirements, system requirements and the delivered change.
  • Support User Acceptance Testing, development of training materials and change management activities.
  • Advocate of the enterprise business process framework and building trusted business partner relationships.

Qualifications

Qualifications & Knowledge:
  • Degree in Business Information Systems, Management and Information Technology, Business Analytics, Information Systems Management, Business Administration with IT or Systems focus, Systems Engineering or a related field.
  • 3 – 5 years’ experience as a Business Analyst, preferably within complex corporate environments or multi-entity organizations.
  • Expert knowledge of structured process, innovation, problem-solving tools/methodologies and customer journey mapping. LEAN, Kaizen or Six Sigma background would be desirable.

Analytical & Critical Thinking:
  • Skilled in requirements gathering, root cause and gap analysis, SWOT assessments, and structured decision-making to drive business improvements.

Data Interpretation & Insight Generation:
  • Proficient in analysing large datasets and reports to extract actionable insights that inform strategic decisions.

Technical Proficiency:
  • Experienced with tools like Microsoft Dynamics 365, Visio, Excel, Power BI, SQL, JIRA, and Azure DevOps; capable of creating process models, wireframes, and prototypes.

Project Delivery & Agile Methodologies:
  • Demonstrated success in supporting digital transformations using Agile, Scrum, and hybrid approaches, including UAT, training, and change management.

Communication & Collaboration:
  • Strong facilitation, stakeholder engagement, and communication skills; proactive, self-motivated, and effective at resolving issues and motivating teams.
