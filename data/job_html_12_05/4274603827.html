职位简介

NEWLY CREATED ROLE - JOIN US TODAY

Location: China (Flexible within Mainland China-preferable Shanghai)


About the Role:

Randstad Sourceright is seeking a dynamic and analytical professional to join our team in China. This unique dual-role opportunity offers a blend of market intelligence and supply chain management (SCM) responsibilities, supporting our talent solutions portfolio across RPO, MSP, and SOW programs.


The successful candidate will spend 50% of their time focused on talent intelligence, generating insights and market reports across multiple industry sectors, and 50% supporting the SCM desk, managing vendor relationships and performance in China.

This role plays a pivotal part in shaping data-driven decision-making for our clients in Life Sciences, High-Value Manufacturing, Banking & Financial Services, and ICT.


Key Responsibilities:

1. Talent Intelligence & Insights (50%)
  • Collect, analyze, and synthesize labor market data, salary trends, competitor intelligence, and workforce insights to create high-impact reports for internal and client use.
  • Develop sector-specific talent dashboards and insight packs aligned to the needs of clients in Life Sciences, HVM, BFSI, and ICT.
  • Provide strategic advisory support during program implementation and optimization phases.
  • Collaborate with global talent intelligence teams to localize global insights for the China market.
  • Present findings to internal stakeholders and clients to inform workforce planning and talent strategy.


2. Supply Chain Management Support (50%)
  • Act as the China-based point of contact for all vendor management activities under the SCM function.
  • Support vendor onboarding, contract compliance, rate management, and performance tracking.
  • Monitor and improve supplier performance metrics (e.g., fulfillment rates, time-to-fill, quality of hire).
  • Partner with the global SCM team to align vendor strategy with broader program goals and local market dynamics.
  • Support escalations, audits, and continuous improvement initiatives with our vendor base in China.


Key Relationships:
  • Dual reporting to APAC Heads of Talent Intelligence and SCM.
  • Day-to-day collaboration with the Enterprise China Country Head.
  • Frequent engagement with client-facing delivery teams, vendors, and cross-functional stakeholders globally.


Requirements:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business, Economics, Human Resources, Data Science, or related field.
  • 3–5+ years’ experience in talent analytics, market research, workforce insights, or supply chain/vendor management within the HR/talent acquisition space.
  • Familiarity with RPO, MSP, and SOW models is highly desirable.
  • Strong analytical and reporting skills using tools like Excel, Power BI, Tableau, or similar.
  • Excellent communication and presentation abilities, both in Mandarin and English.
  • Ability to navigate a matrixed environment and manage multiple stakeholders.
