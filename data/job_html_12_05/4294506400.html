Consumer Insights Manager

职位简介

Job Description:


1. Work closely with all departments within the company (publishing, R&D, marketing, branding, etc.), fully understand business needs in daily communication, and be able to design targeted and feasible research solutions, providing insights and suggestions for R&D polishing, product iteration and publishing strategy.


2. Carry out comprehensive research support (qualitative and quantitative) for key products, be able to independently manage the process and specific execution of various research projects, efficiently coordinate internal and external resources, and produce highly implementable professional research reports in both Chinese and English.


3. Continue to focus on the three-terminal game industry, maintain a keen eye and insight into the game market, proactively identify and propose research topics that have common value to multiple businesses, and provide forward-looking input for the business.


Job Specification:


1. Bachelor's degree or higher, with at least 5 years of experience in market research, user research, or related fields. Experience in the gaming industry is preferred.

2. Excellent spoken English, able to work entirely in English in an international workplace.

3. Extensive experience in qualitative, quantitative, and data analysis; proficiency in complex statistical data analysis, and proficiency in statistical software such as SPSS and Q.

4. Strong learning, communication, and self-motivation skills, able to execute independently and collaborate effectively.

5. Open-minded and innovative, able to multitask and embrace challenges.

6. Passionate about gaming, with a preference for those who are multi-platform and multi-genre players.
