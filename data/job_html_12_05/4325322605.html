职位简介

Why Zurich

We are a Global Insurance Firm with strong presence in Hong Kong. We are seeking a passionate and experienced (Senior) Business & Data Analyst for our Data Engineering / BI ( Business Insights ) Team which oversees the Data Engineering, Visualization and Analytic functions.

Your Role

  • Build / maintain business datamarts to visualize KPIs, metrics in reports / dashboards to empower data analytic capability across functional teams for Life Business
  • Understand the Company’s business processes and applications, including their effect on data and reporting, apply the knowledge gained in designing datasets, reports and dashboards
  • Analyze business data to identify actionable insights for management and stakeholders
  • Drive analysis on customer segmentation / profiling and campaign performance
  • Analyze and verify requirements for dashboards and reports by working with requesters, SMEs and partners
  • Supporting the continuous enhancement, maintenance of datawarehouse, ETL processes, reports and dashboards
  • Work with IT and partner teams for project implementation
  • Prepare and execute test plan, test cases, as well as user training
  • Assemble system performance statistics, analyze them and make changes for continuous performance / capacity improvements
  • Understand / investigate data issues and derive solutions
  • Create / maintain data dictionary, user guide and other related documentation

Your Skills And Experience
  • Bachelor degree with 3+ years of relevant experience in Data / BI domains
  • Experience in data ingestion / ETL, data-modeling for datawarehouse
  • Highly proficient in SQL
  • Experience in PowerBI or similar visualization / dashboarding tools
  • Experience in Microsoft SSIS or similar ETL tools
  • Experience in Life Insurance ( GI – General Insurance an advantage )
  • Experience in Azure, Databricks, Python an advantage
  • Good analytical, problem solving and communication skills
  • Able to articulate business processes / requirements / ideas to all levels
  • Excellent skill in writing and communicating in English / Mandarin / Cantonese
  • Candidate with more experience will consider as Senior Business & Data Analyst
