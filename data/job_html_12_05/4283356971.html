职位简介

Job Overview:

OSL Group is looking for a Token Review Analyst based in Hong Kong. This person will play an important role in the Token Admission Process for both security and non-security tokens by working on the analysis and review for potential listing and subsequent monitoring. The ideal candidate will demonstrate passion for the blockchain industry/regulatory development and have excellent analytical / communication skills.



Job Responsibilities and Duties:
  • In depth research, review and coordinate external/internal resources to perform due diligence on the Tokens (both security and non-security) for potential listing.
  • Reviewing, monitoring, and reporting of tokens made available on OSL platform.
  • Reviewing, monitoring, and reporting on the latest blockchain industry development and regulatory requirements for virtual asset trading platforms globally and especially in Hong Kong.
  • Preparing updates and supporting information for communication with regulators.
  • Coordination and communication with projects and other service providers to achieve the task of token review in compliance with regulatory requirements.
  • Record keeping (including meeting minutes preparation) and documentation as part of the diligence process.



Job Requirements:
  • Education: Degree holder in any analytical discipline
  • Years of Experience: 5+ years (Experience and expertise in Defi a requirement)
  • Excellent command of written and spoken English and Chinese
  • Sufficient understanding of traditional finance and blockchain/digital asset ecosystems
  • Good analytical, critical thinking, and communication skills
