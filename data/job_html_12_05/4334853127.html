Analyst, Procurement CoE

莫仕公司 · 中国 广东省 石碣镇 (现场办公)

职位简介

What You Will Do

  • Manage the supplier life cycle of supplier onboarding, from initial request to the vendor creation.
  • Collect and review proactively with new suppliers’ required documentation (e.g., W-9/W-8, certifications, compliance questionnaire updates.)
  • Review and validate submitted supplier information for completeness, accuracy, and adherence to company policies and regulatory requirements.
  • Coordinate with internal departments (e.g., Legal, Finance, Compliance) to ensure all necessary approvals and system updates are completed for new suppliers.
  • Execute regular updates, changes, and deactivations of existing supplier records in the relevant ERP/Procurement systems (e.g., SAP Ariba, Coupa,)
  • Perform periodic data cleansing and validation activities to ensure data accuracy, consistency, and eliminate redundancies.
  • Respond to internal and external inquiries related to supplier data, providing accurate and timely information.
  • Monitor and action requests for supplier data modifications received through various channels.
  • Identify opportunities to streamline and improve supplier onboarding and data maintenance processes for greater efficiency and accuracy.
  • Conduct training sessions (virtual and/or in-person as needed) for suppliers on how to effectively use the business network functionalities (e.g., invoice submission, order confirmation, catalog management, performance monitoring).
  • Develop and maintain(translation) comprehensive training materials, user guides and video tutorials for supplier enablement initiatives.
  • Track and report on business network-supplier enablement and adoption rates.
  • Function as the primary point of contact for supplier inquiries and technical support related to the supplier enablement and adoption initiatives.
  • Identify opportunities to enhance the supplier enablement experience and streamline related processes (e.g., onboarding workflows, communication methods)
  • Participate in system testing and upgrades related to supplier master data.
  • Bridge effectively with Procurement, Accounts Payable, Legal, and other internal teams to ensure smooth supplier operations.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve any issues or discrepancies during the onboarding process in a timely manner.
  • Work in a project environment and supporting system/process enablement by designing and implementing the guided project.
  • Extensive delivery through analyzing issues in a complex business process and preparing functional updates, user article knowledge, and reports.
  • Perform Ticket management for Level 1 Helpdesk requests.
  • Business on demand support to Contracts Management: Contracts maintenance, Data analysis , Analytics.
  • Maintain the weekly Status and daily reports for traceability and analysis purposes of the incidents.


Who You Are (Basic Qualifications)
  • Experience in procurement, supply chain, or other supplier management-related roles.
  • Possess good English listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills.
  • Good skills in computer, Microsoft office tools, SharePoint, ERP systems – SAP.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Good skills in computer, Microsoft office tools, SharePoint, ERP systems – SAP /SAP Ariba.
  • Good communication skills to communicate with customers, team members, external data providers, and management.
  • Good skills in data administration, design, and master data recording.
  • Good analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Added advantage if the candidate has experience in SAP / SAP Ariba system modules configuration to understand the initiatives and align with business expectations.
  • Added advantage of having Coupa ERP system module knowledge.
  • Added advantage of experience in conducting the training and internal technical sessions within the team for SAP/ SAP Ariba technical support.
  • Experience in Ticket management skill – Added advantage of having SERVICE NOW tool
