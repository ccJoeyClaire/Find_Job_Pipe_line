职位简介

Company Overview:

Bybit is a leading cryptocurrency derivatives exchange designed for professional traders. Launched in 2018, Bybit offers a seamless trading experience for users to trade perpetual contracts on cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Ripple, and more. It provides a robust and secure platform for traders to engage in leveraged trading, allowing them to amplify their exposure to the crypto market.

Overall, Bybit is a comprehensive trading platform that caters to the needs of professional traders in the cryptocurrency market, offering advanced features, robust security, and responsive customer support


Job Responsibilities:
  • Demand Analysis and Design:
  • Collaborate with the finance team to understand and analyze customer requirements, transforming them into product functionalities and solutions.
  • Clarify cash flow and system integration requirements and design products that are easy to use and offer a smooth user experience.
  • Product Development and Implementation:
  • Work closely with the technology and operations teams to manage the entire product lifecycle, ensuring the product meets development milestones and is standardized according to the requirements.
  • Data Analysis and Continuous Optimization:
  • Analyze product data and user feedback, conduct product performance analysis, and generate reports.
  • Use insights to drive continuous improvements and product upgrades based on data-driven recommendations.


Job Requirements:
  • Bachelor’s degree or higher, preferably in finance, economics, or related fields.
  • 3-5 years of experience in financial product management.
  • Familiarity with financial systems and operations, and a strong understanding of product design and user experience.
  • Strong cross-departmental collaboration skills and the ability to drive product innovation in a fast-paced environment.
  • Proficiency in data analysis and the use of relevant analytical tools.
