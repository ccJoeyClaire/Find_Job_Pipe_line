Position and Team Overview

L.E.K.’s Asia Healthcare Practice advises some of the most prominent organisations across the region, including major multinationals with headquarters and/or significant operations in Asia. Our Partner team has extensive experience across the Healthcare / Life Sciences economy, including in LS / Biotech, MedTech and Healthcare Services.

Key Responsibilities


Primary research logistics

o Supporting case teams by sourcing content experts and key opinion leaders (e.g., by with LinkedIn, expert networks, cold calling, etc.) with customized approaches (e.g., referring to papers he / she has published)

o Internet research to help find contact information for top published experts (e.g., email addresses in medical journals or on hospital websites)

o Ensuring documentation of the L.E.K. expert interviews (e.g., required files, summarized notes)


Secondary research support

o Help case teams to identify secondary research resources that address client areas of interest (e.g., journal articles, marketing / industry reports, government publications)

o Perform basic analysis such as benchmarking, industry profiling, basic market sizing and growth projections


Analysis: modeling/surveys

o Data collection, entry and cleaning to prepare datasets for team analysis

o Reviewing client data to understand gaps in datasets


Slide preparation and quality control

o Creating sophisticated visuals in PowerPoint and other software for consulting teams to use in client presentations

o Collaborating with L.E.K. Graphic Design team to recommend visually interesting layouts for showing complex data

o Capturing high-quality slide visuals from team and converting them into general templates for collection in a repository (e.g., on the firm intranet) for firm-wide use

o Reviewing slide formatting for quality control, making layouts consistent and in line with L.E.K. brand standards (e.g., updating L.E.K. IP to current visual standards)


Case closure support

o Transitioning materials from L.E.K. standards to client templates, as needed

o Preparing redacted versions of materials for delivery to KM

o Preparing PowerPoint release documents for case team to deliver to client

o Facilitating access agreements process for teams interacting with investors (e.g., as part of a due diligence)

o Preparing and collecting document release forms, including Access Agreements for both PowerPoint and Excel materials
