职位简介

Job Description

In This Role, Your Responsibilities Will Be:

  • Responsible for the industrial software development and maintenance of laser welding and vibration welding products, including system control, motion control systems, data acquisition platforms and communication protocols, etc.
  • Collaborate with mechanical and electrical engineers to provide reliable and high-performance software solutions to support the product development of laser welding and vibration welding products.
  • Support the customized development needs and provide product technical support for application and service engineers.

Who You Are:
  • You maintain a positive demeanor despite adversity. You build driven and breakthrough strategies that show a clear connection between vision and action. You push yourself and help others achieve results. You act with a clear sense of ownership. You bounce back from setbacks.

For This Role, You Will Need:
  • Bachelor’s degree or above in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related majors.
  • Proficient in C/C++ or Python programming.
  • Experience in application development and deployment on Linux and Windows systems.
  • Experience in database design and applications (e.g., MySQL, Access, etc.).
  • Industrial automation software development or embedded system experience preferred.
  • Strong problem-solving, teamwork, and communication skills.
  • Good English reading and writing skills for technical documentation.

Preferred Qualifications That Set You Apart:
  • 3+ years of software development experience in engineering projects.
  • Experience with PLC interfaces or industrial communication protocols (such as Modbus, EtherCAT, CANopen).
  • Experience in high-performance control systems or automation data acquisition system development.
