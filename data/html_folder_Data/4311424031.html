职位简介

Position Overview:

To accelerate our expansion into core European markets, we are currently seeking one China-based Influencer Marketing Specialist each for the Italian, French, and German markets. You will serve as the influencer marketing associate for a single target country (based on your language proficiency: Italy/France/Germany), fully responsible for developing and executing influencer strategies, cultivating local resources, and enhancing brand influence and sales performance through strategic influencer collaborations.


What you’ll do:
  • Develop and execute influencer strategies tailored to your assigned market.
  • Source, negotiate with, and manage top-tier influencers across various social media platforms, including Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, etc.
  • Create compelling, localized content that resonates with European audiences.
  • Stay ahead of trends and adapt quickly to the evolving social media landscape.
  • Use data to optimize performance and drive real business impact.


What we’re looking for:
  • 1+ years of international influencer or content marketing experience
  • Deep understanding of your target market’s fashion culture and digital trends
  • Professional proficiency in Italian / French / German (CEFR B2+)
  • Strong English (CET-6 / IELTS 6.5+ / TOEFL 95+ preferred)
  • Ability to work across time zones and communicate effectively with local influencers


Bonus:
  • Experience in e-commerce, fast fashion, or women’s wear
  • Familiarity with KOL ecosystems and data tools in your target market
