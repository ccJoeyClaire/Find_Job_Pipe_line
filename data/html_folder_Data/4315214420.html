职位简介

Position Summary

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated SAP S/4 HANA master data consultant to join our team. The ideal candidate will take the lead in master data migration activities and support China SAP S/4 project roll in.

Strong English and Chinese communication and dot line report to Global team.

Extensive experience in master data solutions rollouts on SAP S/4 and data governance is essential. This candidate requires adhere to global solutions and coordinate with local business units to drive solu-tion acceptance and manage the following data migration activities.

Long-term support on master data relevant demands and coordinate with global solution team.

Experience of SAP MM or MDG is a must. Experience of SAP PP or SD is a plus.


Requirements

Minimal Qualifications:

• Bachelor’s degree in computer science, Information Technology, or a related technical field.

• 5-10 years of SAP experience within master data, focus on master data solutions, project rollouts, data migration and data governance activities.

• Proficient in master data functions and processes within SAP MM and MDG.

• Exceptional communication skills in English, with strong experience in coordinating between global solution team, local project team and global/local business team.

Preferred Qualifications:

• Certification in SAP MM or MDG module, or data governance related disciplines is a plus.

• Familiarity with SAP PP or SD is a plus.


Job Description

Main Tasks

• Following global data solution template and data governance, coordinate with local business to clarify data requirements, analysis local gap and drive solution acceptance.

• Strong communication skills to explain data concepts to business stakeholders.

• Demonstrated ability to work collaboratively with global team.

• Contribute master data migration during SAP S/4 roll-in projects or surrounding application.

• Provide long-term support for technical issues related to master data and deliver new demands in SAP S/4 HANA.

• Monitor the data quality of master data across SAP S/4, then build-up improvement plan and drive action with local business users.
