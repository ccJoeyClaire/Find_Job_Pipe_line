Staff Specialist Advanced Analytics & AI

英飞凌 · 中国 江苏省 无锡

职位简介

Involves the development and deployment of advanced statistical and computational methods to create insights and knowledge from data. Involves the collection, cleaning, analysis and visualization of large and complex data sets to identify patterns and trends, and the development of quantitative models and algorithms for data-driven decision making. Involves the AI model selection, training and deployment.

Job Description

In your new role you will:

  • To understand BE operation use cases and explore AI opportunities2.To understand data source and design/build data pipelines accordingly.3.To be AI project team member to work on technical part and collaborate closely with PJM, Data scientist, etc.4.Develop solution and sustain accordingly. (incl. using of AI model for usage, simple GUI, Tableau report, background scripts, etc.)5.Clean, transform, and validate raw data.6.Documentation to overall solution and transform to training material.7.Ensure data privacy and security protocols are followed.

Your Profile

You are best equipped for this task if you have:
  • Degrees in Computer Science, Information Technology or Data Science.2.Experienced in some of below tools from past projects.3.Excel & Google Sheets: For basic analysis and reporting.4.SQL: To retrieve and query structured data.5.Tableau & Power BI: For data visualization.6.Python & R: For more advanced analysis and statistical modeling.7.Apache Hadoop & Apache Spark: For processing large data sets.8.ETL Tools (Extract, Transform, Load): Like Apache Airflow or Talend.9.Cloud Platforms: AWS, Google Cloud, Azure.10.Databases: MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Snowflake.
