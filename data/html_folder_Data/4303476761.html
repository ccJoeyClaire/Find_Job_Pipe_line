Job Description

Trainee (TRA) comprises graduates taking part in Nokia's trainee programme (only for the duration of the programme). Trainees rotate through different functions to experience the company from the ground and up.

How You Will Contribute And What You Will Learn

  • Developing scripts and applications for AI/ML application
  • Developing back end website applications
  • Creating servers and databases for functionality
  • Designing and developing APIs

Key Skills And Experience
  • AI knowlege/experience is perfered
  • Familiar with Python/Java/Javascript
  • Familiar with database technology such as PostgreSQL and MongoDB
  • Fluent written and spoken English
  • Good problem solving skills
