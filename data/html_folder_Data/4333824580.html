Financial Analyst

福特 · 中国 上海市 (现场办公)

职位简介

Job Description

Financial planning and analysis, monthly accounting close and variance analysis

Responsibilities

Daily responsibilities differ across positions, but the responsibilities of a typical Finance Analyst may include:

  • Profit modelling and income statement analysis for future model vehicles
  • Cost tracking and reporting; analysis of cost reduction opportunities and investment returns
  • Analyzing volume or revenue trends, analyzing competitive price positions
  • Consolidation and distribution of market and functional forecast and budget submissions
  • Credit analysis and risk management
  • Cash Flow analysis, liquidity planning and control.
  • Regular data input and tracking; maintaining robust database information
  • Special studies as required


Qualifications
  • Bachelor or Master degree with excellent academic performance at a highly regarded university
  • Major in Finance, Accounting, Economics, Tax or Business Management
  • Strong analytical thinking, a detail-oriented approach to problem solving, strong communication skills and the ability to work as part of a team.
  • Ability to influence your own management and business partners to make the right decisions
  • Must take a proactive approach to your scope of work to deliver optimal results
  • Fluent in English, both speaking and writing
  • Proficient user of Microsoft Office suite of software
