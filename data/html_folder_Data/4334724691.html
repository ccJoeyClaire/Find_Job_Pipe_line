Description

 JOB DESCRIPTION

Honeywell Aerospace is one of the 4 strategic business groups (SBG) in Honeywell, a Fortune 500 company. It is a $13 billion global business and a provider of integrated avionics, engines, systems and services for airlines, aircraft manufacturers, business and general aviation, military, space and airport operations. Our vision is to transform the world with innovative Aerospace technologies and solutions, developed by a highly skilled and motivated global team.

As an integral part of Honeywell Aerospace’s global Engineering and Technology (E&T) team, we, Aero China Engineering team, provide world-class engineering and R&D capabilities to support Honeywell Aerospace growth including working with leading organizations like COMAC, AVIC, AECC, CETC and CAAC to support the rapid growth of the Chinese commercial aerospace industry. We also support key global programs for Airbus, Dassault, Pilatus, Embraer and Gulfstream.

The selected candidate would be working on Displays and Graphics Avionic Software Development for Honeywell’s latest integrated avionics system, which includes unsurpassed integration capabilities, best class navigation & situational awareness capabilities and most advanced pilot user experience. The current in-development programs include Dassault F6X, Pilatus PC12, PC24, Dornier SeaStar, etc.

Responsibilities

The Software Engineer position is responsible for performing design and software engineering activities associated with the Displays & Graphics, which includes:

  • Implement Software based on Requirement;
  • Support System checkout and Integration Test;
  • Issue Investigation and troubleshooting;
  • Perform Software Certification Activities based on criterion of FAA/EASA;

This role requires a strong knowledge and skills of software requirements implementing and related engineering lifecycles. The candidate would be working with various engineering groups.

Qualifications
  • Master’s Degree or bachelor’s degree (211/985, GPA 3.5+) in Aerospace Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Automatic Control Engineering, or a related field
  • Willing to choose Avionic Engineering as long term career direction;
  • Skilled in C/C++ programming on an embedded Real Time Operation System
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills in both English and Mandarin
  • Good Learning Capability, self-motivated
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Proactive attitudes on task commitment and strong teamwork mindset
