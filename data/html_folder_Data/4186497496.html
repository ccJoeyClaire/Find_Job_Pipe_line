职位简介

SCG in China—Where Innovation Meets Opportunity


With a legacy spanning over 100 years, Siam Cement Group (SCG) has transformed from a regional leader into a global force in industrial innovation. Headquartered in Thailand, SCG operates across three core businesses:
  • Cement & Building Materials – Providing construction solutions for large-scale infrastructure projects across Asia.
  • Chemicals – One of the largest integrated petrochemical companies in the region, specializing in high-performance polymers and sustainable materials.
  • Packaging – A leader in smart, sustainable packaging solutions, supporting industries across logistics, retail, and e-commerce.


While SCG is well-known across Southeast Asia, our ambitions in China are growing rapidly. As one of the world’s most dynamic markets, China plays a key role in our global strategy—and we are actively seeking talented professionals to be part of our journey.


SCG’s Presence in China—Expanding Opportunities

SCG has established a strong foundation in China, focusing on international trade, industrial solutions, and sustainable innovation. Our key operations include:
  • SCG International (China) – A strategic hub for cross-border trade, industrial sourcing, and supply chain solutions across China and Southeast Asia.
  • SCG Chemicals & Packaging – Supporting China’s manufacturing and sustainable development goals through high-performance polymers, eco-friendly packaging, and advanced material solutions.
  • Regional Expansion – With offices in Guangzhou, Nanning, Kunming, and a newly established Shanghai office, SCG is continuously investing in deeper market integration.


China’s economic and technological advancements present immense opportunities—and local talent is at the heart of our success.


Why Build Your Career at SCG?


1. Work on High-Impact, Global Projects

SCG operates across borders, connecting China with markets in ASEAN, Europe, and beyond. Joining SCG means working on international business expansion, advanced manufacturing, and digital transformation initiatives.

2. Accelerate Your Career Growth

We are not just hiring employees—we are developing future leaders. SCG provides structured career development, leadership programs, and opportunities to take on strategic roles within our global network.

3. Innovation & Sustainability at the Core

China is a hub for technology and green solutions. At SCG, you’ll be at the forefront of initiatives in AI, smart manufacturing, blockchain, renewable energy, and circular economy innovations.

4. A Multicultural, Collaborative Work Culture

SCG values diversity, and our China offices operate as international hubs, connecting professionals from different backgrounds to create a truly global work environment.

5. Competitive Benefits & Long-Term Career Stability

We offer:
  • Competitive salary packages aligned with China’s evolving job market.
  • Cross-border career mobility within SCG’s operations in China, ASEAN, and beyond.
  • Continuous learning & professional development with leadership training and upskilling programs.
