职位简介

About Tencent

Tencent is an Internet-based platform company founded in Shenzhen, China, in 1998. We use technology to enrich the lives of Internet users and assist the digital upgrade of enterprises. Our mission is "Value for Users, Tech for Good". We embrace a culture of teamwork & creativity and are driven by our values - Integrity, Proactivity, Collaboration and Creativity.


We are rapidly expanding our international operations and are looking for top talent to propel us forward. Combining the results-oriented nature of a start-up with the resources of a profitable and leading Internet company, Tencent offers a unique opportunity for aspiring individuals to thrive.


Responsibilities
  • Act as HRBP to the IEGG business lines in HQ, providing localized support and ensuring alignment with global HR strategies.
  • Being a trusted advisor to provide insights on business decisions from a human capital perspective which includes but not limited to talent management, performance management, workforce strategy, employee engagement & retention.
  • Collaborate closely with the business leaders on people matters which may have short-, medium- or long-term effects on the business
  • Build strong partnerships with HR teams with both local and global investment companies and develop deep understanding of organizational dynamics in each entity
  • Identify opportunities for resource sharing and ensuring compliance in workforce decisions
  • Driving and helping to connect the dots between global/local decisions in contrast with HQ decisions, to mediate and align expectations across all parties
  • Lead and manage cross-functional HR projects and collaborate with other BPs to promote strategic alignment and project execution.


Challenges
  • Ability to operate in ambiguous and grey-zone scopes — defining and creating value where frameworks don’t yet exist.
  • Requires self-direction and initiative to build new relationships, identify gaps, and drive change.
  • Must be comfortable navigating cross-cultural stakeholder management and regional diversity.


Qualifications
  • Above 10 years of experience as a HR Business Partner, demonstrating strong stakeholder management skills and business acumen
  • Possess exposure across the gaming industry, ideally with experience supporting overseas businesses in their expansion
  • Possess experience dealing with Performance Management, Rewards, Talent retention, Talent Management and Employee Relations
  • Someone who is positive, possess a can-do attitude and can navigate in highly matrixed and ambiguous environments
  • Strong people skillsets and able to build relationships across all levels of staff, be culturally sensitive and adaptable to different people styles
  • Strong learning agility, with good ability to bounce back and quickly learn from past experiences, improve, and adapt
  • Bilingual abilities (English/Chinese) will be considered a competitive edge as it will be required to deal with employees locally
