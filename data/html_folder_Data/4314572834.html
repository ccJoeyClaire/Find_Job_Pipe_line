职位简介

Hardware Engineer | Rise Program 2025

Job Details Enabling a software-defined, electrified future. Visteon is a technology company that develops and builds innovative digital cockpit and electrification products at the leading edge of the mobility revolution. Founded in 2000, Visteon brings decades of automotive intelligence combined with Silicon Valley speed to apply global insights that help transform the software-defined vehicle of the future for many of the world’s largest OEMs. The company employs 10,000 employees in 18 countries around the globe. To know more about us click here . Mission of the Role: Visteon's global Early Career program is a unique opportunity designed specifically for recent or soon-to-be electrical/ telecommunications engineer graduates. It serves as a launching pad for your professional journey in the automotive technology field. Our RISE program aims to bridge the gap between your academic life and the professional world by providing you with the necessary resources to enhance your skills and expand your knowledge of the mobility technology industry. As a member of our RISE program, you will undergo a training course led by industry professionals. This training will help you transition smoothly into working alongside highly skilled engineering teams. Additionally, you will have the privilege of being mentored throughout this process. We believe that this program will provide you with a solid foundation to kickstart your professional career in the world of automotive technologies. Key Objectives of this Role:

Participate in Full Hardware Development Lifecycle:

  • Assist in project schedule tracking and documentation management
  • Learn hardware design specification verification processes
  • Support creation of manufacturing hardware EBOM (Engineering Bill of Materials)

Hardware Design & Development:
  • Collaborate on schematic design and support PCB layout engineers
  • Prepare hardware design/software interface documents under mentorship
  • Gain hands-on experience in creating hardware quality documents (WCCA/DFMEA)

Testing & Validation Support:
  • Assist in hardware performance testing (EMC/reliability/certification tests)
  • Participate in defect analysis and design improvement implementation
  • Provide basic technical support for production lines

Continuous Improvement:
  • Engage in cost optimization studies
  • Support engineering change request implementation

Qualification, Experience and Skills:

  • Graduate with bachelor’s/master’s degree in Electrical/Electronic Engineering
  • Solid foundation in analog/digital circuit principles
  • Academic exposure or project experience in:
    • Basic schematic design tools (Mentor/Cadence)
    • Lab equipment operation (oscilloscopes/multimeters)
    • Fundamental PCB layout concepts
  • Strong teamwork mindset and learning agility
  • CET-4 or equivalent English proficiency for technical documentation
Key Behaviors:

  • Collaboration;
  • Team Spirit;
  • Problem-Solving;
  • Critical Thinking;
  • Result Orientation.

Reporting Structure: Hardware Manager Visteon Culture: If you thrive in a fast-paced, organizational culture that requires agility, adaptability, and a growth mindset from its employees to thrive and stay ahead of the curve Visteon is the place. We value high performance and a drive for results. Innovation, risk-taking, and continuous learning help us keep up with the ever-changing landscape of our industry and be Market leaders. About Visteon

Visteon is advancing mobility through innovative technology solutions that enable a software-defined future. Our state-of-the-art product portfolio merges digital cockpit innovations, advanced displays, AI-enhanced software solutions, and integrated EV architecture solutions. With expertise spanning passenger vehicles, commercial transportation, and two-wheelers, Visteon partners with global automakers to create safer, cleaner, and more connected journeys. Headquartered in Van Buren Township, Michigan, Visteon operates in 17 countries, employing a global network of innovation centers and manufacturing facilities. In 2024, we recorded annual sales of approximately $3.87 billion and secured $6.1 billion in new business.
