职位简介

A Career at HARMAN Lifestyle

We’re a global, multi-disciplinary team that’s putting the innovative power of technology to work and transforming tomorrow. As a member of HARMAN Lifestyle, you connect consumers with the power of superior sound.

  • Contribute your talents to high-end, esteemed brands like JBL, Mark Levinson and Revel
  • Unite your passion for audio innovation with high-tech product development
  • Create pitch-perfect, cutting-edge technology that elevates the listening experience

About The Role

The Program Manager will oversee the planning & execution of the assigned programs from product concept to mass production. Lead a multi-discipline team to deliver programs to meet business requirements.

What You Will Do
  • Manage cross functional development teams through a phase gate development process.
  • Manage project timelines (Gantt) task based schedules.
  • Insure accurate product documentation for in-house and ODM supplier designs.
  • Conduct regular team meetings, establishing action items and due dates for activities of Acoustics Engineering, Electronic Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Sourcing, Quality, Supplier, etc.
  • Act as Team Leader and spokesperson (in conjunction with Management) as final arbitrator in Team decisions
  • Insure product collaterals such as photo samples, carton artwork, user manuals and other product literature are developed, reviewed and released for production.
  • Coordinate timely submission of Safety Agency samples on electronic items.
  • Support Supplier's personnel in planning and completing their Design Verification (DV), Process Verification (PV) and Production runs.
  • Provide regular project reports showing status of each program in development.
  • Track progress of cost, budget and timing for each program, reporting any variances and suggesting ways to bring program back to plan.
  • Ensure product concept is preserved, requested performance and application requirements of the product are met, and appearance and packaging standards are adhered to.

What You Need To Be Successful
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business or BSME/BSEE or equivalent technical degree desirable.
  • 5-7 years of previous project management experience in the mid to high volume consumer products industry.
  • Project management experience in the headphone industry is preferred.
  • Requires PC skills using programs such as Microsoft Outlook, Excel, Word and Project and PowerPoint.
  • Previous experience using automated 'Gantt type' scheduling tools.
  • Previous project budgeting and resources loading knowledge/experience.
  • Previous experience in dealing with Asia suppliers.
  • Manual dexterity and lifting ability needed to receive and unpack, and/or build up and pack samples as needed.
  • Must be able to multi-task and have good written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong leadership, management and decision making skills.
  • Professional program management certification (PMP) desirable.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects effectively at one time.
  • Must speak fluent English.

What Makes You Eligible
  • Willingness to travel domestically or internationally for business needs.
  • Willingness to work in an office.
  • Any offer of employment is conditioned upon the successful completion of a background investigation。
