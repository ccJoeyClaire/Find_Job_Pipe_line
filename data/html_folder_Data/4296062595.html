Work you'll do

  • Data Management Analyst is responsible for supporting the on-going qualitative and quantitative data management initiatives. Analyst employs an array of data management techniques and frameworks to ensure the accuracy, timeliness and consistency of key attributes, taxonomies and metrics are managed in the Firm’s systems.
  • Supports data management processes that include workflow, validation and data cleansing activities, entity data management, hierarchy maintenance, taxonomy, contacts and opportunity data management
  • Consistently follows pre-defined, repeatable data management processes.
  • Managing end-to-end basic Data Quality Management processes (e.g., Client Data Profiling, Data Cleansing, etc.) and make significant enhancements to complex Data Quality Management processes (e.g., Data Change Monitoring, Data Assumptive Techniques, etc.)
  • Managing basic Client Segmentation processes
  • Support basic Data Source Intelligence processes (e.g., Source Evaluation, Source Identification, etc)
  • Making significant enhancements (find new external data, execute adds/deletes, etc.) to basic Client Data Integration processes (e.g., Data Match/Merge, Data Change Management, Data Source Integration, etc.)
  • Designing basic Business Process Improvement processes (e.g., Common Methodology Identification, Research Optimization, etc.)
  • Understanding the critical importance of achieving deadlines and identifies issues that may impact on our service delivery
  • Creates and owns process documentation and training materials.
  • Self-trains on supplemental skills for data management methodologies through e-room, publications, etc.
  • Executes processes that support data integrity within standard and non-standard firm systems.
  • Supports basic extract, transformation, and load (ETL) of external information into common storage platform.
