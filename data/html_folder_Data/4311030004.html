职位简介

Purpose of Position

  • CASETiFY is one of the leading global lifestyle brands to offer customizable tech accessories. We're industry leaders when it comes to trend forecasting and protecting what matters most; the community platform for creativity and self-expression.
  • Assist mainly colab/product launches on website/mobile APP
  • Assist in daily operations for visual merchandising of the website (navigation, home banner)
  • Assist Ecom Team to achieve company’s business goals by launching projects


Job Description
  • Assist in project executions - website changes/collection launches/new products/marketing campaigns and content update on web/APP
  • Coordinate with different departments to obtain related materials during the launch preparation including but not limited to copies, translation, web/APP creative assets, and pricing, and sure that they are all up to the business standard.
  • Create web pages for all responsible projects
  • Arrange contents, translations, and pricing for all regional markets at Casetify (localization) in web
  • Ensure the quality of the project output (Quality check) and fix the issues promptly. Lead quality check (QC) for the website with market teams and follow up proactively
  • Ensure and gate keep that the project launch follows the launch guidelines
  • Cooperate with Tech team on system setting/ technical request and ensure accurate website display and rules
  • Coordinate with Creative team on creative assets for the website
  • Cross check with Product Development team on inventory status and provide accurate information on website/APP


Requirement
  • Native Cantonese / Proficient in English
  • Highly systematic and organized
  • Have web / digital project management experience (1-2 Yr)
  • Excellent communications skills and problem solving skills
  • Always curious to learn new things and willing to share knowledge
  • Able to work under tight timeline and pressure
  • Knowledge in MySQL will be a big plus
