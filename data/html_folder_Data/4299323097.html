DOO you have what it takes?

We are looking for a Business Intelligence Analyst with expertise in Microsoft BI Stack (Power BI, SQL Server, SSRS) to transform data into actionable insights, optimize reporting processes, and drive data-driven decision-making.


What you’ll be working on:
  • Work within an agile team to develop and implement BI solutions.
  • Collaborate with management and stakeholders to understand business objectives and data needs.
  • Design, build, and deploy Microsoft BI solutions to enhance decision-making.
  • Develop and execute database queries, optimize data models, and maintain technical documentation.
  • Analyze business processes and recommend improvements for BI usage and reporting.
  • Create and maintain Power BI dashboards and reports using Power Query and DAX.
  • Conduct regular team meetings to track progress and ensure project objectives are met.
  • Provide insights and recommendations based on data analysis.
  • Perform other related duties as assigned.


What we’re looking for:

Background:
  • Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Data Science, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • 1-3 years of experience in business intelligence, data analytics, or a related role.


Required Skills:
  • 1-3 years of experience with Microsoft BI Stack, including SQL Server (Advanced knowledge), Reporting Services (SSRS), Power BI (Power Query, DAX).
  • Strong background in data warehousing and ETL processes.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Strong communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment and take initiative.


Nice-to-Have Skills:
  • Familiarity with MongoDB or other databases.
  • Experience with data analysis programming (e.g., Python, R).
  • Knowledge of business intelligence tools and emerging technologies.
  • Solid understanding of statistics and data modeling.
  • Proficient in writing and speaking Mandarin and English.
