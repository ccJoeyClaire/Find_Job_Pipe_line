Job Description

  • Lead the monthly, quarterly, and annual COGS forecasting process, ensuring accuracy and timeliness.
  • Consolidate COGS forecasts across 9 factories, providing clear insights to management.
  • Develop and maintain forecasting infrastructure, tools, and dashboards to deliver real-time visibility for Business Units, Operations, and Management.
  • Drive process and efficiency improvements and streamlining workflows
  • Track and analyze forecast accuracy, identifying variances and implementing improvements.
  • Partner with stakeholders across Business Units, Product Management, and Finance to support business case, financial analysis and ensuring consistency and integrity of financial plans.
  • Consolidate and track weekly factory site cost outlooks, providing timely updates to leadership.
  • Coordinate and manage the Finance team’s quarterly forecast schedule to meet corporate deadlines.
  • Perform ad-hoc financial analysis and reporting as required to support decision-making.

Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience in accounting or financial analysis, ideally within a manufacturing environment.
  • Strong analytical, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, with attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills, with the ability to convey complex financial information clearly to diverse audiences.
  • Fluent in spoken and written English.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Power BI, and financial modeling tools; ability to build and manage complex financial models.
  • Experience with Oracle ERP or similar enterprise systems is highly desirable.
