职位简介

Join a global graduate programme in software engineering designed to develop future technology leaders.


Our teams build and maintain digital platforms, systems, and products that millions of customers and colleagues depend on every day. This is an opportunity to get hands-on with the latest technologies while solving real-world challenges.


Eligibility Criteria
  • Available full-time for 2 years starting March 2026 or July 2026.
  • Graduating in 2025 (for March intake) or 2026 (for July intake).
  • Minimum 2.1 degree (UK) or 3.0 GPA (US), or equivalent, in a relevant STEM/technology field.
  • Fluent in English and Mandarin.
  • Legally permitted to work in mainland China.


Who We’re Looking For

We’re seeking ambitious graduates who love using technology to make an impact.

You’ll be a great fit if you are:
  • Collaborative – enjoy working across diverse teams and cultures.
  • Analytical – skilled at breaking down complex challenges into clear solutions.
  • Quality-driven – committed to delivering high-standard, efficient code.
  • Proactive – take ownership of your work and look for ways to improve.
  • Eager to learn – ready to adopt new languages, frameworks, and AI tools.
  • Self-aware – thoughtful in your communication and teamwork.


What You’ll Do

Depending on your team, you could be working on a range of exciting projects, including:
  • Developing and enhancing digital products and services.
  • Designing, testing, and maintaining enterprise systems.
  • Partnering with engineers and architects to shape new technology solutions.
  • Building scalable, user-friendly applications.
  • Learning DevOps practices to deliver software faster and more reliably.
  • Monitoring system performance, resolving issues, and driving improvements.


What You’ll Learn

Over two years, you’ll combine training, coaching, and real project experience, including:
  • Exposure to coding languages, frameworks, and modern tech stacks.
  • Classroom learning, self-led development, and hackathons.
  • Industry-recognised certifications (funded by the programme).
  • Opportunities to specialise in technical expertise or step into project leadership.


Application Journey
  • Complete a short online coding test as part of the process.
  • Followed by interviews and placement into a team aligned with your strengths.
