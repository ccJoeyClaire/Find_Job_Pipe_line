职位简介

Do you want to challenge yourself as an analyst in one of the world's leading energy knowledge houses?

Rystad Energy is a leading global independent research and energy intelligence company dedicated to helping clients navigate the future of energy. By providing high-quality data and thought leadership, our international team empowers businesses, governments and organizations to make well-informed decisions.

Our extensive portfolio of products and solutions covers all aspects of global energy fundamentals, spanning every corner of the oil and gas industry, renewables, clean technologies, supply chain and power markets. Headquartered in Oslo, Norway, with an expansive global network, our data, analysis, advisory and education services provide clients a competitive edge in the market.

For more information, visit www.rystadenergy.com.

We are looking for analysts to strengthen our growing global team of researchers in Beijing and Shanghai offices.

The analyst department consists of five groups: Oil and Gas, Renewable and Power, Commodity Markets, Energy Services, and Energy Transition. Rystad Energy is looking for motivated candidates with a strong academic track record and an analytical mindset to fulfill the growing demand of our research across these groups.

Analysts are the backbone of the company and are responsible for our databases and reports, which are used by clients around the world. We need more people to gather, analyze and present our research to our clients. As an analyst you will collaborate frequently with consultants, programmers, and sales and marketing people.

We are seeking candidates who are graduating with a bachelor or master's degree in the fall of 2025 or spring of 2026 that want to join a leading energy intelligence research firm.

Tasks
  • Gather, process, analyze and synthesize information from multiple sources
  • Develop, maintain, and optimize energy data collection and processing workflows
  • Develop new forecast models and processes to analyze our data
  • Contribute and write client commentaries and reports
  • Participate in external and internal presentations
  • Contribute to sales and business development over time
  • Engage with clients to demonstrate our research and analyses
  • Travel when needed for research trips, client engagement, and business development support

Requirements / Skills
  • Strong academic track record in relevant education field (e.g. engineering, finance, economics, math, data science)
  • Desire to work with both the technical and financial aspects of the energy market
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with a hands-on attitude
  • Outstanding ability to synthesize data and information coming from multiple sources (quantitative and qualitative)
  • Solid proficiency in Excel and Power Point
  • Strong communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Strong interest in or direct work experience in the energy sector
  • Fluent in English and Mandarin; other languages are a plus

We offer
  • Flexible career path given competence and interests
  • Hands-on mentorship with an experienced analyst
  • A diverse, dynamic and highly skilled working environment
  • Competitive compensation and benefits
  • Development opportunities and relocation internationally

Application deadline: 21 November 2025. No feedback will be available until the applications close.

Please submit all resumes and cover letters in English
