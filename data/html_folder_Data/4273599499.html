职位简介

Job Description：

  • Conduct in-depth research and analysis of massive datasets to mine effective Alpha signals, optimize and iterate signal fusion models, and enhance predictive capabilities.
  • Develop and optimize model strategies, assist fund managers in constructing and optimizing investment portfolios, and improve the performance and strategy capacity of investment portfolios.
  • Track and optimize the performance of quantitative strategies in live trading, and conduct regular attribution analysis of investment portfolio performance.

Location：

Beijing/Shanghai


Job Qualifications:
  • Major in Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science, Statistics, Operations Research, Electronics, Financial Engineering, or other related science and engineering disciplines.
  • Proficiency in Python/ C++ programming languages.
  • Possess solid foundations, in-depth understanding, and practical experience in one of the following fields:
  • In the field of probability and statistics, analysis and optimization, including but not limited to stochastic processes, time series analysis, operations research and optimization, etc.
  • In the field of data science and machine learning: including but not limited to data mining, recommendation systems, natural language processing, image recognition, reinforcement learning, etc.
  • Have rigorous research literacy, think actively and seek verification, have a strong interest in solving complex problems, and possess strong self-motivation.


Bonus Points:
  • Outstanding achievements in national or international academic competitions, such as the CMO (Chinese Mathematical Olympiad), CPhO (Chinese Physics Olympiad), or NOI (National Olympiad in Informatics).
  • Published papers in top-tier conferences or journals in fields such as Computer Science, Statistics, or Operations Research.
  • Prior internship experience in quantitative research with demonstrable research outcomes.


What We Offer:
  • Ample computing resources, vast data reserves, and a proprietary factor library developed and refined over more than 10 years of dedicated quantitative research.
  • Matching the most suitable mentor according to personal interests and personality, with a 1V1 mentoring mechanism combining standardized training and customized projects.
  • A pure, technology- and research-driven environment, ensuring a seamless transition from academia to the workplace.
  • Clear career progression paths and opportunities for inter-departmental transfers, along with competitive compensation, benefits, and incentive packages for outstanding talent.


About Ubiquant:

Ubiquant is positioned as a technology company that specializes in quantitative methods for value discovery. In 2012, we became one of the first companies in the domestic financial market to adopt a technology-driven scientific investment approach, focusing on quantitative investment strategies supported by big data and mathematical algorithms.

After over a decade of development,Ubiquant Investment has evolved into a China-based quantitative hedge fund with a global perspective. We rely on top international talent in mathematics, physics, computer science, and other professional fields to combine cutting-edge research and technology with financial investment. Our goal is to create long-term and stable value for investors.
