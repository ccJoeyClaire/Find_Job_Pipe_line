职位简介

一、全球高层次人才需求

I. Seeking high-level talents globally

地质、采矿、选矿、冶金、环境、化学、新材料、机械制造、自动化、计算机应用、软件工程、人工智能、人力资源、会计、新闻、中文等前沿领域和关键领域的高层次科技人才。

High-level scientific and technological professionals are needed in such frontier and key sectors as geology, mining, mineral processing, metallurgy, environment, chemistry, new materials, machinery manufacturing, automation, computer applications, software engineering, artificial intelligence, human resources, accounting, journalism, and Chinese language.
  • 首席科学家

1. Chief Scientist

一般应当具有博士学位，在国内外著名高校、科研机构或世界一流企业担任高级职务，在本专业领域取得较大国际影响力的高水平研发人才。岗位对应专业技术通道职级（参照研究院技术序列L10级标准）。

The applicant is generally required to hold a doctoral degree and to have served in senior positions at prestigious universities, research institutions, or world-class enterprises at home or abroad. The applicant should be top-level R&D talents with substantial international influence in their professional fields.

The position corresponds to the professional and technical level of L10 in the CGRI’s technical career path.

2.技术带头人

2. Technical Leader

具有博士学位，在国内外知名企业、科研院所、高等院校正式工作不少于3年;或在国内外知名企业、科研院所、高等院校从事博士后研究2年以上。在相关专业领域取得一定影响力的科研成果，具有较大发展潜力，能够作为科研团队核心骨干支撑重大科研任务。岗位对应专业技术通道职级（参照研究院技术序列L9级标准）。

The applicant must hold a doctoral degree and have worked formally for at least three years in well-known enterprises, research institutions, or universities at home or abroad; or have conducted postdoctoral research for more than two years in such institutions. It is necessary that the applicant has produced influential research achievements in the relevant field, demonstrate strong potential for further development, and be able to serve as a key member of research teams undertaking major research projects.

The position corresponds to the professional and technical level of L9 in the CGRI’s technical career path.

3.高级技术专家

3. Senior Technical Expert

取得相关专业领域世界知名高校的博士学位，具有较强的科研创新潜力，从事国际前沿领域研究，特别优秀者可适当放宽申报条件。岗位对应专业技术通道职级（参照研究院技术序列L8级标准）。

The applicant must hold a doctoral degree from a well-known university in a relevant field and demonstrate strong potential for scientific and technological innovation. They should engage in research at the international frontier. Exceptionally outstanding applicants may be granted appropriate flexibility in the qualification requirements.

The position corresponds to the professional and technical level of L8 in the CGRI’s technical career path.

4.技术专家

4. Technical Expert

具有硕士学位，能承担重大科研项目的工程实现任务，推动科研成果落地实施。具备相关专业的工程实践基础。岗位对应专业技术通道职级（参照研究院技术序列L7级标准）。

The applicant must hold a master’s degree and be capable of undertaking implementation tasks in major research projects to promote the application and industrialization of scientific research achievements. They should have a solid foundation in engineering practice relevant to their professional field.

The position corresponds to the professional and technical level of L7 in the CGRI’s technical career path.
