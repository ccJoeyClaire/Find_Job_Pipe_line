职位简介

Overview

We are seeking a highly motivated and analytical professional to join our APAC team to drive excellence in demand planning and S&OP processes. Reporting to the Operations Director, APAC - this role plays a key part in coordinating regional forecasts, supporting data-driven decision-making, and enhancing forecast accuracy across multiple markets. The ideal candidate is a proactive team player with strong communication skills, a keen eye for detail, and the ability to thrive in a dynamic, matrixed organization. The position will be based at our plant in Suzhou.

Main Responsibilities

  • Coordinate, support, and consolidate the monthly rolling 12-month forecast for all IG businesses across Asia Pacific.
  • Upload the regional rolling forecast into the IBP system.
  • Lead and facilitate the APAC S&OP Demand Review meetings.
  • Oversee and standardize regional forecast reporting processes, ensuring timely and consistent monthly updates.
  • Provide analytical support to enhance forecast accuracy and identify opportunities for continuous improvement.
  • Support APAC-wide S&OP initiatives and related projects.

Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree in Supply Chain Management, Business Administration, or a related discipline
  • Minimum of 5 years of progressive experience in supply chain functions including planning, procurement, and demand analysis.
  • Prior experience in a manufacturing environment is highly desirable.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with strong command of English, with the ability to collaborate effectively across diverse teams and functions.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and critical-thinking capabilities.
  • Demonstrated negotiation skills with the ability to influence and achieve win-win outcomes.
  • Proven experience in leading change initiatives within a complex and highly matrixed organization.
  • Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint.
  • Hands-on experience with JDE or other ERP systems.
