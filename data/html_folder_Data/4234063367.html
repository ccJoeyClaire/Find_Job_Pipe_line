We're looking for a material, surface finishing, and color engineer to join the Snap Lab team!

What you’ll do:

  • Develop and oversee the implementation of machining processes for plastics, metals, glass and other materials.
  • Ensure high-quality machining components that meet Snap's stringent standards for functional and durability.
  • Innovate and explore new machining technologies and techniques to provide Snap products with unique and superior quality.
  • Optimize machining processes to address cosmetic and functional issues.
  • Work with PD, ID, and SQE teams to drive material for sample reviews, in order to align on targets or specs with other cross-functional teams.
  • Identify, evaluate, and manage relationships with suppliers specializing in machining for plastics, metals, and glass.
  • Conduct regular audits and assessments of supplier capabilities and performance.
  • Develop and enforce rigorous quality control standards for all machining processes.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve any machining issues that arise during product development and into production.
  • Provide technical guidance and support to internal teams on machining feasibility.
  • Stay current with industry trends and advancements in novel materials and in new manufacturing processes.
  • Expected travel 25% to Asia-region countries and occasional travel to the US.

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities:
  • Deep understanding of the machining process of plastics, metals, glass and other materials.
  • Familiarity with various machining techniques and their impact on final quality.
  • Extensive hands-on experience with CNC programming.
  • Extensive experience and knowledge of CNC machine operation and optimization.
  • Ability to identify root causes and implement effective corrective actions.
  • Excellent project management skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines.
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
  • Effective communication skills to work with cross-functional teams and external suppliers.
  • Ability to convey technical information clearly and concisely.
  • Passion for innovation and continuous improvement in surface finishing techniques.
  • Ability to stay updated with industry trends and implement cutting-edge technologies.

Minimum Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering, or a related field.
  • 5+ years hands-on 3 and 5 axis CNC machining experience.
  • Experience with machining,tool selection and fixture design.
  • Proficiency with CAD / CAM.
  • Ability to design manufacturing processes from concept to implementation.
  • Hands on experience with application of GD&T.
  • Proven track record of creating and managing deep supplier relationships.
  • Experience in a fast-paced manufacturing environment, preferably in the consumer electronics or technology industry.

Preferred Qualifications:
  • MS, or PhD in Mechanical, Manufacturing, Industrial Engineering, or related field.
  • 10+ years Siemens NC CAM programming experience.
  • Deep knowledge of GD&T
  • High volume manufacturing experience with high precision and cosmetic parts.
  • CNC Machine Maintenance and repair.
  • Excellent problem solver with a foundation in Six Sigma
  • Familiarity with machine learning and deep learning applications in manufacturing
  • Proficient in written and verbal communication in both Mandarin and English
