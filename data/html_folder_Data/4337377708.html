职位简介

Your role

As a HR Manager, Start Up Company , you will report to General Manager, New Start-up Company.

You will

  • Develop and own the end-to-end talent acquisition strategy for the company, aligning hiring plans with business objectives and growth projections.
  • Establish and refine the hiring framework to ensure all recruitment activities and decisions consistently reflect the group Cultural Behaviors and core values.
  • Coordinate essential training sessions focused on compliance, systems, and core professional skills to enhance organization capabilities.
  • Establish metrics and evaluation methods to assess training effectiveness, continuously refining programs to meet evolving business needs.
  • Provide expert advice and guidance to the managers and employees to mitigate risk and resolve employee relations concerns when they occur.
  • Proactively manage and resolve workplace conflicts, performance issues, and grievances, ensuring strict compliance with all labor laws and company policies.
  • Design and manage an equitable and compliant compensation structure, including salary benchmarks and benefits programs, based on market analysis and VT HR Strategy.
  • Administer all aspects of payroll coordination and ensure accurate and timely processing in adherence to local regulations.
  • Promote and build up AC culture and values in the acquired companies so to have the common awareness and expected behaviors from the new employees.

To succeed, you will need

We encourage you to apply even if you don't meet every single requirement. We value diverse experiences and perspectives and are excited to see what you bring to the role.
  • Minimum of 10 years of experience in HR field with at least 5 years of management experience. Experience of working with a variety of corporate cultures and business areas, including but not limited to skilled, professional and senior management populations is highly desirable
  • Ability to deal with conflict – communication, negotiation, persuasion and influencing skills, ability to work independently, whilst also being able to judge when additional support is required
  • Up to date knowledge of appropriate employment legislation and evidence of putting employment law knowledge into practice
  • Previous experience in managing projects, design and implementation of HR tools
  • Experience of supporting/delivering culture change projects
  • Strong interpersonal and leadership skills
  • Ability to set own priorities, manage multiple tasks and reschedule work in a changing environment
  • Team player with experience of management and supervision of direct reports as well ability to build support from key stakeholders in order to successfully achieve goals
  • Fluent in English and Mandarin

In return, we offer
  • Culture of trust and accountability
  • Lifelong learning and career growth
  • Innovation powered by people
  • Comprehensive compensation and benefits
  • Health and well-being
