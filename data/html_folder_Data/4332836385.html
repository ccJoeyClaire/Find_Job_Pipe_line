职位简介

At L'Oréal, innovation has been our driving force since 1909, when chemist Eugène Schueller founded the company. Our Research & Innovation (R&I) division remains at the heart of this legacy, constantly pushing the boundaries of beauty science. Within R&I, the International Operational Excellence team plays a crucial role in ensuring we conduct this groundbreaking research in the most effective and efficient way possible. We act as a transversal change agent, optimizing R&I's operating model by implementing best practices, streamlined processes, and cutting-edge technologies. From designing and managing state-of-the-art workspaces and equipment to overseeing environmental, health, and safety standards, quality systems, and our advanced chemical analysis department, our mission is to create an environment where innovation thrives with serenity, speed, and scale. Join our dynamic team and play a key role in shaping the future of R&I's global footprint.

About The Role

As a Real Estate Project Manager for China R&I Hub, you will oversee a major real estate project for China R&I Hub, collaborating closely with the Group Real Estate Department and Campus Management teams. This role will be based in Pudong.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead and manage a China R&I real estate project from inception to completion, including:
    • Defining project scope, goals and requirements in collaboration with internal stakeholders.
    • Overseeing architectural competitions and conducting comprehensive project analyses (architectural, technical, workplace, sustainability, financial, and legal).
    • Follow-up of design studies and obtaining necessary administrative authorizations.
    • Follow-up of construction phases, ensuring adherence to timelines and budgets.
    • Orchestrating the final delivery phase and employee move-in.
  • Develop detailed project plans, timelines, budgets, and resource allocation strategies.
  • Ensure strict adherence to project timelines and budget while maintaining high-quality standards.
  • Cooperate with the corporate and North Asia Real Estate team, that will provide operational support, expertise and lead the execution phase
  • Ensure Group’s guidelines are followed at every step of the project
Your Profile
  • Master’s degree in engineering, project management, or a related field.
  • Minimum 7 years of experience managing complex projects, ideally gained within a real estate development company, project management firm, general contractor, or corporate real estate department.
  • Strong understanding of tertiary buildings, particularly offices and/or laboratories, with a keen awareness of the specific needs of research environments (flow and space optimization, equipment requirements, storage, etc.).
  • Proven ability to manage complex projects from concept to completion, including budgeting, scheduling, risk assessment, stakeholder communication, and problem-solving.
  • Keen on sustainable building solutions.
  • Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills, with the ability to influence and collaborate effectively in a matrix organization.
  • Fluency in English is essential.

What We Offer
  • A unique opportunity to shape the future of beauty innovation.
  • A dynamic and challenging work environment within a passionate and collaborative team.
  • Exposure to a unique project that will share the future of R&I China.

If you are a results-oriented, highly organized, and collaborative individual with a passion for real estate and innovation, we encourage you to apply!
