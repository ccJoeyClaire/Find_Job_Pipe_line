Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Master the 3DEXPERIENCE platform usage by maintaining knowledge of the Industry Process Experiences, for a set of Industry or Brand offers.

  • Propose best methodologies and usages of the 3DEXPERIENCE platform related to customers’ business processes modeling, analysis and reengineering on targeted scope
  • Maximize and valorize the 3DEXPERIENCE Platform standard usage while minimizing and documenting the potential gaps and their impacts, and provide reporting to the account team or project team in order to anticipate and mitigate risks on the engagement.
  • Closely collaborate with the DS internal ecosystem.
  • Identify, document and capitalize the best practices on the specific SWYM Community. Document storyboards through the capture of use cases to support iteration workshops with clients and convergence on the proposition to address targeted business processes Perform standard solutions demonstration from the EXPERIENCE Center.
