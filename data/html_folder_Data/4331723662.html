About The Role

Position Overview

We are seeking a highly motivated Editorial Data Analyst to join our Business Analytics & Enablement team at Wiley. This role offers an exciting opportunity to work in a collaborative environment, leveraging data analytics and business intelligence tools to influence the performance of our products, platforms, and services within Research Publishing. The ideal candidate will be passionate about data discovery, possess strong analytical and communication skills, and enjoy using data to inform key decisions and drive continuous improvement across editorial operations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Performance Reporting and Analysis
  • Generate and review product, service, and platform performance reports and dashboards, sharing insights with key stakeholders.
  • Support and maintain Research Publishing's specific data tools, ensuring alignment across editorial models.
  • Monitor and assess platform and product performance, providing analytical support to drive improvements and meet KPIs.
  • Present data insights to stakeholders in a clear and actionable format.
  • Data Insights and Stakeholder Collaboration
  • Analyze performance trends across journals, services, and platforms, with a focus on both routine operations and periods of heightened attention (e.g., following releases).
  • Collaborate with internal teams such as Peer Review Performance Managers, Editorial Vendor Managers, and in-house editors to improve product/service performance.
  • Data Integrity Management
  • Ensure data integrity by troubleshooting issues and collaborating with stakeholders to provide solutions for data validity concerns.
  • Document activities in line with departmental frameworks, ensuring reproducibility and discoverability.
  • Decision-Making and Data Communication
  • Evaluate the significance of data insights and decide when to take action or communicate findings.
  • Identify key stakeholders who need to be informed of specific analyses and provide appropriate recommendations.
  • Offer analytical support for maintaining, optimizing, or sunsetting products or services.

Required Qualifications
  • Education:
  • Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical field.
  • Experience:
  • 1-3 years of relevant or transferable experience in data analytics or a related field.
  • Experience with data analytics methodologies, including data cleaning, exploration, and visualization.
  • Technical Skills:
  • Proficiency in SQL (Intermediate to Advanced level).
  • Experience with BI dashboard development (Intermediate to Advanced level), ideally in PowerBI.
  • Strong expertise in Microsoft Excel and data analysis.
  • Experience in using statistical tools like SPSS, SAS, STATA, or programming languages like Python or R for data analysis.
  • Familiarity with Power Automate is a plus.
  • Core Competencies:
  • Strong analytical and communication skills with a passion for data.
  • Ability to collaborate across teams and gain trust from various stakeholders.
  • Results-oriented, self-motivated, and able to work independently to achieve objectives.
  • Capable of troubleshooting and solving problems with attention to detail.
  • Experience in data storytelling and presenting complex information clearly.

Desirable Skills & Experience
  • Exposure to the scholarly publishing industry is preferred, but not required.
  • Previous experience in data integrity management or data visualization will be an advantage.
  • A proactive approach to learning new skills and methodologies in data analytics.
