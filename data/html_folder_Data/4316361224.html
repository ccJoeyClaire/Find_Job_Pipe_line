POSITION

We are looking for a Language Data and Quality Reviewer with proficiency in Korean and English to join our team.

This role is critical in researching and analyzing data for various entities, tools, and datasets that will be introduced through on-the-job training. The ideal candidate should have a background in technology, with strong logical and analytical skills to assess data and provide valuable insights.

Key Responsibilities

  • Language Translations: Provide translations and contextual understanding where necessary to ensure data is aligned with language and cultural nuances.
  • Recommendations: Offer suggestions aimed at improving business processes and outcomes based on your data analysis.
