关于Meshy

Meshy 是一家创立于硅谷的生成式 AI 公司，始终以「解放 3D 创造力」为使命，致力于推动 3D 内容创作方式的根本变革。我们正全力构建业界领先的 3D 基础大模型，能够生成高精度几何结构与逼真材质贴图，让专业设计师与创作爱好者都能通过简单的文字或图片，在几分钟内轻松打造出高质量 3D 资产。过去耗时费力的建模流程，如今正被重塑为高效、便捷的创意体验。

Meshy 汇聚了来自计算机图形学、人工智能与 3D 艺术领域的全球顶尖人才。团队成员毕业于 MIT、斯坦福、伯克利等世界一流高校，并曾任职于英伟达、微软等高科技企业。公司业务遍及全球，在多地设有分部，形成了多元包容的文化氛围与高效协同的全球协作机制。Meshy 的技术实力与创新潜力也赢得了资本市场的认可，已获得包括红杉资本、GGV 纪源资本在内的顶级机构 5200 万美元融资。

在行业影响力方面，Meshy 已确立全球领先地位。我们被 A16Z Games 2024 年度报告评选为最受欢迎的 3D AI 工具，并在 SimilarWeb 网站流量统计中位列第一，月访问量突破 300 万。目前，Meshy 已服务全球 500 万用户，累计生成 4000 万以上 3D 模型，成为越来越多创作者与开发者的信赖之选。

创始人兼 CEO 胡渊鸣（Ethan Yuanming Hu） 拥有 MIT 计算机图形学与人工智能博士学位。他主导开发了广受认可的 Taichi 编程语言，该项目在 GitHub 上收获超过 2.7 万星标，已被全球 300 多家机构采纳使用。

About The Role

We are looking for a professional, proactive, and tech-passionate IT Engineer to join the Meshy team.

In this role, you will be responsible for maintaining the company’s core IT infrastructure that supports daily operations, including employee desktop support, asset management, network and system maintenance. You will play a key role in ensuring a smooth employee experience and stable technical environment, helping to build an efficient, secure, and scalable internal IT setup.

If you enjoy hands-on problem solving, optimizing processes, and want to make an impact in a fast-growing international team, this position is for you.

What You'll Do

  • Provide first-line IT support and resolve issues related to devices, systems, and software.
  • Manage and maintain internal IT assets, including hardware and software licenses.
  • Configure, optimize, and troubleshoot office networks and VPNs.
  • Assist with onboarding new employees by setting up IT environments (account creation, device allocation, network access, permission setup, etc.).
  • Conduct regular checks on IT systems and security policies to ensure device and data safety.
  • Maintain IT documentation and asset inventory to standardize IT operations.
  • Collaborate with various departments to continuously improve the internal IT experience.

What We're Looking For
  • 3+ years of experience in IT support, desktop maintenance, or system administration.
  • Familiarity with Windows, macOS, and Linux operating systems and common office applications.
  • Basic understanding of networking concepts (TCP/IP, VLAN, VPN, DNS, DHCP, firewall, etc.).
  • Experience with common networking and device brands (Cisco, H3C, Hillstone, etc.).
  • Familiarity with virtualization platforms (ESXi, Hyper-V, etc.).
  • Strong communication skills and service mindset, able to respond efficiently to internal user needs.
  • Detail-oriented, highly responsible, and eager to learn new technologies.

Nice To Have
  • Experience with IT asset management systems.
  • Experience managing enterprise services such as Google Workspace or Okta.
  • Experience with scripting or automation tools (Python, Bash, PowerShell, Ansible, etc.).
  • Ability to read technical documentation in English and communicate at a basic technical level.
  • Knowledge of information security standards and best practices.
