职位简介

At Meta Reality Labs, we are developing the future of Wearables and Mixed Reality products and this team focuses on developing display and optics technologies for Meta products. Their mission is to create see-through display systems that seamlessly blend the real and virtual realms. This involves designing the architecture and all display sub-systems for AR glasses based on leading technologies developed by Meta’s teams. We work on creating advanced display technologies that are essential for AR and VR systems while having sub-teams who connect bits, electrons, photons, and experience for display products across wearables and the metaverse while focusing on display silicon architecture, applied perception studies, image quality, and defining the roadmap and long-term platform development strategy. Within XDO we also aim to develop and implement test, characterization, metrology, and data solutions to support the creation of transparent display systems. We are seeking a highly accomplished Optical Process Engineer to join the newly-founded Display Engineering China team. As a key member of our China team, you will play a critical role in productizing display modules and systems that are central to delivering transformative experiences in AR products. Your expertise will be focused on tackling complex display integration and assembly challenges, developing optic module assembly process and tools, lamination process and tools, novel adhesive material development, and maturing these new processes from concept to ramp. You will bridge the gap between proof-of-concept to mass production, ensuring seamless integration into our product lines.

System Integration Process Lead Responsibilities:

  • Develop and implement new optical processes and equipment for optic modules and display systems, in particular, 6-axis optical assembly and active optical alignment processes
  • Adhesive selection, surface preparation, dispense and cure process development and qualification for integration processes and high-precision active alignments
  • Support and execute design reviews, tolerance analysis and FMEA reviews for process and equipment development
  • Drive equipment development from DFM reviews thru implementing rigorous equipment FAT/SAT validation plans
  • Develop and maintain process documentation, including requirement specifications (ERS), standard operating procedures (SOPs), measurement procedures (mSOP), calibration procedures, and work instructions
  • Lead and drive the execution of engineering builds on-site, identify build risks, drive Failure Analysis and corrective actions (FACA), and implement process, yield, reliability, and throughput improvements across multiple builds
  • Identify and implement UPH improvement and cost-saving opportunities through process and equipment optimization
  • Lead process discussions in factories and serve as the process interface between multiple functions (Optics, metrology, reliability engineering, Tech Ops, etc)
  • Efficiently collaborate with cross-functional teams, especially the teams in the USA on all aspects of product development

Minimum Qualifications:
  • Bachelors degree in Mechanical, Materials, Physics, Chemical or Optics Engineering or equivalent experience
  • 8+ years of experience in the development of assembly processes and equipments for display/optical imaging assemblies in consumer electronics devices
  • Experience in New Product Introduction (NPI) processes, including design for manufacturability, prototyping, and production ramp-up in consumer electronics industry
  • Experience with optical adhesive, structural adhesives, active alignment adhesives, related dispenser technologies and dispense and cure optimization
  • Experience developing fully automated precision 6-axis alignment processes and equipment to optimize optical assemblies
  • Experience setting up and implementing DOEs, data analysis, SPC control, Gauge R&R, root cause investigation, failure analysis, implement corrective action
  • Knowledge of statistical methods and experience in data analysis with tools such as MATLAB, JMP
  • Experience with common optical metrology instruments and measurement methods, such as interferometry, CMM, and OMM
  • Experience directing product and process development efforts at China contract manufacturers
  • Collaboration and Ambiguity: Proven experience to handle ambiguity and collaborate effectively with large cross-functional teams and multilayer supply chains
  • Proven experience working across teams, including internal cross-functional engagements and external domestic/international vendor engagements

Preferred Qualifications:
  • 6-sigma experience (Black Belt)
  • Experience in 3D modeling/2D drafting using NX, ProE, or SolidWorks
  • Knowledge of display quality metrics, basic optical aberrations, and test equipment used to measure optical performance
  • Familiarity with minimizing equipment vibration thru simulation, measurement, analysis and control
  • Experience developing active alignment equipment for light engines, camera modules, or similar
  • Experienced in developing processes and equipment for AR/VR applications
  • Experience with precision optical assembly such as glass bonding, collimator lens assembly, and film lamination
  • Track-record of delivering high volume products to the market
  • Skilled in evaluating and developing new technology capabilities at external vendors
