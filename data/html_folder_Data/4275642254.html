Job Description

  • Perform direct outreach to technical BI developers and citizen data scientists in the embedded BI teams, providing them with the technology and support needed to accelerate their Data Science and Machine Learning maturity.
  • Need to come to the job with strong soft skills and the ability to distill complex data science concepts and outcomes into a format consumable by non-technical business heads.

Qualification
  • Pursuing in bachelor’s degree or above in Statistics, Information Technology or related fields of study.
  • Demonstrated knowledge and experience in applying inferential and predictive statistical methods required.
  • Proficiency in spoken and written English required.
