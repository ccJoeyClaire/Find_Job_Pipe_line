职位简介

About The Role

This role requires strong technical understanding to lead technical progress, drive cross-functional decision making and communicate project technical status and risk to senior management; whilst interacting with key stakeholders in the engineering, operations and quality teams as well as key suppliers.

  • Lead communication with all stakeholders acting as a first technical point of contact for the project.
  • Communicate between teams within the research and development organisations to ensure effective and timely input; resolve conflicts and lead technical solutions.
  • Alongside the project manager, organise regular progress meetings with all stakeholders (Engineering, Category, Operations, and Service teams). Including chairing PLT sessions, technical reviews and representing engineering in RDD phase reviews and milestones.
  • Partner with the Project Manager to define level 0-2 plans and ensuring their delivery through prioritisation and escalation of unresolved conflicts.
  • Drive and follow-up the execution of the development plans in conjunction with the project manager, resolving or escalates problems timely.
  • Engage third parties for engineering and procurement activities, requiring excellent communication skills and regular travel.
  • Set project technical direction and define the project team setup in conjunction with the project manager and engineering/technical leads detailing the makeup of the team to achieve the project.
  • Break down major requirements in to smaller cross-functional features and prioritise with stakeholders.


About You
  • Degree in a relevant Engineering discipline or equivalent.
  • Demonstrated experience in an engineering and manufacturing industry designing and delivering products through a cross functional team network.
  • Proven experience and successful track record of delivering robust design solutions on time, to specification and within cost and budget constraints.
  • Full design cycle experience - has played a key role in projects from early concept to start of production, global experience of site-to-site project transfers.
  • Significant experience of interacting and building relationships with senior stakeholders.
  • Knowledge across a variety of technical areas and expert leadership to ensure the products are delivered on time, to specification, cost and quality.
  • Ability to see the "bigger picture" of the technology and platform as a whole, whilst also being able to go into the details when required to do so.
  • Ability and credibility to motivate teams, strong influencing skills and leading with and without formal power and line authority.
  • Capable of multitasking, prioritising your work and the work of others.
  • Knowledge and proven experience in change management processes implementation.
  • Excel at working with multiple stakeholders in a global organisation whilst keeping technology highly confidential.
  • Experience and capability for engaging with third parties for engineering and procurement activities, with fluency in RFx processes.
  • Technical and personal credibility to work effectively in cross-functional design teams throughout the project development cycle.
  • Strong conflict resolution skills (assertiveness, active listening, empathy).
  • Excellent presentation, networking and communication skills. Comfortable and used to present to senior leaders within the business.
  • An agile learner, understanding problems quickly whilst being able to identify experts to help with the details.
  • Flexibility: ability to cope with the introduction of new products, modification of existing products, change of delivery schedules, varying demand volumes, changes in the product and resources mix.

Dyson solves real-world problems and creates better products through the application of engineering, science, design and creativity. It is a family-owned, global technology company, founded by Sir James Dyson who remains at the helm alongside his son Jake.

Since inventing the first cyclonic bagless vacuum cleaner, the DC01, Dyson has consistently invested in research and development to improve its products and technologies radically. Dyson offers products across a growing range of areas: floorcare, air purification, robotics, haircare including formulations, lighting, hand drying, and most recently audio. Dyson continues to expand into new areas.

Today, Dyson sells products in more than 80 markets, has 450 Dyson stores worldwide and is available in all major technology and beauty retailers. Dyson has global headquarters in Singapore and major technology campuses in Singapore, the UK, Malaysia, and the Philippines. Its global team of engineers, scientists and software developers are focused on developing technology-enabled products which work better and which people love to use. Key areas of focus have included high-speed electric digital motors, sensing and vision systems, robotics, machine learning and aerodynamics.

Beyond products, to encourage an inventive future, Dyson is also inspiring the next generation of engineers and inventors through the Dyson Institute of Engineering and Technology, the James Dyson Foundation and the James Dyson Award.

The Dyson family applies its problem-solving approach in other fields, and established Dyson Farming in 2012. It is one of the largest farming businesses in the UK, extending to 36,000 acres across Lincolnshire, Oxfordshire, Gloucestershire and Somerset.
