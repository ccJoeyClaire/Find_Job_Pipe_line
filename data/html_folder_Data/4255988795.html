职位简介

Job Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for hosting international conferences and events, completing hosting-related work, including but not limited to pre-meeting planning, conference processes, and writing conference host scripts.
  • Participate in the full process of event planning, proposal, and execution, launch event solutions and execution plans that are in line with the tone, monitor and analyze the effectiveness of the event, and propose improvement suggestions.
  • Handle internal collaboration communication, cooperate with other departments to improve collaboration efficiency.


Requirements:
  • Fluent in both Chinese and English, able to host bilingual events. Broadcasting major is preferred.
  • Have experience in event planning and hosting and have field control ability.
  • Those with planning and project experience are preferred.



工作职责

1. 负责国际会议、活动相关的主持，完成主持相关工作包括: 会前策划、会议流程、会议主持稿；
  • 参与活动策划、提案及执行的全流程，推出符合调性的活动方案与执行方案，对活动效果进行监测和分析，并提出改进意见；
  • 处理内部协作沟通，与其他部门（如IBU、海外业务线）合作，提升协作效率。


要求：
  • 中文流利，可双语主持。播音专业优先；
  • 有活动策划和经验及控场能力；
  • 有策划及项目经验者优先。
