【关于我们】

滴滴自2018年初收购巴西出行平台99开始，正式启动了国际化战略。目前，滴滴的国际业务覆盖了拉美、亚太和非洲的14个国家，为当地市场提供以出行为主、涵盖外卖和金融的多样化服务。


Join our dynamic Marketplace Team in China where you'll leverage data to uncover insights that shape our marketplace strategies and enhance our customer experience. If you're passionate about transforming data into actionable solutions and want to contribute to a purpose-driven organization, we want to hear from you! 【About the team】

我们是滴滴国际化交易市场诊断团队。国际化出行业务作为滴滴最重要的增长方向之一，我们深入其最核心的交易市场方向，运用机器学习、算法、因果推断等量化模型，解决各个区域的资源分配&收益管理问题，闭环产出方案、落地策略。团队包容、年轻、多元。团队有着完善的培养机制、足够的空间等着你来大展拳脚！


Job Responsibility,

• Conduct diagnostic research in macro supply & demand and user persona to achieve business goals

• Develop & align core decision-making frameworks across multi-functional teams, and propose feasible strategies

• Facilitate quantitative analysis to support regional operation

• Present analytical results to the leadership team

• Collaborate with other teams to implement & evaluate tests in order to understand different strategy use-cases and refine framework by scenarios


Qualification,

• Master's degree (highly preferred) in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering or similar disciplines

• 1 - 5 year's experiences working in data science or business analytics related field

• GPA - 3.5 or higher is preferred

• Strong proficiency in query language (e.g. SQL) and programming skills (e.g. Python, R)

• Knowledge in Machine Learning, Causal Inference and Optimization Methodology is a plus

• Strong presentation skills in Mandarin and English
