Your Contribution

As a System Engineer, you will be responsible for the development of Power electronics and auxiliary electronics products.
  • Analysis and Definition of requirements (requirements engineering).
  • Set up of functional and physical architecture and Model-based Systems Engineering (SysML)
  • Interface with hardware/software/mechanics team
  • Support System Validation, System Integration and System Integration Test
  • Coordination of the implementation of the system functions and features
  • Maturity assessment
  • Lead the VAVE/iPCO on technical side
  • Support of FMEA activities
  • Functional and Technical Safety activities

Your Experience & Qualifications
  • Bachelor degree or above, major in Control Engineering / Computer Science /Software engineering /Automotive engineering / Motor controlling / Electrical Engineering/Mechatronics.
  • Automotive Power electronics experience of minimum 3 years.
  • Product Know how – E-Compressor , E-Pump.
  • Understanding of the complete system development.
  • Lead the various competencies in parallel.
