职位简介

About the position

As a Database Architect, this role is responsibility of architecting, designing secured databases for Microservices, API's and other state-of-the-art applications too. It is also responsible for high performance and secured migrations from Trustees to the on-perm platform adhering to strict governance processes. Need to think beyond databases about how we can enable engineers to build high performance and multi-hundred-terabyte databases.


The Job
  • Architect and implement scalable, high-availability infrastructure on on-perm Cloud using services like Oracle, MS SQL and Redis
  • Architect, design and execute homogeneous and heterogenous database migrations from Trustees to the centralized database platform
  • Design and implement a Disaster Recovery Solution and a Near Zero Downtime maintenance architecture to minimize business interruptions
  • Lead the design and execution of eMPFA enterprise data architecture strategy across a complex ecosystem
  • Architect scalable and secure data solutions that meet evolving regulatory requirements
  • Create reusable, scheme-oriented data models that promote consistency, interoperability, and self-service across business units
  • Define and implement architectural patterns supporting both real-time and batch data processing by scheme and cross schemes
  • Implement monitoring solutions using Oracle OEM and AppDynamics and SiteScope, and other tools to ensure the performance, availability, and reliability of applications
  • Apply security best practices to safeguard the database platform and ensure compliance with relevant regulations and standards
  • Diagnose and resolve issues related to infrastructure, deployments, and application performance
  • Work closely with development teams to integrate Platform practices and support continuous improvement efforts
  • Identify performance problems and work with various cross functional teams to resolve them efficiently
  • Participate in agile sprints including story writing, planning, daily standups, backlog grooming sessions, reviews, and post-mortem
  • Understand API/microservice architecture and can participate in architecture discussions of applications and suggest the right database
  • Develop and maintain CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins to automate deployment processes



The Person
  • Bachelor's Degree with minimum 10+ years of experience in Technology or in lieu of degree, minimum 10 years of experience in Technology
  • Minimum 10 years of strong Technical Expertise in one of the RDBMS: Oracle, MySQL, SQL Server
  • Proficient with monitoring and logging tools such as Oracle OEM, AppDynamics, Grafana, and SiteScope
  • Familiar with Imperva database fire wall tool and CSI Database Security standard
  • Ability to quickly learn innovative technologies and frameworks
  • Ability to analyze, use structured problem solving and available tools to troubleshoot systems, identify root cause, action plans, impact, and resolution options
