Description & Requirements

Electronic Arts creates next-level entertainment experiences that inspire players and fans around the world. Here, everyone is part of the story. Part of a community that connects across the globe. A place where creativity thrives, new perspectives are invited, and ideas matter. A team where everyone makes play happen.

EA SPORTS FC Mobile Shanghai is a global team dedicated to developing and operating a high-quality mobile football game experience. Creativity, autonomy, and collaboration lie at the heart of our team culture, empowering us to deliver world-class games and experiences to players around the globe. We are passionate, and open to new possibilities. We value team cooperation and believe that a relaxed and supportive work environment leads to better results.

The Senior Data Analyst will report to the Sr. Manager of Data & Analytics team.

Responsibilities

  • Design telemetry logs to track in-game behavior per game design and partner requests.
  • Conduct deep dives and modeling techniques to understand player segments, behaviors, and spending to enhance monetization.
  • Evaluate game events, content updates, and marketing campaigns; provide actionable insights.
  • Analyze daily performance data to identify opportunities and develop strategies.
  • Create and maintain dashboards, reports, and ad-hoc queries.
  • Communicate analysis results and recommendations to partners effectively.

Qualifications

Technical Skills:
  • 5 or more years of experience in SQL, R or Python for data analysis.
  • Strong data analysis skills; experience in modeling, A/B/multivariate testing, machine learning, and statistics
  • Familiarity with distributed data stores (Hadoop, Redshift, etc.).

Soft Skills
  • Strong collaboration across teams and regions.
  • Gaming enthusiast, especially mobile games.

Other
  • Football fandom is a plus
  • Experience as an analyst in Agile environments.
