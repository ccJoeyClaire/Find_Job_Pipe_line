职位简介

At Principal, we invest in what matters. And building talented teams is where it all begins. We’re drawn to people who bring unique perspectives, passion, and expertise to help us advance the financial security and well-being of our customers, transform our growing business, and drive positive change in the communities where we live and work.


When we invest in you, and you invest in us, great things happen.


Join Principal Financial Group as Intern to gain or real-world investment management exposure and career readiness.


What You'll Do
  • Provide timely, courteous, and excellent service to all customers, ensuring operations are conducted ethically and at an acceptable cost.
  • Maintain a high degree of integrity while actively cooperating and interacting with all entities within the group.
  • Support all sales and customer service activities, including due diligence material preparation, investment research data collection, and production of periodic market/fund manager comment reports.
  • Collect market intelligence, including product comparisons and competitor information.
  • Assist the administration manager with office and logistics management activities.


Who You Are
  • Postgraduate students majoring in Finance, Business, or related fields.
  • Good understanding of financial market principles and terminology, investment management processes, and the nature of the investment industry.
  • Ability to prioritize and manage multiple concurrent tasks.
  • Strong communication skills in English and Chinese.
  • Strong analytical skills and proficient in PC skills.


Why Join Us
  • When you invest in us, and we invest in you, outstanding things happen. You'll be working in a dynamic and collaborative environment where your exceptional perspectives and passion will be valued. At Principal, we believe in nurturing young talent and providing opportunities for growth and development. Join us and be part of a team that is ambitious, driven, and committed to success!
