Description:

Ericsson China R&D RAN SW is looking for Machine Learning Engineer on Ericsson Machine Learning 5G team.

What You Will Be Doing

As part of the team, you will be enrolled into this most state of art wireless technology development with machine learning empowered 5G from prototype design to commercial. You as a Machine Learning Engineer will work together with the most talented colleagues from all over the world. Collaborate closely with the architecture, research, libraries, tools, and system software teams at Ericsson to influence the design of next-generation architectures, software platforms, and AI models.

Requirements:

  • MS.c in AI, CS, Wireless Communication, EE or related field.
  • Experience with Deep Learning frameworks like TensorFlow or Pytorch.
  • Experience with languages like Python and MATLAB.
  • Experience with parallel programming, ideally CUDA C/C++.
  • Experience with latest Gen AI technologies, LLM, RAG etc.
  • Linux software development experience.
  • Fluent English in oral and written.

Standout from Crowd:
  • Paper published on AAAI/IEEE or other organization with influence.
  • Knowledge of Wireless Communication.
  • Strong programming skills in C and C++.
  • Experience with natural/formatted language processing.
  • Experience deploying features in a container.
  • Experience with Continuous Integration and Deployment.
  • Able to think creatively and solve complex problems.
