Salary: Competitive and based on experience

Keywords: global management experience, commercial background, international expansion, high-growth sectors, publicly-listed enterprise, strategic leadership, collaborative culture, stakeholder engagement, inclusive workplace, supportive leadership

A leading global enterprise is seeking a visionary Chief Executive Officer to guide the organisation through its next phase of international growth and market leadership. This is an exceptional opportunity for you to shape the future of a company that stands at the forefront of advanced solutions across consumer electronics, telecommunications, automotive, industrial applications, and medical technology. As CEO, you will be entrusted with defining and executing a bold corporate strategy, driving profitable expansion in key markets including North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and China. The role offers significant scope for influence and impact, reporting directly to the Board of Directors and working closely with a talented executive team. You will benefit from a highly competitive compensation package, performance-based incentives, and comprehensive benefits designed to reward your commitment and expertise. The organisation is committed to fostering a collaborative and inclusive culture where your interpersonal skills and strategic vision will be valued and supported.
  • Lead a publicly-listed enterprise with a market capitalisation of approximately RMB 20 billion, operating across diverse high-growth sectors such as consumer electronics, telecommunications, automotive, industrial applications, and medical technology.
  • Shape and execute global strategies for expansion into North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and China while building a high-performance culture rooted in collaboration, accountability, and innovation.
  • Enjoy a comprehensive executive compensation package including performance-based annual bonuses tied to strategic KPIs, long-term incentive plans such as stock options, and extensive benefits supporting your professional journey.

What you'll do:

As Chief Executive Officer you will play an instrumental role in steering the organisation towards ambitious goals in international markets. Your daily activities will centre around shaping corporate strategy for expansion into North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and China while maintaining oversight of global operations. You will collaborate closely with an experienced executive team to ensure alignment with long-term objectives. Success in this position requires you to balance commercial acumen with empathetic stakeholder engagement—building trust among board members, investors, employees, and external partners alike. You will be responsible for identifying new opportunities for growth through mergers or strategic partnerships while championing operational excellence throughout every division. Your ability to foster an inclusive culture that encourages collaboration will be essential in driving both financial performance and employee satisfaction.
  • Develop and implement the organisation’s long-term strategic plan to accelerate growth across all target markets including the Americas, EMEA, APAC regions.
  • Identify new market opportunities and lead market penetration strategies that drive sustainable revenue growth and expand the company’s global footprint.
  • Hold full P&L accountability for the worldwide business operations by driving top-line revenue growth and bottom-line profitability through commercial initiatives.
  • Build, mentor, and lead a high-performing C-level executive team across multiple geographies while nurturing a culture of excellence, accountability, collaboration, and innovation.
  • Serve as the primary liaison to the Board of Directors, investors, shareholders, and external partners by communicating strategy, performance metrics, and future prospects effectively.
  • Oversee day-to-day operations on a global scale ensuring organisational efficiency aligns with strategic objectives and delivers on commitments to customers worldwide.
  • Evaluate potential mergers, acquisitions, and strategic partnerships to enhance capabilities, product portfolio diversity, and geographic reach.
  • Champion operational efficiency improvements by streamlining processes across international divisions for optimal results.
  • Foster an inclusive environment that values diverse perspectives from employees across cultures and regions.
  • Represent the organisation externally at industry events and forums to strengthen brand reputation among stakeholders.

What you bring:

To excel as Chief Executive Officer you will bring deep expertise in executive leadership gained over many years guiding complex organisations through periods of transformation. Your proven success managing large-scale businesses internationally—particularly across North America Europe Southeast Asia—will enable you to navigate cultural nuances while driving commercial outcomes. A robust academic foundation complemented by advanced degrees equips you with analytical skills necessary for strategic planning. Your interpersonal strengths allow you to connect meaningfully with colleagues at all levels fostering loyalty respect shared purpose. Experience in mergers acquisitions further enhances your ability to identify opportunities for growth while maintaining operational discipline. Above all your commitment to inclusivity ensures every voice is heard contributing positively toward organisational success.
  • At least 15 years of progressive executive leadership experience with five or more years as CEO or President overseeing large-scale businesses with revenues exceeding 200M.
  • Mandatory strong commercial background demonstrated by successful revenue growth initiatives and market share expansion on a global stage.
  • Extensive global management experience managing businesses across North America, Europe, Southeast Asia; experience in the Chinese market is highly desirable.
  • Proven track record within large matrixed organisations preferably in publicly listed environments where complex stakeholder relationships are managed effectively.
  • Bachelor’s degree required; MBA or other relevant advanced degree strongly preferred for strategic decision-making capabilities.
  • Exceptional communication skills enabling effective engagement with diverse cultures as well as board members investors employees alike.
  • Outstanding interpersonal abilities that foster trust empathy understanding among teams stakeholders globally.
  • Experience evaluating leading mergers acquisitions strategic partnerships enhancing product portfolios geographic reach.
  • Ability to build nurture high-performing executive teams promoting collaborative accountable innovative work environments.
  • Demonstrated commitment to inclusive practices valuing diversity equity belonging within multinational settings.

What sets this company apart:

This organisation stands out as a publicly-listed leader renowned for delivering advanced solutions across multiple high-growth industries including consumer electronics telecommunications automotive industrial applications medical technology. With a market capitalisation approaching RMB 20 billion it has established itself as a trusted partner in key international markets such as North America Europe Southeast Asia China. The company’s dedication to continuous expansion is matched by its supportive leadership style which prioritises collaboration knowledge-sharing professional development among employees worldwide. By joining this enterprise you become part of an environment where your contributions are recognised rewarded through generous incentives comprehensive benefits packages tailored for executives who value both achievement personal wellbeing. The company’s commitment to inclusivity ensures that individuals from all backgrounds feel welcomed empowered making it an ideal setting for those seeking meaningful impact on a global scale.
