About This Opportunity

You will apply cutting-edge machine learning techniques to real-world problems in wireless communication and intelligent networks. This internship offers practical experience in model development, experimentation, and deployment within a collaborative engineering team.

What You Will Do

  • Design, develop, and optimize AI/ML models for communication and network applications.
  • Collect, preprocess, and analyze datasets for model training and evaluation.
  • Conduct experiments, validate model performance, and visualize results.
  • Implement and test algorithms in areas such as deep learning, reinforcement learning, and data-driven optimization.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate AI solutions into existing systems.
  • Document progress and contribute to internal knowledge sharing.
  • Continuously improve model performance and development efficiency.

The Skills You Bring
  • Master’s degree (or above) in Computer Science, Data Science, Electronic Engineering, or a related field.
  • Knowledge of heterogeneous computing, FPGA design, or hardware acceleration techniques.
  • Proficiency in Python and ML libraries such as NumPy, pandas, and scikit-learn.
  • Experience with deep learning frameworks (e.g., PyTorch, TensorFlow).
  • Strong grasp of ML principles: data preprocessing, model evaluation, hyperparameter tuning.
  • Solid foundation in statistics, linear algebra, and probability.
  • Familiarity with version control tools (e.g., Git) and clean coding practices.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and teamwork skills.
  • Clear communication and documentation abilities.
