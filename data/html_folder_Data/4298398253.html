About This Opportunity

Join our innovative team at Ericsson as a Software Developer (AI focus). In this role, you will contribute to building customer solutions throughout the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), from requirement analysis and coding to verification and optimization. You will also apply AI and data-driven technologies to enhance network platforms and solve complex challenges.

This is an excellent opportunity for high-achieving students or graduates with a strong academic background in Computer Science, Data Science, or related fields, who are eager to deepen their expertise and make an impact in the telecom industry.

What You Will Do

  • Develop and maintain customer solutions across the full lifecycle: requirement analysis, solution design, coding, scripting, integration, verification, and support.
  • Apply the latest AI and data science technologies to optimize network platforms and improve performance.
  • Support the construction, testing, and continuous improvement of network and AI-based solutions.
  • Solve technical challenges in system and platform development.
  • Share knowledge and collaborate with colleagues to strengthen team capabilities.

What You Will Bring
  • Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, or related disciplines.
  • Solid academic foundation with excellent academic performance in core technical courses.
  • Hands-on experience in software/automation development (Java, Python, or similar).
  • Strong English communication skills (listening, speaking, reading, and writing).
  • Knowledge and skills in:

Statistical analysis, probability theory, design of experiments, and machine learning.

Data preparation and pre-processing.

Relational Database Management on Unix/Linux platforms.

IP technologies.

AI technologies.
