Claims Operations Specialist

达信 · 南京地区 (混合办公)

职位简介

We are seeking a talented individual to join our Claims Operations team. This role will be based in Tianjin. This is a hybrid role that has a requirement of working at least three days a week in the office.

We will count on you to:

  • First notice of loss, insurer notification and client acknowledgment
  • Independently follow up routine claims for clients
  • Collaboration with insurers, loss adjusters and other related parties
  • Data analysis and regular claims reports
  • On-site claims meetings and trainings with clients
  • Close in touch with business teams

What you need to have:

  • Bachelor degree
  • Business English
  • Experience in Property All Risks/Cargo/Employer’s Liability/Public Liability/Product Liability product lines
  • Some of Property and Casualty claims experience
  • Demonstrate taking responsibility, being patient as needed and being customer oriented
  • Good understanding of the business context
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite tools

Why join our team:
  • We help you be your best through professional development opportunities, interesting work and supportive leaders.
  • We foster a vibrant and inclusive culture where you can work with talented colleagues to create new solutions and have impact for colleagues, clients and communities.
  • Our scale enables us to provide a range of career opportunities, as well as benefits and rewards to enhance your well-being.
