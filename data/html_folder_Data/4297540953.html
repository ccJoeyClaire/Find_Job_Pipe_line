The Position

Job Responsibilities

  • Strategic Opportunity Identification: Discover, evaluate, and monitor application scenarios for new digital technologies, specifically AI and advanced analytics, that align with the specific business needs of a pharmaceutical company's commercial operations
  • Project Leadership & Implementation: Participate in project planning related to these initiatives and assist in developing detailed implementation strategies to integrate AI and advanced analytics solutions into commercial processes. Serve as a key technical member of project teams tasked with developing and deploying these solutions, ensuring projects advance according to plan
  • Model Design & Development: Design, develop, and implement statistical models, machine learning algorithms, and AI techniques relevant to complex commercial data analysis (e.g., sales data, prescription data, market research data, patient data, CRM data)
  • Cross-functional Collaboration: Collaborate closely with internal cross-functional teams (e.g., Sales, Marketing, Market Access, IT) and external partners to understand business questions and ensure project deliverables are highly aligned with commercial requirements
  • Data Exploration & Evaluation: Perform exploratory data analysis to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in commercial data. Evaluate the performance of models and algorithms, iterating to improve their accuracy and robustness
  • Continuous Innovation: Continuously monitor advancements and iterations of new technologies to identify opportunities that can bring new value to the company, and translate these opportunities into actionable, implementable projects to support business objectives
  • Capability Building & Best Practices: Promote the application of AI and advanced analytics techniques within the organization. Assist in training sessions and knowledge-sharing activities to enhance the overall technical proficiency of the team, and contribute to the development of best practices for data analysis and interpretation
  • Insight Visualization & Communication: Visualize and communicate complex data insights and findings through reports, presentations, and interactive dashboards tailored for commercial stakeholders
  • Documentation & Team Contribution: Document methodologies, code, and results clearly and comprehensively. Participate in cross-functional team meetings and contribute to project planning and execution


Requirements
  • Education: Master's or Ph.D. in Data Science, Statistics, Business Analytics, Computer Science, or a related quantitative field
  • Passion for Pharma & Data: Genuine interest and demonstrated experience applying data science, AI, and advanced analytics to enhance pharmaceutical commercial operations
  • Technical Expertise (AI & Advanced Analytics):
  • Proven experience (typically 10 years for Senior Data Scientist) applying data science, AI, and advanced analytics to real-world business problems, ideally within pharma commercial or related projects
  • Proficient in utilizing GenAI techniques such as large language models (LLMs) for data analysis and interpretation
  • Experience in applying Generative AI to develop innovative solutions for complex commercial business problems in the pharmaceutical industry
  • Demonstrated ability to translate business needs into data science problems and deliver actionable insights that drive business value
  • Strong proficiency in programming languages (e.g., Python, R) and relevant data science libraries
  • Solid understanding of statistical inference and diverse machine learning algorithms (e.g., regression, classification, clustering, time series, NLP, deep learning) applicable to commercial data
  • Experience with large commercial datasets (e.g., sales, Rx, CRM) and cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) is a plus
  • Excellent data visualization skills
  • Pharmaceutical Commercial Background:
  • Deep understanding of pharmaceutical commercial operations (e.g., sales, marketing, market access, forecasting, CRM)
  • Direct experience within a pharmaceutical company's commercial functions or significant project contributions to this area. Relevant experience in consulting is a plus
  • Familiarity with pharma commercial data sources and privacy considerations
  • Problem-Solving & Collaboration:
  • Strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills, with the ability to independently address complex business questions
  • Experience contributing to or leading AI/Advanced Analytics projects
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, effective in presenting insights to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
  • Ability to thrive in collaborative, interdisciplinary, and cross-functional team environments

公司简介

罗氏公司

罗氏是全球制药和诊断领域的领导者，致力于通过推动科学进步，改善人类生活。结合了制药和诊断两大业务的独特优势，使罗氏集团成为个体化医疗的领导者，通过个体化医疗为每一位患者提供最具针对性的治疗方案。

作为全球领先的生物技术公司之一，罗氏在抗肿瘤、免疫、抗感染、眼科和中枢神经系统领域拥有一流的差异化药物。罗氏在全球体外诊断和基于组织的肿瘤诊断领域享有领导地位，同时也是糖尿病管理领域的先驱者。

自1896年成立以来，罗氏始终致力于不断探索更好的疾病预防、诊断和治疗方案，持续为社会做出贡献。同时，公司不遗余力地与有关的利益相关方开展合作，提升患者对于创新药物的可及性。

在世界卫生组织基本药物目录中，有32个罗氏开发的药品，包括用以挽救生命的抗生素、抗疟药和抗肿瘤药物。罗氏连续11年位列道琼斯指数（DJSI）制药、生物技术和生命科学领域可持续发展的全球领导者。

罗氏总部位于瑞士巴塞尔，业务遍及全球150多个国家。2019年，罗氏全球拥有员工97,735名，研发投资120亿美元，总销售额达629亿美元。美国基因泰克公司（Genentech）是罗氏集团的全资子公司。此外，罗氏也是日本中外制药株式会社（Chugai）的控股方。
