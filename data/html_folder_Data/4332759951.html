职位简介

Some careers have more impact than others.

If you’re looking for a career where you can make a real impression, join HSBC and discover how valued you’ll be.

HSBC is one of the largest banking and financial services organisations in the world, with operations in 64 countries and territories. We aim to be where the growth is, enabling businesses to thrive and economies to prosper, and, ultimately, helping people to fulfil their hopes and realise their ambitions.

We are currently seeking an experienced professional to join our team in the role of AVP - DACRM.

Principal responsibilities

  • Design, formulate, and implement relevant analytics deliverables for operational process of the bank and MI/BI with business insight, and convert analytical findings/solutions that will lead to higher productivity of banking process.
  • Keen to take initiatives to translate business problems into analytics-led solutions with appropriate methodology, manage the business application effectively and evaluate the results rigorously.
  • Highly sensitive to state of business and anticipative of business problems and priorities.
  • Ensure timely and quality analytics deliverables to satisfy business needs effectively under the dynamic business environment.
  • Take the ownership of driving the discussion with business users, other tech leads and architects.
  • Design and develop client-side and backend of analytics platform.
  • Sharing knowledge and good practice, working collaboratively with different parties to create a productive, diverse and supportive working environment.
  • Ability to work under pressure, handle multiple tasks and work to tight deadlines.

Requirements
  • University degree in Statistics, Data Science, Mathematics, Computer Science, Information System or other related discipline.
  • 5 years or above working experience in analytics, modelling in financial industry
  • Ability to communicate with impact, with the capacity to ensure complex information is articulated in a meaningful way to a wide and varied audience
  • Extensive experience and skill in mining massive volume of data for quick insight discovery
  • Great sense of ownership and servicing mind-set to ensure efficient and effective customer service delivery
  • Proven experience in full stack development using Python and Node.js, with strong skills in SQL/BigQuery and cloud platforms (GCP, AWS, Azure).
  • Hands-on experience with modern web frameworks (React, Angular, Vue.js) and backend frameworks (Express, FastAPI, Django).
  • Ability to design, develop, and deploy end-to-end data-driven applications, including APIs, databases, and user interfaces.
  • Experience with data mining, analytics, and visualization tools (Tableau, QlikSense, Looker Studio).
  • Familiarity with DevOps practices, CI/CD pipelines, and cloud infrastructure.

Candidate with less relevant experience or skills may be offered a lower Global Career Band than stated above.

Due to the urgent hiring need, candidates with immediate right to work locally and no relocation need will be prioritised.
