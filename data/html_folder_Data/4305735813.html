About Tencent

Tencent is an Internet-based platform company founded in Shenzhen, China, in 1998. We use technology to enrich the lives of Internet users and assist the digital upgrade of enterprises. Our mission is "Value for Users, Tech for Good". We embrace a culture of teamwork & creativity and are driven by our values - Integrity, Proactivity, Collaboration and Creativity.


We are rapidly expanding our international operations and are looking for top talent to propel us forward. Combining the results-oriented nature of a start-up with the resources of a profitable and leading Internet company, Tencent offers a unique opportunity for aspiring individuals to thrive.


About IEG Global

Tencent Games was established in 2003. We are a leading global platform for game development, operations and publishing, and the largest online game community in China.


Tencent Games has developed and operated over 140 games. We provide cross-platform interactive entertainment experience for more than 800 million users in over 200 countries and regions around the world. Honor of Kings, PUBG MOBILE, and League of Legends, are some of our most popular titles around the world.


Meanwhile, we actively promote the development of esports industry, work with global partners to build an open, collaborative and symbiotic industrial ecology, and create high-quality digital life experiences for players.


Position

Level Infinite – Overseas Game Market & User Research Manager


Responsibilities
  • Work closely with internal teams (publishing, R&D, marketing, branding, etc.) to fully understand business needs, and design actionable research solutions tailored to those needs. Provide insights and recommendations to support product polishing, iteration, and publishing strategies.
  • Conduct comprehensive research on key products (both qualitative and quantitative). Independently manage research project workflows and execution, efficiently coordinate internal and external resources, and deliver highly actionable research reports in both Chinese and English.
  • Continuously monitor the gaming industry across PC, console, and mobile platforms. Maintain strong market sensitivity and insight, proactively identifying research topics with cross-business value, and provide forward-looking inputs for the business.
  • Think proactively and critically to diagnose core issues faced by products at different development stages. Drive collaboration on high-value research projects to help the business clarify target markets and better understand user needs early on, supporting effective product positioning and direction.


Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree or above, with 5+ years of experience in market research, user research, or related roles. Experience in the gaming industry with exposure to multiple game genres is a plus.
  • Strong English communication skills, able to work effectively in a fully international environment.
  • Solid experience with both qualitative and quantitative research, as well as data analysis.
  • Proficiency in handling complex statistical analysis, with strong command of tools such as SPSS, Q, or similar.
  • Strong learning ability, communication skills, and self-motivation. Capable of working independently while collaborating effectively with teams.
  • Open-minded, innovative, able to multitask, and comfortable taking on challenges.
  • Passionate about games; being an active player across different platforms and genres is preferred.


岗位

Level Infinite 海外游戏市场与用户研究经理


岗位职责
  • 与公司内各部门（发行、研发、市场、品牌等）紧密合作，在日常沟通中充分理解业务需求，并且能针对性地设计可落地的研究解决方案，为研发打磨、产品迭代及发行策略提供洞察和建议
  • 对重点产品展开全面的研究支持工作（定性和定量），能独立负责各类研究项目的流程管理和具体执行，且能高效协调内外部资源，并输出具有高度可落地性的中英文专业研究报告
  • 持续关注三端游戏行业，对游戏市场保持敏锐度和洞察力，能主动识别和提议对多方业务有共性价值的研究课题，为业务提供前瞻性输入
  • 积极主动地进行思考，深入诊断业务产品在各个发展阶段所存在的核心问题。大力推动关键价值研究课题的合作事宜，助力业务更加前置化、节奏化地理清产品的目标市场以及深入理解用户需求，协助对产品的定位和方向进行有效把控


岗位要求
  • 本科及以上学历，5 年以上市场研究或用户研究等相关岗位的工作经验，有游戏行业工作经验且有多品类游戏研究经验者优先；
  • 具备出色的英语口语能力，能在国际化职场环境中使用全英文进行工作 具备丰富的定性、定量以及数据分析经验；
  • 能够熟练处理复杂的统计学数据分析工作，并且熟练掌握SPSS, Q等统计软件的使用；
  • 具备较强的学习力、沟通力和自我驱动力，能够独立执行并高效协作 思维开放、创新，能够同时处理多任务，乐于迎接挑战 对游戏充满热情，不同平台与品类多栖玩家优先


This role is based in Shenzhen, Guangdong, China.
