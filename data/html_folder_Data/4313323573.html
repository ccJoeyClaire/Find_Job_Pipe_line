Overview

  • PMO supporting management reporting
  • Support internal and external management Audits
  • Consolidate and respond to audit requests
  • Operational Support on Quality Assurance
  • Prepare and consolidate Data Centers capacity management reports
  • Assist in preparing of BAU/ Project budgets
Job Responsibilities and Duties
  • PMO support to DC Ops Managers on management reporting
  • Consolidate KPIs and other statistics for Asia and global reporting
  • Review documentation and standards and consolidate comments from different teams and individuals for reporting back to requestor in a timely manner
  • Ability to chase and follow-up people on assignment
  • Assist EDC ops Managers in preparing budget for BAU Operation and Projects
  • Prepare management reports such as TFR / READ approval and supporting documents
  • Organise meetings, take meeting minutes and follow up with responsible person for follow-up actions
  • Perform quality assurance on data and reports
  • Work closely with Risk management team on regular internal and external audits
  • Document auditable evidence to the different teams that the duties within this document have been carried out correctly.
  • Inform the EDC Ops Manager of any discrepancies or irregularities in the information you have received or generated yourself.
  • Ensure that the EDC Ops Managers are made aware of any issues that may affect their ability to deliver.
  • Escalate any issues or concerns related Inventory Database drive issues to the Country EDC Ops Manager in a timely fashion.
Key Skills Required
  • Language: Proficient in written English and Chinese, Fluency in spoken Mandarin is required. Business level of spoken English is a plus.
  • Ability to switch context quickly and work on multiple streams of work concurrently
  • Ability to identify appropriate stakeholders within and beyond immediate team and work to build and maintain tight network/relationships with them
  • Strong communication skills - written and verbal, hosting remote conference and video calls
  • Self-starter able to identify where focus is needed and bring clarity to requirements
  • Ability to work in fast-paced environment with tight deadlines
  • Ability to work with remote teams across Asia
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • To be proficient in the use of a computer, particularly Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel, SharePoint.
  • This post carries no direct budgetary responsibility but a strong sense of financial awareness is required.
Education and qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree level / equivalent
  • Minimum 4 years of relevant working experience
  • ITIL, PMP certification (preferred)
