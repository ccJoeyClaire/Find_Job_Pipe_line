职位简介

Our Company


AppInChina is a leading publisher of international software in China, publishing over 300 of the world’s most popular apps, SaaS platforms, and games in the Chinese market. Our mission is to deliver a major positive impact on the world by becoming one of the world’s largest technology companies and we’re making good progress towards this goal with an average growth rate of 60% per year for the past 10 years. Our success is due to the outstanding colleagues who make up our company and we are looking for more truly exceptional people to join our team.



What do we offer?


- Monthly salary of CNY 30,000+, plus performance-based bonuses.

- Excellent work-life balance including 20 days of paid annual leave in addition to national holidays, no overtime work, and flexible working hours.

- Grade A office space at China World Tower B in Guomao, including gold-level membership of Trainyard gym worth CNY 18,888 per year.

- You’ll be working alongside a team of outstanding colleagues, ensuring that your working environment is both enjoyable and inspiring.

- You’ll be working at the cutting edge of the global and Chinese tech ecosystems, giving you an opportunity for ongoing learning on a daily basis.

- You’ll be working within a company culture that enables you to focus on your work and achieve your goals.



What will you do?


You will be responsible for maximising the sales of our software, products and services in China including:


- Advertising: managing ad campaigns across all of the major Chinese ad platforms (ByteDance, Tencent, Baidu, and RedNote), international platforms (Meta and Google), e-commerce platforms (Tmall, JD, and Douyin) and app stores (Apple, Huawei, Tencent, Xiaomi, and Oppo).

- Social Media: creating content (including text, images, and videos) to be posted on China’s major social media platforms including RedNote, Douyin, and WeChat.

- E-Commerce Platforms: creating stores and product pages on the major Chinese e-commerce platforms including Tmall, JD, and Douyin.

- B2B: creating B2B sales content to be published on company websites and suitable online platforms.


You’ll report directly to our company’s CEO, and work closely with our operations, marketing and sales teams.



What will you bring to our team?


- Native Mandarin Chinese and fluent English.

- An exceptional ability to write clear, concise, persuasive Chinese-language content.

- A high level of data literacy and a strong analytical mindset.

- A track record of academic and/or career success.

- Strong logical thinking and problem-solving skills.

- Excellent communication skills and a high level of attention to detail.

- Self-motivation and good time management skills.

- A desire to innovate, learn, and take on new challenges.
