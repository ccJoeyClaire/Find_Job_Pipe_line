职位简介

About A5 Labs

A5 Labs builds cutting-edge AI-driven security solutions that safeguard fairness and integrity across online strategy games, including online poker. Our anti-cheat systems leverage deep learning, game theory, federated learning, and real-time behavioral analysis to detect collusion, bots, and real-time assistance (RTA). Join us to work at the intersection of machine learning, data science, and competitive gaming to fight cheating and preserve trust at scale.


Overview

We’re hiring a Data Team Manager to lead and unify our cross-disciplinary data team — including data scientists, machine learning engineers, deep learning researchers, and analysts — focused on developing and scaling our AI-powered anti-cheat systems.

This is a strategic and technical leadership role responsible for aligning workstreams across bot detection, collusion analysis, and adversarial modeling. You’ll play a key part in designing scalable ML pipelines, coordinating data workflows, and building models that detect unfair play in real time. Your leadership will ensure seamless collaboration across R&D, engineering, and security operations.


Must-Have Expertise (Not Optional)

Candidates must have significant prior experience in the Anti-Fraud / Anti-cheat / online gaming security solutions. Seasoned technical expertise for Deep Learning and Neural Network/NN are required.



Responsibilities

Team Leadership & Management
  • Lead and grow a hybrid team of data scientists, analysts, ML/DL engineers, and researchers.
  • Define team OKRs, manage priorities, and ensure alignment across multiple anti-cheat workstreams (e.g., bot detection, collusion, exploit modeling).
  • Provide technical guidance and career development mentorship for team members.
  • Build cross-functional processes to support collaborative research, experimentation, and deployment.

Data Strategy & Architecture
  • Own the end-to-end data lifecycle, from ingestion and labeling to feature engineering and model monitoring.
  • Define and enforce best practices for data governance, privacy, versioning, and reproducibility.
  • Oversee data infrastructure and pipelines to support real-time and batch ML workflows.

Machine Learning & Deep Learning Operations
  • Coordinate the development and productionization of machine learning models for fraud detection, player behavior profiling, and exploit identification.
  • Drive MLOps best practices, including model retraining, drift detection, and automated evaluation.
  • Collaborate with the AI architecture team to ensure data and model alignment for deployment at scale.

Anti-Cheat & Game Intelligence
  • Partner with game theory experts, security teams, and domain specialists to identify cheating patterns, design data labeling strategies, and validate model output.
  • Facilitate the integration of exploitative modeling, inverse reinforcement learning, and human vs. AI behavioral analysis into data-driven workflows.
  • Oversee experiments to evaluate detection accuracy, false positives, and impact on user experience.

Cross-Functional Collaboration
  • Act as the data team's interface with AI architecture, software engineering, product, and operations.
  • Collaborate closely with product and leadership to translate detection needs into actionable technical projects.
  • Work with gaming operators and platform partners to ensure real-world effectiveness of data-driven defenses.


Qualifications

Required
  • Master’s or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, Machine Learning, or related field.
  • Strong hands-on knowledge of deep learning, Neural Networks and data engineering tools (e.g., Python, TensorFlow/PyTorch, SQL, Spark, Airflow, etc.).
  • Familiarity with gaming security solutions, gaming Anti-cheat, fraud detection, or adversarial AI environments is required.
  • 5+ years of experience in data science or ML, with 2+ years leading high-performing teams.
  • Experience managing multi-role data teams across scientist, analytics, ML, and research.
  • Solid understanding of MLOps practices, production model lifecycle, and real-time inference systems.

Preferred
  • Experience working on bot detection, user profiling, or behavioral modeling.
  • Background in game theory, reinforcement learning, or graph-based models.
  • Exposure to federated learning, privacy-preserving AI, or secure data collaboration frameworks.


Why Join A5 Labs?
  • Work at the frontier of AI, security, and online gaming.
  • Collaborate with world-class researchers and engineers.
  • Build systems that protect fairness and integrity at global scale.
  • Enjoy a flexible, remote-friendly, and innovation-driven culture.
