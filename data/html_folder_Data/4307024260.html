职位简介

Preferred Qualification:

  • 1-2years of experience in corporate banking
  • Graduated from a top university.



Job Purpose
  • Prepare comprehensive credit proposals and support the marketing team in maintaining client relationships, addressing any related issues.

Key Responsibilities
  • Conduct detailed analysis of financial statements.
  • Collaborate with the Relationship Manager or Account Manager to discuss customer needs, line structures, and associated terms and conditions.
  • Work closely with the Relationship Manager to gather all necessary information for processing credit proposals from clients.
  • Develop credit proposals with a strong focus on risk analysis and mitigation strategies, considering financial, operational, and environmental factors. Ensure financial highlights are well-documented and align with bank policies. Prepare credit presentations that are clear, concise, and of high quality.
  • Gain thorough knowledge of the bank’s credit policies, products, and services.
  • Cultivate a positive relationship with the Credit Manager to address any queries.
  • Attend to customer calls as necessary.
  • Manage account onboarding and prepare call reports.
  • Conduct KYC/AML related tasks, deal execution, and documentation.

Working Environment
  • Operate in a competitive, dynamic, and regulated business landscape with low entry/exit barriers. Adhere to the bank’s Credit Policy Manual while liaising effectively with superiors, direct reports, and support units to meet business goals.

Problem Solving
  • Assist the Account Manager/Relationship Manager in resolving issues to enhance customer satisfaction.

Decision-Making Authority
  • Responsible for preparing accurate credit proposals.

Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Finance, Accounting, Business Management, Economics, Entrepreneurship, or Engineering
  • Fresh graduates or candidates with up to 2 years of relevant experience in the banking or financial industry.
  • High proficiency in English and Mandarin; knowledge of Cantonese is a plus.
  • In-depth knowledge of the bank’s credit policies and operational systems.
  • Essential skills in credit, legal matters, and marketing.
  • Awareness of various financial products and services.
  • Excellent communication and analytical abilities, with a team-oriented mindset.
  • Strong command of the MS Office Suite.
