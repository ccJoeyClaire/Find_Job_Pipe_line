Job Description:
  • Conduct in-depth analysis of sourcing system modules and identify optimization opportunities.
  • Assess and document system requirements to ensure alignment with business objectives.

  • Work closely with procurement, logistics, and quality teams to ensure effective integration of sourcing solutions.
  • Facilitate workshops and meetings to gather insights and feedback for continuous improvement.
