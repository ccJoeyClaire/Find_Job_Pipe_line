Customer Success Manager

职位简介

Are you our "TYPE":

Named "One of the Most Innovative Companies in Design'' by Fast Company, Monotype brings brands to life through type and technology that consumers engage with every day. The company's rich legacy includes a library that can be traced back hundreds of years, featuring famed typefaces like Helvetica, Futura, Times New Roman and more. Monotype specializes in the design, development, licensing, and management of typefaces and font technologies for the world’s biggest global brands and individual creative professionals, offering a wide set of solutions that make it easier for them to do what they do best: design beautiful brand experiences.

Want to learn more about who we are, what we do, and how you can become part of our team of over 1,000 talented employees across the globe? Visit us at www.monotype.com


Who do we need:

We’re looking for a Customer Success Manager to partner with enterprise clients in the Greater China markets, helping them adopt our solutions and achieve measurable impact through design and typography.


What you’ll be doing:

Customer Relationship & Growth

  • Serve as a trusted advisor to enterprise customers, helping them succeed and grow through effective use of Monotype’s solutions
  • Understand each client’s business, brand, and creative workflow to help them realize full value from Monotype’s platform.
  • Drive customer satisfaction, retention, and advocacy through proactive engagement and success planning.
  • Identify and pursue opportunities for upselling and cross-selling in partnership with the Sales Team

Customer Success Operations
  • Lead onboarding and implementation to ensure smooth adoption and usage.
  • Respond to and resolve customer requests and complaints with enthusiasm and efficiency
  • Analyze customer usage data to identify risks and growth opportunities.
  • Conduct training sessions and platform demonstrations tailored to client needs.

Cross-functional Collaboration
  • Partner with sales, product, pre-sales and support teams to ensure a unified customer experience.
  • Gather and communicate customer feedback to influence the product roadmap.
  • Represent the customer voice internally to drive continuous improvement.


What we’re looking for:
  • 3+ years of experience in customer success, account management, or enterprise client services.
  • Passionate about solving customers’ problems and building strong customer relationships
  • Strong communication skills, both in English and Chinese.
  • Highly organized, proactive, and comfortable working across departments.
  • Familiarity with SaaS products, digital asset management, or enterprise software.
  • Preferred: Experience or knowledge in font design, typography, or design technology industries.
  • Experience with Salesforce or similar CRM systems is a plus.


What’s in it for you:
  • Flexible work arrangements and yearly paid vacation and sick time.
  • Competitive commercial medical insurance coverage to meet all your healthcare needs.
  • Yearly medical checkup arrangement
  • Global Recognition & Reward program
  • Shopping card/gifts for festivals, regular team buildings and employee activities
  • Extensive development and training offerings.
