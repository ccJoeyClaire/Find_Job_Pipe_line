The opportunity

Hitachi Energy is renowned worldwide as a specialist for electrical power transmission and distribution. As a global leader, we revolutionized high-voltage technology and pioneered many world’s firsts to bring safety, reliability and efficiency to power networks around the globe. High Voltage Products manufacturing facilities around the world, >100 locations worldwide providing 24/7 service. We have No.1 market share, installed 1 out of 4 high-voltage switchgear in the world, >500k high-voltage circuit-breakers installed globally.

How You’ll Make An Impact

  • You will assist communication between different departments about SPC project.
  • You will assist collecting the requirement for SPC from relevant departments.
  • You will assist in completing the technical requirements of the quality SPC project.
  • You will supervise the process and delivery time of SPC projects on time.
  • You will assist in completing the validation of quality SPC system.
  • You will be responsible to ensure compliance with applicable external and internal regulations, procedures, and guidelines.
  • Living Hitachi Energy’s core values of safety and integrity, which means taking responsibility for your own actions while caring for your colleagues and the business.

Your background
  • You hold a bachelor's degree or above, with statistics related majors.
  • No prior experience required.
  • Good English written and oral communication.
