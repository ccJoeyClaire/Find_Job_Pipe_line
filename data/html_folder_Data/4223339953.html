Overview

This is an exciting opportunity in an environment where you will embark on an exciting journey that goes beyond just an internship. We believe in empowering our interns to make a real impact in the dynamic and ever-evolving digital marketing landscape. You will work closely with our regional senior data team leadership on daily basis and all round data & project related tasks. In addition, you will be exposed to sizable internal & external projects that make true impact to our business and to our clients.

Responsibilities

WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING ON ANY GIVEN DAY

  • Collaborate with Data Manager and regional data team to build data solutions for clients
  • Support maintenance of existing data products
  • Perform query writing to clean, consolidate, manipulate raw dataset according to project needs
  • Connect, modify, QA, and incorporate any data sources needed
  • Data visualization with Tableau
  • Data and query QA
  • Regular reporting and hygiene checks
  • Support dashboard building
  • Any other tasks as directed

DOES THIS SOUND LIKE YOU?

  • Data-related educational background
  • Must know and can write SQL well
  • Proficient in Tableau (Preferred) / Power BI / Google Data Studio
  • Native in Mandarin and fluent in English
  • Self-motivated and keen to learn

NICE-TO-HAVE (Not Required):

  • Knowledge of media buying. i.e. google ads, baidu ads, red ads
  • Experience automating data workflows using Python
