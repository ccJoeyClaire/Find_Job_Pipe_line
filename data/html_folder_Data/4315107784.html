Your Impact

You will enable our clients to become truly data-driven and AI-powered by designing and implementing modern data ecosystems, cloud platforms, agentic workflows, and scalable AI solutions.

You will help shape agile analytics practices and foster a transformative data and AI culture.

You will have opportunities to work across various domains within our data advisory practice, including data architecture, AI strategy, data analytics, data governance, and agentic system design. You will advise clients on developing and optimizing their conceptual, logical, and AI-augmented data systems. You will assess new data and AI system requirements, design and deploy intelligent data ingestion, storage, integration, and analytical pipelines, and implement data, model, and AI agent migrations.

You will collaborate with clients and data users to establish long-term data and AI architecture goals, document current architectures, and define principles, standards, guidelines, and roadmaps toward target AI-enabled data architectures. You will design and implement data middle-platforms, big data platforms, data warehouses, and AI agent frameworks, and develop advanced analytics, predictive models, agentic automation cases, and interactive KPI dashboards. You will help clients understand how AI and agentic solutions can generate business value, working across industries to build compelling data and AI narratives through dashboards, analytical tools, agent prototypes, and executive presentations.

You will apply deep expertise in data management, AI governance, and agentic infrastructure in end-to-end client engagements. You will establish and promote an enterprise data and AI governance framework, including policies, standards, and practices for responsible AI and automated decision-making. You will articulate the impact of AI and advanced analytics solutions through business storytelling, data visualization, and demonstrations of agentic behavior. You will deconstruct complex client challenges, bringing expertise in AI technologies, agentic systems, analytics methods, tools, and operating models.

You will provide thought leadership on achieving data and AI architecture strategies and drive reviews to ensure solutions meet architectural and ethical AI requirements. You will assist clients in defining AI and data governance frameworks, processes, controls, and agent oversight mechanisms. You will apply hands-on experience with metadata management, master data management, data quality tools, AI platforms, and agentic workflow design. You will lead small teams in fast-paced client projects and manage multiple initiatives across industries, delivering end-to-end data and AI-driven solutions.

Your Qualifications and Skills
  • Undergraduate or master's degree in data science, computer science, AI, statistics, or a related field
  • 3+ years of work experience including large-scale data governance, enterprise AI, or data architecture projects; understanding of industry best practices in data and AI governance
  • Familiarity with big data, AI solutions, and agentic architectures
  • Experience designing and implementing agentic use cases and AI automation workflows
  • Ability to lead large data and AI projects with strong planning and implementation skills
  • Experience in top-tier consulting firms (domestic or international) is preferred
  • Hands-on experience with AI-driven KPI dashboards, analytics use cases, and applications such as ERP, MES, HR, PLM, CRM, e-Commerce, and OA
  • Proficiency in PowerPoint and Word
  • Excellent organizational and coordination skills; strong leadership and team collaboration abilities; ability to perform under pressure
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Willingness to travel
