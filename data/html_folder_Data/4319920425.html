职位简介

Your New Company

Our client is a globally recognised consumer and entertainment group with diversified businesses across digital, retail. The company places strong emphasis on privacy governance and compliance, and is now seeking a Legal Counsel - Data & Privacy to join its China legal team to support the ongoing enhancement of its data protection and privacy framework.



Your New Role

As Privacy Counsel, you will play a key role in implementing and maintaining the company’s privacy and data protection compliance program in China. You will work closely with the global privacy team and business functions to ensure alignment with both local and international data protection standards.


Key Responsibilities:
  • Develop, implement, and enhance privacy and data protection compliance programs across the China business.
  • Review and advise on data collection, processing, storage, transfer, and vendor management activities.
  • Draft, review, and negotiate data protection agreements and privacy terms.
  • Conduct privacy impact assessments (PIAs), data mapping, and risk assessments.
  • Monitor legislative and regulatory developments in China and globally, providing compliance guidance to internal stakeholders.
  • Deliver privacy training and awareness initiatives across the organisation.
  • Handle data subject requests and support incident response or regulatory inquiries.
  • Support internal audit preparation and global privacy compliance initiatives.



What You’ll Need to Succeed
  • PRC-qualified lawyer with around 5-8 years of experience;
  • Minimum 2–3 years of solid experience in privacy, data protection, or cybersecurity compliance;
  • Strong understanding of China’s data protection and privacy legal framework;
  • Experience gained from international law firms or multinational companies preferred;
  • Excellent analytical, drafting, and advisory skills with business-oriented thinking;
  • Strong communication skills in both English and Mandarin;
  • Detail-oriented, proactive, and capable of managing multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.



What You’ll Get in Return

You’ll join a global organisation with an established legal and compliance function, where privacy is a key strategic focus. This role offers exposure to international privacy initiatives, cross-functional collaboration, and a supportive, inclusive culture for long-term professional growth.



What You Need to Do Now

If you’re interested in this role, please contact Mark Yuan, Senior Consultant at Hays Legal China, for a confidential discussion about your career move.
