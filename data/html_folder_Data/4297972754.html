Key Responsibilities


1. Strategic Analysis & Thematic Research

• Develop research frameworks and analytical approaches on key topics such as overseas capacity planning, labor efficiency, tech ROI, and organizational design.

• Design data models and integrate perspectives from operations, finance, and HR to identify inefficiencies and structural opportunities.

• Produce forward-looking research briefs and strategic insights based on trends in global manufacturing, supply chain dynamics, and client strategies.


2. Cross-Regional Project Coordination & Strategy Execution

• Participate in major operational strategy projects across China, Vietnam, and Cambodia, breaking down goals, drafting timelines, and coordinating with frontline teams.

• Collaborate with business units to push forward critical initiatives, such as automation improvement, workforce modeling, and production-sales alignment.

• Assist in strategy review and monthly performance retrospectives to enhance organizational learning and real-time feedback loops.


3. Institutional Development & Governance Support

• Contribute to the design or refinement of institutional mechanisms supporting strategic execution, such as review systems and cost-efficiency assessments.

• Actively support the preparation and follow-up of executive management meetings, thematic workshops, and strategy-related reviews.

• Assist in strategy conference planning, regional reporting, and performance target alignment across business units.


4. Special Assignments from Senior Leadership

• Execute research, emergency analysis, or cross-border coordination tasks assigned directly by the Chairman.

• Support end-to-end management of select strategic initiatives, including planning, data integration, stakeholder meetings, and outcome reviews.

• Develop independent organizational insight through frontline observations, benchmarking, and critical assessments.
