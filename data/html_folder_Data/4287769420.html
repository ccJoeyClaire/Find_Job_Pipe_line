职位简介

Location: Beijing or Shanghai

Employment Type: Full-time, On-site

Job Description
  • Develop planning & control algorithms that determine and execute autonomous vehicle behaviors based on road environments and real-time traffic conditions.
  • Design and implement high-performance, high-reliability planning & control algorithm architectures.
  • Develop and optimize high-precision computational geometry and numerical computation algorithms.
  • Enhance autonomous vehicle capabilities in behavioral prediction and driving decisions using machine learning algorithms.

Requirements
  • Master’s/PhD in Mathematics, Computer Science, Robotics, Autonomous Driving, or related fields.
  • Strong programming skills with proficiency in C++; ability to implement complex algorithms efficiently.
  • Solid mathematical foundation and familiarity with mainstream machine learning algorithms.
  • Exceptional problem abstraction and modeling capabilities for novel industry challenges.

Preferred Qualifications
  • Expertise in computational geometry and numerical optimization algorithms.
  • Prior experience in hardware competitions/development (e.g., autonomous vehicles, robotics, drones).
  • Outstanding performance in competitions (e.g., ACM-ICPC, TopCoder).
