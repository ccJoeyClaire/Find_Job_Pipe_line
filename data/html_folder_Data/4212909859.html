About The Job

  • Creating automation standards for Alfa Laval
  • Developing automation system specifications
  • Creating automation system hardware design
  • Investigating next generation core automation platform
  • Reviewing and testing other team members code blocks
  • Supporting our automation solutions throughout the organisation

Who are you?
  • You are marine engineer or electrical engineer at bachelor or master level combined with related work experience
  • Experience in Automation software design
  • Experience in creating automation system specifications
  • PLC and HMI programming knowledge
  • Object orientated software design
  • High Level programming knowledge preferred
  • Experience in creating test specifications is preferred
  • Sensor and actuator regulation experience preferred
  • Genuine interest and abilities in industrial and marine processes
  • Good English skill both oral and written.

Alfa Laval is a leading global provider of first-rate products in the areas of heat transfer, separation and fluid handling. With these as its base, Alfa Laval aims to help enhance the productivity and competitiveness of its customers in various industries throughout the world. We define their challenges and deliver sustainable products and solutions that meet their requirements.

Alfa Laval today has customers in some 100 countries, employs more than 21,300 people, and annual sales were SEK 63.6 billion (5.5 BEUR) in 2023. The company is listed on Nasdaq Stockholm.
