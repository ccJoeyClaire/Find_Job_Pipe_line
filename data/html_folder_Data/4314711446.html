Scientist, Consumer Data Evaluation

欧莱雅 · 中国 上海市 上海市 (现场办公)

职位简介

Main Responsibilities

  • Data Analysis & Insight Generation: Conduct in-depth analysis of large, complex product evaluation datasets and translate technical data into clear, concise, and actionable insights that inform product optimization and strategic planning.
  • Capability Building: Develop and implement analytical models, methodologies, and tools to enhance the department's ability to extract deeper intelligence from test data. Contribute to building a strong data-driven culture within the team.
  • Reporting & Visualization: Design, build, and maintain interactive dashboards and reports using data visualization tools to present findings effectively to various stakeholders
  • Database Querying & Management: Work proficiently with our existing database to extract, transform, and load (ETL) data for analysis, ensuring data integrity and accessibility.
  • Collaboration & Communication: Partner closely with product evaluation scientists and managers to understand specific data needs and deliver tailored analytical solutions. Clearly communicate complex analytical concepts and results to non-technical audiences.

Requirements
  • Capability in a data analysis, business intelligence, or a similar quantitative role, preferably within a product evaluation, R&D, or quality assurance context.
  • Strong proficiency in SQL for data extraction and manipulation.
  • Expertise in at least one statistical programming language (e.g., Python with libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn; or R).
  • Experience with data visualization tools (e.g.,Power BI) to create compelling and interactive dashboards.
  • Solid understanding of statistical analysis techniques (e.g., hypothesis testing, regression analysis, ANOVA) and their application to experimental data.
  • Ability to translate complex analytical findings into clear, actionable business recommendations.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to present technical information to diverse audiences.

Educational Background & Experience
  • Master's above degree in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering, or a closely related quantitative field.
  • Fresh gratulate talent or with 1-3 years of experience in data analysis, business intelligence are both accepted

Personal Qualities
  • Possesses a strong analytical mindset, with exceptional attention to detail and a passion for uncovering insights from data.
  • Proactive and inquisitive, with a natural inclination to identify problems and develop innovative data-driven solutions.
  • Eager to explore new data methodologies, tools, and industry trends to continuously enhance capabilities.
  • Able to articulate complex technical information clearly and concisely, fostering understanding and driving alignment across teams.
  • A team player who thrives in a collaborative environment and can build strong working relationships with colleagues.
  • Driven by impact, with a focus on delivering tangible value and measurable improvements through data insights.
