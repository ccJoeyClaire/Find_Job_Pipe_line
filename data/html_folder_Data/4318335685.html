职位简介

Business Unit


Cloud & Smart Industries Group (CSIG) is responsible for promoting the company's cloud and industry Internet strategy. CSIG explores the interactions between users and industries to create innovative solutions for smart industries via technological advancements such as cloud, AI, and network security. While driving the digitalization of retail, medical, education, transportation and other industries, CSIG helps companies serve users in smarter ways, building a new ecosystem of intelligent industries that connect users and businesses.


What The Role Entails


Position Overview

Tencent Cloud is inviting 2026–2027 graduates to join our Solution Architecture Internship Program. From Jan–May 2026, you’ll work with our pre-sales and technical consulting teams to design cloud solutions for enterprise clients across Asia Pacific markets. You will gain exposure to cloud solution design, proposal preparation, technical enablement, and customer engagement while building practical experience in IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS use cases. This is an exciting opportunity to learn how global cloud solutions are planned, designed, and delivered.


📅 Internship Period: Jan–May 2026

📍 Location: Shenzhen

🎓 Eligibility: 2026–2027 Graduates

✨ Perks: Internship allowance, Accommodation allowance, Return flight ticket allowance, Company canteen & gym, Corporate insurance, Festive gifts, and more!


Responsibilities
  • Assist solution architects in understanding client requirements and mapping them to Tencent Cloud’s offerings.
  • Support the preparation of technical proposals, documentation, and client presentations.
  • Participate in proof-of-concept (PoC) testing and solution validation.
  • Research industry-specific architecture patterns and prepare recommendations.
  • Contribute to internal knowledge sharing on cloud solution best practices.


Requirements:
  • Pursuing a Bachelor’s/Master’s in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (other majors with strong interest in cloud welcome).
  • Graduating in year 2026–2027, with valid student status during the internship.
  • Able to commit minimally 3 months full-time between Jan–May 2026.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP, or Tencent Cloud) preferred.
  • Strong logical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills.
  • Proficient in English, Bahasa Indonesia and Mandarin to work with global and China HQ-based teams.
