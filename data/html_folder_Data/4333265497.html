职位简介

At Crossing Hurdles, we work as a referral partner. We refer candidates to our partner that collaborates with world’s leading AI research labs to build and train cutting-edge AI models.


Position: Bilingual Simplified Chinese Education Expert

Referral Partner: Crossing Hurdles

Type: Hourly Contract

Compensation: $25–$30/hour

Location: Remote

Commitment: 10–40 hours/week


Role Responsibilities (Training support will be provided)
  • Translate, adapt, and synthesize educational workflows and curriculum content from the Chinese education system for AI model training.
  • Write fluently in both Simplified Chinese and English, ensuring precise educational terminology and nuanced expression across languages.
  • Provide cultural insights specific to Chinese teaching practices, assessments, and school norms.
  • Collaborate with peers and reviewers to validate accuracy, refine instructional content, and align with educational standards.
  • Contribute structured feedback to enhance AI understanding of pedagogical reasoning and instruction design.


Requirements
  • Native-level fluency in Mandarin (Simplified Chinese) with strong English proficiency.
  • Advanced degree in Teaching, Pedagogy, Education, or a related field.
  • Strong experience teaching in China across various educational levels.
  • Excellent writing, analytical, and communication skills.
  • Experience creating or evaluating assessments is highly valued.
  • Experience with rubric development is a plus.
  • Familiarity with generative AI or machine learning concepts is beneficial but not required.


Application process: (Takes 20 min)
  • Upload resume
  • AI interview based on your resume (15 min)
  • If shortlisted, complete a short follow-up AI-led interview assessing professional background
  • Paid work trial to evaluate project fit
  • Submit form
