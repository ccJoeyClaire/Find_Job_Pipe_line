We are currently seeking a Helpdesk Analyst - Full Time to join our team in Dalian, Liaoning (CN-LN), China (CN).

  • Will be responsible to Manage Desktop / Laptop and IT Equipment.
  • Will be responsible to trouble shoot escalated Calls or unresolved calls related to Desktop / Laptop and IT Equipment.
  • Will be responsible to prepare Knowledge base document.
  • Customer service orientation and ability to work in a team.
  • Responsible for troubleshooting hardware/ software and application related issues.
  • Troubleshooting of all IT equipment’s.
  • Resolve Incidents/ Service Request with in the agreed time lines.
  • Ensure the tickets are resolved with the agreed norms in end user computing.
  • Prepare RCA for violated incidents with preventive and corrective measures
  • Follow Escalation Matrix for unresolved calls
  • Coordinate with various support providers for technical issues, Team mentoring and training skills.
  • Good interpersonal and communication skills
  • Ability to perform in adverse situations.
