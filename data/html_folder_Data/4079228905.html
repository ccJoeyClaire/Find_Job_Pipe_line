Responsibility


- Responsible for the account-based reporting

- Oversee the account-based analytics. Consultative Partner for progressing account-based engagement with client & stakeholders. Close relationships with delivery teams. Identify trends and provide recommendations. Interprets the data in relation to the business.

- Project manage analytics initiatives

- Ensure data visualization tools provide actionable insights. Develop new reports and sets the direction for ad hocs.

- Provide weekly analysis of distributed reporting by performing monthly quality checks/governance audits

- Ongoing knowledge sharing & guidance to support the development of RA's

- Supports QIMs with consultative insights
