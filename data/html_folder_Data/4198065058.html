Description

Job Description:

  • Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) for Total Company Closing Activities
  • Independently lead the closing activities and work closely with Account-to-report (ATR) team, Commercial Support finance team, FP&A COE team
  • Ensure the closing activities are carried out timely and compliantly, issues are actively followed-up, clearly communicated and cleared in a timely manner
  • Monitor actual result versus latest thinking and planning cycle, and provide insightful Budget vs Actual analysis, as well as any action plan for enhancement
  • Planning and Latest Update Cycle
  • Actively engage in planning process including BP/JU/NU/LRFP/LT/etc, work closely with Business Unit Finance (BUF) and FP&A COE team to lay out the Medical China calendar, and facilitate the line manager to setup Finance internal review meeting as well as meeting follow-ups
  • Support line manager build review template based on business dynamics and consolidate the template from BUF team for CFO review, monitor business trends and identify upside/downside on top-line & bottom-line and communicate timely to management for actions to deliver financial commitments
  • Management Review Deck
  • Support line manager to develop and improve BU actionable dashboard and business performance analysis with recommendations to help business partners to make fact-based decisions
  • Draft and finetune finance related deck, such as monthly board deck, planning cycle review deck, global / regional visitor review deck etc
  • Business Partnering to Enabling Functions
  • Act as a strategic partner and provide financial expertise to Enabling Function heads
  • Lead the planning process with stakeholders for the supported Enabling Functions in line with the total company’s planning timeline
  • Timely provide management reports to the Enabling Function heads and closely monitor the actual performance and department spending as well as any risks and opportunities and mitigation plans
  • Others / Projects / Ad-hoc
  • Participate in initiatives to continuously streamline financial planning processes and drive operational efficiency
  • Ad hoc analysis to support Business needs
  • Ensure Business related activities / transactions are in line with J&J Worldwide Procedures and internal control requirement

Qualifications
  • Bachelor Degree in Accounting or Finance, master degree is a plus
  • Professional certification is preferred (e.g CPA, CMA, ACCA)
  • 5+ years working experience of big MNC with Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) experience
  • Solid working knowledge of accounting principles, concepts, and compliance
  • Familiarity with ERP system (such as SAP) and planning system (such as TM1), advanced Excel and PowerPoint skills and ability to handle and interpret big data sets; Data automation and visualization skills are preferred
  • Team player, and strong communication and influencing skills
  • Learning agility and good trouble shooting and problem solving skills in a complex and dynamic environment
  • Business acumen, strong analytical and framing skills
  • Able to read, write and speak English fluently
