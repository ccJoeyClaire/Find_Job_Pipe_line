职位简介

位于南京的巴斯夫中国数字化中心吸引、培养和发展充满热情的数字化人才，他们将对巴斯夫的数字化未来产生深远的影响。快来加入我们，成为巴斯夫数字化之旅的一部分。

Digital Hub China in Nanjing attracts, grows, and develops passionate people who will meaningfully impact the digital future of BASF. Come join us and be a part of our digitalization journey.

Objectives:

Our Manufacturing & OpEx Intelligence Team is supporting the operational divisions and central functions with global data around Manufacturing, Asset Effectiveness and provides the required transparency for BASF production. This data is the essential part of all improvements of our manufacturing processes.

You are heading the holistic data analysis and initiate improvements of data quality in core systems. Your tasks include preparation of a wide range of data, creation of statistical analyses, selection and training of machine learning models and their evaluation.

Main Task:

  • You initiate and drive the data mining activities based on our ERP and production data and transform them into meaningful structures.
  • You work in cross-functional projects at the interface between manufacturing and engineering, understand data workflows and build a reliable data foundation.
  • You work with state-of-the art data science frameworks, develop new algorithms, and create solutions for our internal business partners.

Job Requirements:
  • A university degree in the field of Natural Science, Engineering or Mathematics or other subjects, such as business informatics, industrial engineering, or other comparable education.
  • Minimum 3 years’ related working experience
  • Experiencing Agile way of working or agile implementation approach
  • You have in-depth experience in data analysis, data management, security, visualization as well as in consulting for business stakeholders and have practical experience in the field of data analytics, data science, data mining or machine learning.
  • Fluent English communication skills

Nice to have:
  • Experience in project management
