Job Description

Senior Revenue Analyst

Job Summary：

  • Perform business analysis to generate market-specific insights across all regions and channels to assist China revenue growth.
  • Develop promotion and pricing related strategies, and improve trade investment efficiency through various data analysis.
  • Responsible for driving revenue growth projects from end to end, and deliver results through collaborating with cross function teams such as Category, Marketing and R&D.
  • Develop deep understanding in business, help to establish reasonable and effective analysis model and system.
  • Support Revenue Management team across key tasks, and collaborate with Global Revenue teams.


Job Requirement：

1.5+ years working experience, Business/Econ/Data Science/Marketing Analytics major preferred.
  • Working experience in FMCG/Retail/Consulting is preferred.
  • Strong analytical skills with solid business acumen, able to generate insight through data.
  • Strong experience with Excel & PPT and BI tools (Tableau/Power BI etc.), SQL strongly preferred, Python is plus.
  • Equipped with fast learning capabilities, able to take initiatives, and be comfortable challenging the status quo.
  • Strong stakeholder management to work with colleagues in different functions across various levels.
