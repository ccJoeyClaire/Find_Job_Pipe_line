职位简介

Veeva Systems is a mission-driven organization and pioneer in industry cloud, helping life sciences companies bring therapies to patients faster. As one of the fastest-growing SaaS companies in history, we surpassed $2B in revenue in our last fiscal year with extensive growth potential ahead.

At the heart of Veeva are our values: Do the Right Thing, Customer Success, Employee Success, and Speed. We're not just any public company – we made history in 2021 by becoming a public benefit corporation (PBC), legally bound to balancing the interests of customers, employees, society, and investors.

Join us in transforming the life sciences industry, committed to making a positive impact on its customers, employees, and communities.

The Role

Veeva is looking for a developer who can produce deliverables throughout the full lifecycle of Veeva production to a wide range of Life Sciences clients. As a developer, you will mostly be responsible for the business analysis, functional design, development standardization, and technical framework of the application.

What You’ll Do

  • Responsible for business process analysis, including product design, core development, and system integration. Collaborative commerce in the pre-sales stage
  • Individual contributor for Veeva data products delivery. Responsible for high-quality customer project deliverables
  • Hands-on production of project deliverables, including technical design and related documents

Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business/Management Information Systems, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or other similar degree
  • Familiar with the product implementation processes and popular programming languages, such as Java, Python, and SQL
  • 5+ years of work experience in the IT industry

Nice to Have
  • Work experience in the Life Sciences
  • Familiar with AWS and popular open source, Spring Cloud, Springboot, and Gateway

Veeva’s headquarters is located in the San Francisco Bay Area with offices in more than 15 countries around the world.
