Position Summary

Fully support reporting manager in developing pricing strategies. Integrate and coordinate the pricing of all POS in CNHK, standardize and unify pricing standards for all POS. Pricing structure & competition analysis. Market investigation and data analysis.

Key Accountability

  • Fully support Regional Manager in developing pricing strategies.
  • Integrate and coordinate pricing across all points of sales (POS) in CNHK, ensuring the standardization and unification of pricing standards.
  • Serve as the key point of contact (PIC) for submitting CNHK pricing requests to the Revenue Management Department (RMD) and coordinating price approvals.
  • Review and optimize pricing for all MH products.
  • Conduct pricing structure and competitor analysis.
  • Perform market research / investigation and data analysis.
  • Manage price releases and maintain partnerships with Global Distribution Systems (GDS) such as Travelsky
  • Develop and continuously optimize the NDC pricing strategy.
