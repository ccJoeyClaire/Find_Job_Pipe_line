About the job

Delart is home to a team of world-class engineers and project leaders dedicated to developing the next generation of advanced networking technologies, consumer devices, and innovative technology solutions. Trusted by some of the world's leading technology companies, we deliver highly scalable, mission-critical solutions that power both consumer and business applications globally. Since our founding in 2017, we have been at the forefront of designing cutting-edge networking, telecommunications, and consumer products, while driving end-to-end project execution and creating transformative technology applications.

Overview

Join our innovative team as an HW Validation Automation Engineer, where you'll play a crucial role in developing and maintaining automated test scripts for hardware validation using cutting-edge technologies. Your expertise in Python scripting and test automation will drive consistent and reliable validation processes, fostering product excellence. Collaborate with multi-disciplinary teams to design solutions that enhance our validation processes, ensuring our continued leadership in technology.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain automation test scripts for hardware validation using Python.
  • Analyze test data and results, identify anomalies, and provide effective solutions.
  • Troubleshoot and debug issues in the automation framework and test scripts to ensure consistent validation results.
  • Document user guidelines, procedures, and equipment configurations for automation.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements and design automation solutions.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to enhance the automation process.
  • Travel up to 30% as needed to various locations for validation processes.

Minimum Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • Graduates from Tier 2 (C9) universities or equivalent overseas institutions (e.g., QS100 rankings).
  • 0 to 1 year of experience in software or Android development.
  • Proficient programming skills in Python with a focus on scripting and test automation.
  • Experience using source control tools like Git or SVN.
  • Excellent oral and written English communication skills.
  • Strong communication and teamwork abilities.

Preferred Qualifications
  • Master's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • Graduates from Tier 1 universities, such as Tsinghua or Peking University, or equivalent overseas institutions.
  • Prior experience in validation and testing of electronic hardware.
  • Knowledge and experience working with Android/iOS devices and embedded systems.
  • Experience in ARM-based software development, MCU firmware, and Linux shell scripting.
  • Familiarity with data analysis and visualization tools such as Matplotlib and pandas.
  • Understanding of electronics and instrumentation, including oscilloscopes, power supplies, and electronic loads.
