Quality Control Engineer

威德福 · 中国 山东省 淄博 (现场办公)

职位简介

Job Description

Assess and improve quality control processes, personnel and inspection equipment to ensure QC competency and standardization throughout completions quality control and to drive efficiency.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct process audits according to Standard Work/Technical Work Instructions and analyze non-conformances to implement corrective actions.
  • Perform product inspections to ensure compliance with Weatherford drawings and specifications.
  • Assist in investigating quality escapes and communicate findings with customers and internal teams.
  • Support technical and manufacturing teams in resolving design or process-related quality issues.
  • Review and improve inspection procedures, work instructions, specifications, and forms to enhance Quality Control efficiency, including Supplier QC.
  • Participate in new supplier audits and collaborate with Sustaining Engineering to address drawing issues and implement design-for-manufacturing/inspection improvements.
  • Train personnel on quality procedures, inspection tools, and equipment while ensuring compliance with Weatherford Quality Policy, Quality Systems Manual, operating procedures, and applicable HSE rules.
  • Track quality improvement initiatives and follow up on actions to ensure effectiveness, performing additional duties as assigned.

Required

 QUALIFICATIONS

  • Minimum of 3 years of relevant work experience.
  • Associate degree or above in Mechanical Engineering, Industrial Engineering, or a related field.
  • Proficient in using various standard inspection tools and equipment, such as calipers, micrometers, hardness testers, and surface roughness testers.
  • Experience in inspecting special processes, including non-destructive testing and welding inspection.
  • Familiarity with API Q1/ISO 9001 standards and AWS D1.1.
  • Competent in computer operations and software applications.
  • Skilled in applying quality analysis tools.
  • Good English reading and writing skills.

Preferred
  • Bachelor’s degree in a technical discipline; manufacturing engineering a major plus
  • Good English communicating skills.
