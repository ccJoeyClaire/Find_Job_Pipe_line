Job Overview

Quectel is the world-leading IoT solution provider, serving a growing customer base of more than 7000 in IoT sector across North America, Latin America, Europe & Middle East Africa, and APAC. With ambitious business growth strategy, we are looking to bring an IoT Market Intelligence analyst into our Market Intelligence team. Our team functions as an internal intelligence role to support Quectel’s business growth in IoT sector. This is your chance to shape the future of IoT as part of the industry innovation pioneers team.

Our associate is supposed to be: “Knowledgeable”, “Self-Starter,” “Organized”, “Energetic” and most importantly “Passionate”.


Key Responsibilities
  • Research market segments with leads generation result oriented.
  • Output industry trend reports with insights on future potential growth or technology evolvement. This typically includes developing hypotheses, managing data collection, model creation and analyses, conducting primary and secondary research, creatively tackling information limitations, and surfacing insights.
  • Create models about market size and growth trend of hardware shipment not only by technology and region but also by vertical.
  • Support due diligence projects from management team through accurate and high-quality case execution.


Required Education, Experience and Competencies
  • Based in Shanghai
  • Fast-learner with background in Telecommunication, Electronic, Computer engineering or related area is highly preferred. Previous experience or internship in research institute is preferred.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills with self-driven passion to work in a team.
  • Basic knowledge of IoT sector, from devices to cloud (Components, Devices, Connectivity, IoT platform).
  • Track record of multi-tasking and result-driven projects
  • Fluent verbal and written communication skills in English.
