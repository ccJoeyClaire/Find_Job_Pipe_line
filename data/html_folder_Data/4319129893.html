职位简介

Our client is a top-tier, technology-driven hedge fund from China, expanding its global operations. They are known for their advanced, AI-powered trading systems. They have recently set up their international office in Singapore and are now looking for a Portfolio Finance Trader. (Can be based in Hong Kong/ Singapore/ Shanghai/ Shenzhen)


This is the first Portfolio Finance Trader role covering offshore business. It is a great opportunity to join a young, energetic team during a key growth phase, offering high ownership and the chance to make an immediate impact.


Key Responsibilities:
  • Execute and manage the firm's trading strategies in the offshore environment.
  • Work directly with top-tier Prime Brokers (e.g., Morgan Stanley, Citi, Goldman Sachs) to ensure seamless strategy fulfilment, manage stock loan recalls, and optimize the trading setup.
  • Monitor and manage risk and P&L for the offshore trading book.
  • Collaborate closely with the quantitative research and technology teams in China to implement and refine algorithmic trading models.
  • Proactively identify and solve operational and trading challenges inherent in the offshore structure.


Requirements:
  • A minimum of 5 years of relevant experience
  • Proven, hands-on expertise with prime brokerage systems and the complete trade lifecycle in Asian market
  • Familiar with stock loan market, securities lending and equity swaps etc
  • Strong programming skills, with experience in C++, Java, and/or Python for strategy implementation or trading system interaction.
  • A self-starter mentality with the ability to operate autonomously; this is not a training role and requires immediate impact.
  • Energetic, collaborative, and thrives in a fast-paced, flat-structure team environment.
  • Fluent in English; Mandarin language skills are a plus but not mandatory.
