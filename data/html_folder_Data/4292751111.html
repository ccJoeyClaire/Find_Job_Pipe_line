Assistant Project Manager - Architecture

Burberry · 中国 上海市 (现场办公)

职位简介

Responsibility:

· To project manage a portfolio of fast track fit out projects ensuring they are within programme, budget and design tolerances.

· Manage the contractor tender process and the activities of the development team, the general contractor, specialist contractors and suppliers.

· Define and maintain a project cost plan covering all aspects of the planned CAPEX.

· Analyse each works package contract and identify the key interfaces critical path and milestones.

· Advise upon all areas of risk to the Works and risk management.

· To carry out inspections both on and off-site of the works by the general contractor, the specialist contractors and suppliers appointed directly and effectively communicate to the business on all matters relating to progress.

· To support the layout development and further project revision through the regional stakeholders

· Prepare organisational plans and structures and define responsibilities and reporting relationships.

· Advise on the strategy for procurement of all construction packages and purchase orders for works required to complete the project and manage the orderly handover of the site to the business - ensuring all administration is complete and up to date.

· Follow the Gateway process and issue close out schedules, leading up to completion. Snagging lists issued in a timely manner and followed up with contractors to a high standard. Issuing of the complete O+M manual in our standard format, within the agreed timescales.

· Growth in the role to manage renovations and major projects once they have gained experience in the role.

Personal Profile:

· University degree – with emphasis on Project Management / QS, Engineering, Architecture or equivalent.

· Relevant experience managing construction projects.

· Complete understanding of luxury retail construction.

· Ability to budget and manage project costs.

· Ability to manage consultants and local teams to ensure timely completion and maintenance of global standard for all projects.

· Communication skills written and oral – many times communicating in cultures where English is the secondary language.

· Flexibility and willingness for extensive travel.

· A solid understanding of financial reconciliation of construction related costs.

· Construction experience in the region is an advantage.

· Ability to read MS project and analyze a construction schedule, as well as, ability to complete one.

Born from innovation, Burberry is a global luxury brand with a rich British heritage. 

Founded in 1856, our brand is underpinned by our founder’s passion for the outdoors. Thomas Burberry invented the innovative rain-ready fabric gabardine to protect explorers from the elements. In doing so, he created the foundation for the iconic Burberry trench coat, which remains core to the business even today. 

Guided by our history of exploration and our shared belief that ‘creativity opens spaces’, our purpose is to unlock the power of imagination to push boundaries and open new possibilities for our people, our customers and our communities.

As a values-driven brand, we are committed to creating the next generation of sustainable luxury and believe that diversity, equity and inclusion are essential to fulfilling our purpose. Fostering an inclusive culture where differences are valued and embraced enables us to be more creative, engaged and make a more meaningful contribution to the world around us.
