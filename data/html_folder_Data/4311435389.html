职位简介

Job Description

Our internship is the perfect introduction for you to get an enjoyable experience of our firm, culture, people and the opportunities we provide. Working alongside colleagues on real client projects, you’ll enjoy structured work-based learning, skills training and presentations in Pinsent Masons.

As part of our recruitment strategy, we strive to recruit trainees from those who have interned with us. Therefore, candidates who would like to be considered for our trainee programme are strongly encouraged to apply for our internship.

What We Are Looking For

We are seeking an energy and dynamism student with a Law School background; passing the PRC Bar Exam is a valued criterion.

You’ll excel both as a collaborative team player and as an independent contributor in a fast-moving environment. Demonstrating composure and resilience under pressure, you communicate fluently in both English and Mandarin Chinese, bringing outstanding interpersonal and organizational skills to the role.

Proactive, effective time management, and a commitment to confidentiality and professionalism are essential.

Applications for an internship should include the following:

  • Cover letter indicating your preferred area of practice & availability
  • Bilingual Curriculum Vitae (CV) in Chinese and English
  • Current degree transcripts
  • Any other supporting documentation you consider relevant


If you have any queries relating to Internship in Beijing, please contact us at recruitmentbj@pinsentmasons.com .

Should you have any issues with your application, please email applications@pinsentmasons.com .
