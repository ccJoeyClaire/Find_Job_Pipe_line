职位简介

Some Careers Have More Impact Than Others.

If you’re looking for a career where you can make a real impression, join HSBC and discover how valued you’ll be.

We are currently seeking an experienced professional to join our team in the role of Senior Consultant Specialist.

Business: Enterprise Technology L3

Principal Responsibilities

  • Conduct application engineering as a full stack engineer.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders across business, architects, analytics and IT to an intelligent led, deliver data driven solution on top of cloud platform.
  • Work with Tech Lead and Solution Architects to complete the low-level design.
  • Collaborate with central teams (architecture, security, engineering, networks) who are responsible for delivering HSBC baseline cloud architecture.
  • Ensure Dev Sec Ops capabilities are built in as part of the solution.
  • Ensure development is completed at a high standard and is aligned with IT governance and control frameworks.

Requirements

You will be tenacious about doing things the right way and building efficient and ‘brilliantly simple’ business solutions. You will also be adept at working in customer facing roles within enterprise environments, helping clients to capitalize on technology for their commercial benefit.

Knowledge & Experience/Qualifications
  • Overall 8+ years of experiences in application delivery.
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, Engineering or equivalent; advanced degree is preferred.
  • Excellent core Python skills.
  • Prompt engineering experience preferred.
  • Hands on experiences in Google / Azure Cloud platforms.
  • Hands on experiences in CI/CD pipelines.
  • Experiences in Generative AI applications with good understanding of RAG, Agentic Framework.
  • Experiences in micro-service architecture design.
  • Experience working with Kubernetes, Docker, Storage, Cloud Functions.
  • Security. (IAM, AD, ADLDS, roles, service accounts, entitlements)
  • Events & Data Streaming: Data Proc, Pub-Sub, Confluent/Kafka.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and able to work independently and under pressure.
  • Ability to communicate and explain complex ideas in both oral and written English.
  • Good stakeholder management and influencing skills.
  • Experience using GIT, Jira, Maven, Gradle, Jenkins and Ansible.
  • Agile development, particularly Scrum/Kanban.

Key HSBC Role Capabilities [non-technical]:
  • Experience in Banking and Financial Services with good understanding of the legal and compliance business.
  • Good communication (verbal written).
  • Ability to explain complex ideas.
  • Ability to work in a multicultural environment.
  • Keen to learn and keen to mentor.
  • Experience in delivering complex solutions at large organizations of varying size and complexity.
  • Experience working in a global environment.
  • Highly motivated, outcome driven, and results oriented with the ability to prioritize and drive measurable value.
  • Strong problem solving and analytical skills.

Ways Of Working Experiences Sought
  • Experience of Agile principles and processes and leading teams.
  • Methods using DevOps tools and processes.
  • Understanding of and practical experience working with DevOps teams.
