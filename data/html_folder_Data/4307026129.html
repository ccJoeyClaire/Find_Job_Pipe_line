职位简介

Responsibilities:

  • Preparation of a project execution strategy for the Projects in liaison with the CCMS Operation Team including the establishment and implementation of project management best practices and standards to better ensure the timely delivery of projects
  • Preparation of technical proposals, Materials specification, detail designs and SOO (Sequence Of Operation) and take lead to his/her responsible CCMS projects. Manage and report monthly project progress to Chief Engineer. Establish and implement standard project management best practices to help improve timeliness of project delivery.
  • Liaison with other Company department to develop new functions of CCMS system and enhancement the system continually.
  • Liaison with the Company’s Facilities Procurement and Supply Chain (P&SC) department in tendering procedure for CCMS projects. Preparation and compilation of technical documents for inclusion in tender packs. Actioning and drafting of both pre and post tender technical queries, with the latter responsible for the commercial evaluation of returned tenders, review contract draft and follow up contract approval status.
  • Developing and Monitoring of overall project timelines, programmers and cost management
  • Monitoring and management of site progress including works coordination and progress monitoring and reporting, ensure the projects can be completed in a timely fashion and within budget.
  • Technical reviews of and comment on consultant and contractor submissions / deliverables in liaison with the MEP CCMS Team.
  • Assessment of claimed progress payments submitted by consultants and contractors in liaison with the MEP CCMS and P&SC Teams.
  • Implementation of completion / close out procedures including compilation of defects/snagging lists, compilation and filing of all completion and handover related documentation required under the respective works and services agreements, including O&M manuals, as-built drawings.
  • Performance of other tasks and duties as assigned by Vice President of MEP department


Requirements:
  • A strong and effective leader with excellent communication and team building skills.
  • Experienced in project management best practices, project delivery standards, processes and templates.
  • Schedule development and monitoring skills including base line schedules, critical path development and analysis and progress reporting.
  • Experienced in the use of Microsoft Office Suite programmes, including Excel.
  • Strong technical background on HVAC design field, able to modify/justify/enhance the quality of SOOs which are submitted by CCMS contractor.
  • Strong knowledge of CCMS architecture and Honeywell/Niagara based CCMS systems
  • Basic knowledge of IT network architecture. Ability to communicate with IT department on IT network Design
