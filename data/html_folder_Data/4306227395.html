Job Description

Job description

Join our team and start a new adventure in an international and dynamic environment, where you will be able to fulfil your career expectations in a fast-growing organization. As an Engineering Consultant, you will contribute to innovative automotive projects, working closely with experienced engineers and cross-functional teams. This position is ideal for recent Master’s graduates in mechanical engineering or related fields, as well as graduates with up to 2 years of working experience, who are eager to develop their skills and grow within the consulting sector.

Your missions

  • Assist in the design and development of automotive products in collaboration with experienced engineers and project teams
  • Contribute to technical studies, feasibility analyses, and engineering problem-solving
  • Perform modeling and simulation of mechanical components using specialized software
  • Participate in project management and follow-up of development phases
  • Support the continuous improvement of processes and technical solutions
  • Collaborate with internal and external partners to ensure project success
  • Engage in technology watch and innovation within the automotive sector
  • Benefit from personalized coaching by an experienced professional in the automotive industry


Your profile
  • Master’s degree in Engineering field
  • Up to 2 years of relevant professional experience (internships, graduation projects, or relevant project experience are a plus)
  • Fluent Chinese and English; German is a plus
  • Experienced in mechanical design software (CATIA, SolidWorks, CAD, Creo) preferred
  • You have strong communication skills, teamwork abilities, adaptability, and a willingness to learn


What We Offer
  • Exposure to cutting-edge technology and projects in the automotive industry.
  • Opportunities for professional development and career advancement. Guidance from experienced industry professionals and structured training programs.
  • An international community bringing together 110+ different nationalities.
  • An environment where trust has a central place: 70% of our key leaders started their careers at the first level of responsibilities.
  • Strong commitments to CSR, notably through participation in our WeCare Together program.
