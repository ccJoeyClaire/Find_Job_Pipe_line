A Career at HARMAN Lifestyle

We’re a global, multi-disciplinary team that’s putting the innovative power of technology to work and transforming tomorrow. As a member of HARMAN Lifestyle, you connect consumers with the power of superior sound.

  • Contribute your talents to high-end, esteemed brands like JBL, Mark Levinson and Revel
  • Unite your passion for audio innovation with high-tech product development
  • Create pitch-perfect, cutting-edge technology that elevates the listening experience

About The Role

As a DSP Project Lead at Harman, you are responsible for successfully running the DSP part of our Projects and ensure project deliveries in time, budget and quality.

What You Will Do
  • Establish communication and synchronization with global cross functional teams involved in the project on a regular base
  • Plan, monitor, control and track the DSP project to achieve the goals with respect to schedule, effort, product quality and process quality in a timely manner
  • Provide project status updates on a regular base
  • Define and establish planning guidelines and priorities for the development
  • Serve as the face to the customer for all DSP related topics
  • Work with DSP and Acoustic team to deliver quality works

What You Need To Be Successful
  • Expert knowledge in project management and coordination in a globally distributed development environment
  • Expert knowledge in software engineering
  • Knowledge of signal processing and acoustic
  • Knowledge of risk management
  • Excellent communication skills, strong interpersonal and relationship management skills with excellent conflict resolution and negotiation skills
  • Structured and result-oriented working style
  • Ability of teamwork with internal/external teams

Bonus Points if You Have

  • Self-starter, goal oriented and result driven
  • Strong team player
  • Customer focus
  • Self-awareness, willing to learn and open to changes

What Makes You Eligible
  • Willingness to travel.
  • Willingness to work in an office.
  • Any offer of employment is conditioned upon the successful completion of a background investigation and drug screen.

What We Offer
  • Flexible work environment, allowing for full-time remote work globally for positions that can be performed outside a HARMAN or customer location
  • Access to employee discounts on world-class Harman and Samsung products (JBL, HARMAN Kardon, AKG, etc.)
  • Extensive training opportunities through our own HARMAN University
  • Competitive wellness benefits
  • An inclusive and diverse work environment that fosters and encourages professional and personal development
