职位简介

We are looking for a talented intern for our Clarivate Consulting Internship Program to join our Academia & Government team in Beijing. This is a unique and unparalleled opportunity for an ambitious and driven individual to gain invaluable industry experience, global exposure, skill development and extensive networking opportunities. As an intern, you will report to Director of Consulting business in China and collaborate with Sales, Customer Success team and other key stakeholders to deliver top-tier service to our clients. We would love to speak with you if you have a strong background in STEM and academic consulting.

What will you be doing in this role?

Consulting Delivery
  • Designing and preparing analysis, data visualizations, report and presentations which clearly communicate findings.
  • Collaborating with diverse individuals including other analysts, technology experts, and scientific subject matter experts.
  • Secondar research and data collection.
  • Consulting with clients to identify their needs, manage their expectations and present complex research findings.
  • Typical projects include reports evaluating a research portfolio, interactive tools for examining a research field, organization or network, and datasets for use by institutions in their internal reporting systems.

Service Delivery
  • Diving deep into the full project execution process of service delivery, ensuring that tasks are advanced on schedule and completed to the highest quality, providing clients with an exceptional service experience.
  • Engaging in effective communication with stakeholders to accurately grasp requirements and clearly articulate project outcomes.
  • Collaborating with local and global teams to well execute delivery throughout the whole process to ensure customer satisfaction.
  • Leveraging creativity and professional knowledge to design and prepare service content, reports, and presentations.

About The Team

Clarivate’s Academic and Government Consulting is a diverse group of practitioners working across the global delivering consulting revenue globally. The Academic and Government Consulting group is made up of three practice areas: Research Analytics, Reputation, and Evaluation & Assessment. Across our practice areas, there is also a wide range of skill sets (e.g., data scientists, bibliometricians, visualization specialists, analysts, etc.). Across our different practice areas there is also a wider variety of capabilities and services we provide to clients along with different commercial and go-to-market models.
