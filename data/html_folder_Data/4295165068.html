Tool Engineer

Coperion · 中国 江苏省 南京市 (现场办公)

职位简介

Position Summary

Ensure the tool efficiency according requested quality.

确保刀具效率符合要求的质量。

Work You’ll Do

1、Tool Management (Assembly, preset, calibrate the tools, quality checking for incoming tools, maintain the tools, tool inventory, PO of Tool releasing etc.).

工具库的管理 (刀具装配，预设，调校，进货质量检查，刀具维护、盘点、刀具采购订单的下发等)；

2、Preparing of Tool Fixtures, order related documents.

根据生产订单，准备刀具及工装；

3、Controlling and optimizing the tool in warehouse.

控制、优化刀具库存；

4、Administering the purchasing order of the tools of tools.

负责刀具的采购订单的管理；

5、Support the investigation, development and implementation of new technologies.

支持新刀具调查、开发，新技术执行；

6、Follow up the use condition of tools and to solve tool problem under guidance of MTD,addressed by the manufacturing team.

负责刀具使用状况的跟踪和信息收集，并在工艺部门的指导下解决制造部门提出的刀具问题；

7、Other tool-related work.

其它与刀具相关的工作。

Team

Operation - Manufacturing Team - Tool Warehouse

运营部 - 制造团队 – 刀具库

Basic Qualifications

College or above.

大专及以上学历；

Mechanical manufacturing related industry with tool management experience.

有机械制造相关行业刀具管理经验；

Knowledge of various tools used in the manufacturing.

了解生产制造中使用的各种工具。

Preferred Qualifications

Experience in CNC operating is preferred

有CNC操作经验者优先

Who We Are

Coperion is an industry leader in compounding and extrusion, feeding and weighing, bulk material handling and service, bringing a wealth of know-how and experience to the market.
