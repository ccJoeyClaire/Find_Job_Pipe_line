职位简介

What are the key objectives and expectations from this role?

  • New Categories (NC) Operations covers 30+ markets 3 Commercial Regions and the US, a diverse range of cultures and volatile operating environments.
  • NC Operations is also driving supplier process and system standardization, as well as building capability within the suppliers’ network on core processes, and exception-based management.


Reports to

Manufacturing Performance Manager


Core Relationships


BAT – Operations Team, Procurement, New Product Development (NPD), Quality, R&D, Compliance

External – Tier 1 suppliers and key Tier 2 suppliers

Role is based at supplier site


ACCOUNTABILITIES


  • Drive Daily, Weekly & monthly DDS (Daily Direction Settings) drumbeats with supplier
  • Daily liaison and supply base management for Quality, Cost and Delivery.
  • Resolves supplier issues and concerns immediately which threaten supply and demand.
  • Drives Manufacturing Performance KPI But not limited to (OEE, MTBF, Scrape, Yield) to meet organization targets
  • Delivers and manages an effective and flexible supply base to deliver OTIF.
  • Ensures manufacturing capacity is optimized to deliver future demand.
  • Assesses performance in the supply base to increase output without impacting quality.
  • Works closely with the Supplier to deliver Continuous Improvement initiatives.
  • Ensures good Project Management principles are applied and all risks, issues and opportunities are correctly and timely communicated.
  • Deploy Loss elimination tools to reduce production losses for cost optimization


EXPERIENCE, SKILLS, KNOWLEDGE

Experience Required

  • Engineering background in the electronic consumer industry (5-10 years).
  • A proven track record of managing first tier suppliers and subsequent supply chains.
  • Proven project management skills with robust action and recovery plans, focusing on maintaining delivery to schedule whilst applying permanent solutions.
  • Experience working within monthly & weekly control cycles
  • The ability to call and facilitate effective reviews both internally and within the supply chain, prioritizing actions and allocating accountability to deliver the targets we set.


Technical/Functional/Leadership Skills Required

  • Understanding of Production processes of electronic industry
  • Understanding in Supply planning processes.
  • Experienced in Root Cause Analysis and FMECA Process
  • Experienced in successful launches of TPM Pillars specially AM & PM Pillars, Lean, six-sigma, automation, MES system (Automatic Data collection)
  • The ability to drive suppliers’ performance align with company goals and strategies
  • The ability to train and educate inexperienced suppliers and groups toward mastering these standard tools.
  • Ability to generate, ‘cost down’ opportunities to reduce waste throughout the process.
  • Demonstrable interpersonal and communication skills, able to influence stakeholders across the commercial and operational business including suppliers.
  • Business focused and proven change agent with project management skills (preferably in a Prince II environment).
