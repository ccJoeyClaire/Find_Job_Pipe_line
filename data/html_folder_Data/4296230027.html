职位简介

A Career at HARMAN Lifestyle

We’re a global, multi-disciplinary team that’s putting the innovative power of technology to work and transforming tomorrow. As a member of HARMAN Lifestyle, you connect consumers with the power of superior sound.

  • Contribute your talents to high-end, esteemed brands like JBL, Mark Levinson and Revel.
  • Unite your passion for audio innovation with high-tech product development.
  • Create pitch-perfect, cutting-edge technology that elevates the listening experience.

About The Role

Design, develop, and maintain scalabel, efficient, and robust backend systems using AWS services and backend technologies.

What You Will Do
  • Design, develop, and maintain scalable, efficient, and robust backend systems using AWS services and backend technologies.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features and improvements.
  • Ensure code quality by implementing best practices, coding standards, and performing code reviews.
  • Troubleshoot, debug, and optimize system performance to achieve the highest levels of reliability.
  • Assist in integrating data engineering and cybersecurity principles into the development process.
  • Continuously research, evaluate, and adopt new technologies to maximize development efficiency.
  • Mentor junior team members, provide technical guidance, and promote a culture of continuous learning and improvement.

What You Need To Be Successful
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, engineering, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
  • 5+ years of backend development experience, including hands-on experience with AWS and backend technologies.
  • Excellent problem-solving and critical-thinking skills, along with a passion for staying up to date with emerging technologies.
  • Good knowledge of cloud infrastructure fundamentals: networking, security, container technologies, etc.
  • Production experience with AWS and Linux environments.
  • Strong software development skills in at least one of following programming languages: Go, Python, Java.
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills.

Bonus Points if You Have

  • Experience in data engineering, including ETL processes, data warehousing, and big data technologies.
  • Knowledge of cybersecurity principles, best practices, and tools.
  • Familiarity with containerization and orchestration technologies, such as Docker and Kubernetes.

What Makes You Eligible
  • Experience with Agile development methodologies and the use of CI/CD pipelines.
  • Knowledge of embedded system and firmware development.

What We Offer
  • Flexible work environment, allowing for full-time remote work one day per week for positions that can be performed outside a HARMAN or customer location.
  • Access to employee discounts on world-class Harman and Samsung products (JBL, HARMAN Kardon, AKG, etc.)
  • Extensive training opportunities through our own HARMAN University.
  • Competitive wellness benefits.
  • Access to HARMAN Campus Fitness Center and Cafeteria.
  • “Be Brilliant” employee recognition and rewards program.
  • An inclusive and diverse work environment that fosters and encourages professional and personal development.
