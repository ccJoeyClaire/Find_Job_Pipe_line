职位简介

Syensqo is all about chemistry. We’re not just referring to chemical reactions here, but also to the magic that occurs when the brightest minds get to work together. This is where our true strength lies. In you. In your future colleagues and in all your differences. And of course, in your ideas to improve lives while preserving our planet’s beauty for the generations to come.
We are looking for:

  • A student with chemistry analytics skill to work in the Analytical Lab

We count on you for:
  • Literature search on some analytical methods and different sample composition and application;
  • Conducting basic chemistry laboratory 5S operations;
  • Support the multi projects and research topics across the team
  • Be responsible for related testing of laboratory samples;
  • Analysis and processing of experimental data;
  • Ensure all daily work meets HSE requirements.

You can count on us for:
  • On-job training and practical instructions from our experienced scientist.

You will bring:
  • Background on Chemistry related, Chemistry Engineering, or material science etc.;
  • Basic English listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills;
  • Basic competencies in experiment, basic chemical analysis skills;
  • Good skills in team work and complete tasks independently ;
  • Strong learning ability
  • A full-time internship for 6 months is an ideal option. At least 2 days per work is also accepted.

You will get:
  • Competitive salary
  • Free shuttle bus
  • Free lunch and coffee

Additional information:
  • Location: Minhang, Shanghai
  • Onsite working model
