Import & Export Specialist

职位简介

Job Description

Key Responsibilities:

  • Execute daily import/export customs clearance for chemical raw materials & finished products, including document preparation, freight forwarder coordination, and shipment tracking.
  • Maintain and file customs records, ensure accurate data entry into the system.
  • Assist in logistics cost verification.
  • Support Finance with customs declaration copies and duty accrual data collection.
  • Update and maintain basic product HS codes and declaration requirements.
  • Coordinate cross-departmental communication internally, and manage and evaluate external freight forwarding suppliers.

Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s degree or above with 1-3 years of import/export customs operations experience ;
  • Basic knowledge of customs and CIQ policies;
  • Experience in chemical product import/export is preferred;
  • IMDG certification or IATA certification is a plus.
  • Familiarity with SAP system is advantageous.
  • Good English communication skills with strong stress resistance, adaptability, organizational and communication abilities; highly responsible.
  • Willing to travel as needed for customs/CIQ documentation procedures and on-site inspections.
