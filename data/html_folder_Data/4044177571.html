职位简介

About Us: At Booking.com, data drives our decisions. Technology is at our core. And innovation is everywhere. But our company is more than datasets, lines of code or A/B tests. We’re the thrill of the first night in a new place. The excitement of the next morning. The friends you encounter. The journeys you take. The sights you see. And the memories you make. Through our products, partners and people, we make it easier for everyone to experience the world.

Key Job Responsibilities and Duties:

  • Identify key customers and collaborate proactively through effective communication and addressing their needs.
  • Prioritize impactful business problems, translate them into feasible data problems, and solve them effectively by applying suitable statistical data analysis and modeling methods.
  • Identify, source, and prioritize data required for the solution and prepare it in a way that makes it fit for use.
  • Transform large, sophisticated datasets into pragmatic, useable insights.
  • Maintain the data processing workflows that provide clean and curated data in their area.
  • Use data visualization skills and storytelling to drive actions from internal customers.
  • Craft and interpret quantitative experiments to objectively guide key business decision making.


Role Qualifications and Requirements:
  • Experience solving real problems using data mining techniques and with statistical rigor, and translating business problems into data problems.
  • Knowledge and practical experience of Statistical Methods (e.g., Testing, Generalized Linear Models (GLM), Inference), Statistical/Machine Learning (e.g., Dimension Reduction, Clustering, Regularisation, Bagged/Boosted methods), and Time Series.
  • Strong experience with A/B Testing and a range of Experimentation methods.
  • Solid understanding of Hadoop, Spark, SQL, Python, and/or R.
  • Solid understanding of data management, including schema design, data pipelines, and workflows.
  • Strong stakeholder management skills and fluent in English and Chinese communication.
