Entrepreneur (Bootcamp Participant)

深圳科创学院 · 中国 广东省 深圳 (现场办公)

职位简介

We’re seeking talents with strong entrepreneurial drive and interest in developing smart hardware startups to join our 2026 Winter InnoX Bootcamp from Jan 26th 2026 to Feb 2nd 2026.


What we can offer as an incubator?

InnoX connects various resources based on entrepreneurs' needs and fosters a collaborative community where entrepreneurs can thrive together.
  • Community & Collaboration – Join a network of hardware entrepreneurs, user groups, and university-enterprise partnerships.
  • Global Resources – Access international channels, branding support, overseas talent, and localization services.
  • Government Support – Benefit from talent policies, workspace, subsidies, and application scenarios.
  • Funding & Investment – Connect with investor clubs, roadshows, tailored fundraising strategies, and post-investment support.
  • End-to-End Enablement – Integrated resources across talent, global networks, government, and capital to accelerate growth.


Who should apply?

Students:
  • Clear entrepreneurial drive with strong interest in tech ventures (startup ideas are a plus)
  • Junior year or above (Bachelor’s/Master’s), with time to fully commit to offline entrepreneurial exploration
  • Deep insights into their field of study, perferrably with engineering background but not a must - open to all fields of study
  • Strong hands-on ability; experience with hackathons, engineering, robotics competitions is highly valued


Professionals with Global Experience:
  • Clear intent to start a venture (startup ideas are a plus)
  • 1–5 years at leading tech/hardware companies (e.g. Apple, NVIDIA, DJI, Insta360), now considering full-time entrepreneurship in Shenzhen
  • Backgrounds mainly in engineering or product management
  • Deep understanding of a specific industry or technology
  • Open mindset, reflective, able to integrate diverse perspectives


Startup Founders:
  • Already fully committed to building a startup, but looking to explore new directions
  • Focused on smart hardware terminals (e.g. smart sports, smart home, robotics, and beyond)
  • Team direction shows innovation and strong growth potential
  • Seeking co-founders, as well as support in technology, market, supply chain, and fundraising


Pre-Angel round startups seeking support in technology, market development, supply chain management and fundraising etc. are welcomed to apply as well as we have a non-bootcamp track for startups with existing ideas (Direct Message to connect for details).
