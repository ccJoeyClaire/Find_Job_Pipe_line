职位简介

Conviva is the intelligence layer for digital businesses, turning every consumer interaction into outcome-based intelligence—linking engagement patterns across AI agents, apps, websites, and streaming video to real results such as purchases, bookings, and resolved support requests. Powered by its patented Time-State Technology®, the Conviva® Operational Data Platform delivers real-time insights and automation that help leading enterprises grow, improve satisfaction, and build lasting trust.

We’re looking for passionate new graduates to join our team and build scalable data solutions. You’ll work with cutting-edge technologies to process massive datasets and power data-driven decisions. Mentorship and real-world projects will be key parts of your onboarding.

What Success Will Look Like

  • Learn & Develop: Design, build, and optimize data pipelines (ETL/ELT) under guidance
  • Big Data Tools: Hands-on experience with cutting-edge big-data technologies and cloud platforms (AWS/GCP/Azure).
  • Data Processing: Assist in batch/real-time data processing, debugging, and performance tuning.
  • Collaboration: Work with senior engineers, data scientists, and analysts to support data infrastructure needs.
  • Innovation: Explore open-source tools and propose improvements to existing systems.

Who You Are & What You've Done
  • Recent graduate (2025/2026) with a Master’s degree or above in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related fields
  • Strong understanding of Computer Science fundamentals, including data structure, OS, distributed systems, data base, AI & ML, and algorithms.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and curiosity about technologies
  • Fluent English Communication: Verbal and written English skills, with the ability to articulate complex technical concepts clearly to diverse audiences.
  • Plus: Internship or hackathon experience in data engineering is a plus.
  • Plus: Demonstrated success in domestic or international competitions (e.g., IMO, IPhO, ACM-ICPC, Kaggle, Topcoder).
