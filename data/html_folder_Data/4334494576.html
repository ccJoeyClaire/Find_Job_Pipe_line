职位简介

Looking for a role with complete flexibility where your earnings are based on results?

At SME Careers, you can work as many hours as you wish - this is a fully remote opportunity where success is measured by the experts you bring in, not the time you clock.

We connect skilled professionals with opportunities in the rapidly evolving AI industry, and we’re now seeking an experienced Recruiter to help us expand our global network.


The Role

We are seeking an experienced Recruiter who can help us expand our network by identifying and referring highly qualified experts across different fields. This is a unique, performance-based opportunity: instead of a fixed salary, you will earn a referral fee for every expert you refer who is successfully hired — and continue to receive that fee for as long as they remain engaged.


As a Recruiter, you will:
  • Proactively source and identify skilled professionals across a variety of industries.
  • Build and maintain strong candidate pipelines, ensuring a continuous flow of qualified experts.
  • Act as a long-term talent partner, strengthening and expanding our professional community over time.


Your Profile

We are looking for someone who brings both recruiting expertise and strong communication skills:
  • Proven experience in recruitment or talent acquisition, ideally across multiple industries.
  • Experience hiring in China and other Chinese-speaking countries across Asia.
  • Native or bilingual Chinese proficiency.
  • Strong English skills (B2–C1 or above) for effective communication with our international team.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to engage candidates persuasively.
  • Self-motivated, target-driven, and comfortable working independently.


Why Work with SME Careers?
  • Competitive Pay: Help grow our network and get rewarded for your referrals by receiving a referral fee for every expert you refer who is hired - and continue to earn that fee for the duration of their engagement.
  • Your Schedule, Your Rules: Work fully remote, set your own hours, and manage recruitment entirely around your lifestyle.
  • Global Reach: Connect experts from around the world with opportunities in AI training projects.
  • Long-Term Rewards: Build a sustainable income stream from successful placements.
