职位简介

Our client is a leading Blue Chip Conglomerate who is a leader in its industry.

We are seeking a highly skilled Solution Architect specializing in Infrastructure to join our client, a leading blue-chip conglomerate. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience in enterprise environments, focusing on voice and data networks, security, and data protection. You will play a critical role in designing and implementing robust infrastructure solutions that align with business objectives.


Key Responsibilities:
  • Design and architect infrastructure solutions for enterprise environments, ensuring scalability and reliability.
  • Develop and implement voice and data network solutions, optimizing performance and security.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and mitigate potential security vulnerabilities and ensure data protection compliance.
  • Provide technical leadership and guidance throughout the project lifecycle, from requirements gathering to deployment.
  • Conduct regular assessments of infrastructure performance and recommend improvements.
  • Stay updated on emerging technologies and best practices in IT infrastructure and security.
  • Ensure adherence to ITIL practices and frameworks in all project implementations.


Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Over 12 years of working experiences of which at least 3 years experiences as a Solution Architect in an enterprise environment.
  • Strong technical competencies in voice and data networks, security protocols, and data protection strategies.
  • Familiarity with ITIL practices and frameworks.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work under pressure.
  • Strong communication skills, with the ability to convey complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
  • Good communication in Cantonese / Chinese.
