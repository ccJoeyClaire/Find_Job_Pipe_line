职位简介

Research Associate_Automotive and IoT, China


Counterpoint is a fast-growing global analyst firm. Our analysts produce a significant body of research that needs to be optimally presented. We are seeking a talented and enthusiastic Research Associate to assist our research efforts.


Job Description:

As a Research Associate, you will play a pivotal role in driving insights into the Automotive and IoT (Internet of Things), and ecosystem landscape by researching, analyzing, and tracking key data and trends. You will have the opportunity to develop skills and insights to become a future star analyst.


The role will involve:
  • Analyzing and processing relevant data and information to be maintained within our proprietary databases.
  • A key contributor to automotive and IoT research related work, including custom research projects, regular projects, research blogs, reports and whitepapers - by working with various internal teams.
  • Frequently communicating with industry stakeholders, tracking key drivers and inhibitors in the automotive and IoT space, and understanding how these factors affect overall industry trends.
  • Co-working with marketing team to promote services. Frequently express your industry viewpoints and enhance your personal industry influence.


Key Skills/Experience Required:

You are a masters or bachelor with 1+ years of relevant work experience in technology, telecom, internet, media, or finance sectors. Fresh graduates with strong potential are also encouraged to apply. Candidates from all academic disciplines are welcome, though a background in Telecommunication, Automotive Engineering or Electronics Engineering is a plus. Preference will be given to those with experience in automotive, semiconductors, or the broader pan-TMT (Technology, Media, and Telecom) sector.


In addition, you can demonstrate the following:
  • A strong sense of self-motivation with a deep interest in analyzing trends within the technology sector.
  • The ability to analyze and synthesize the available data and bring out value-added insights.
  • Exceptional analytical and critical thinking skills, with the ability to evaluate complex information and present clear, logical insights.
  • Excellent communication skills with a proven ability to collaborate effectively within diverse, international teams.
  • Fluent in both spoken and written English, with the ability to collaborate with global colleagues and clients across regions including Europe, North America, Korea, Japan, India, and other key markets.
  • Strong Microsoft Office skills especially Excel and PPT.
  • Ability to work confidently as an individual contributor under the coach of Senior Analysts.


Location:
  • Beijing, China. You must be eligible to work legally in your service region.


Benefits & Perks:
  • Collaborate with a global network of industry pioneers and experts.
  • Engage directly with tech powerhouses.
  • Cultivate your expertise through continuous learning, training and coaching.
  • Support for wellness and personal growth.
  • Progressive sabbatical opportunities.
  • Fun, vibrant work atmosphere (Frequent contests and challenges with cash gifts around the year).
  • Annual training retreats to exotic destinations (previous locations: Bali, Kuala Lumpur, Bangkok, Ko Samui, Dubai etc).
  • Annual support for tech gadgets such iPhones and Android smartphones.


How to Apply:
  • Apply via the link (please attach your CV in English)
  • We will accept applications on a continual basis.
