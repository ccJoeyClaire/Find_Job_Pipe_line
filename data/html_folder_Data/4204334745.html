What You'll Do

As a part of BCG's X team, you will work closely with consulting teams on a diverse range of advanced analytics, including AI, Generative AI, machine learning, optimization, simulation, and cutting-edge engineering topics, among many others. You will have the opportunity to leverage analytical methodologies to deliver value to BCG's Consulting (case) teams and Practice Areas (domain) through providing analytical and engineering subject matter expertise.

As a Data Analyst, you will play a crucial role in developing, delivering and supporting analytics solutions that empower our clients to make informed business decisions. You will provide technical support through a deeper understanding of relevant data analytics solutions and collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including data scientists, data engineers, and business stakeholders, to deliver high-quality data solutions that meet our clients' needs.

What You'll Bring

Who You Are

  • Strong hands-on experience in at least one analytics topic or platform, with a broad understanding of all analytics capabilities
  • Strong proficiency in statistics (concepts & methodologies like hypothesis testing, sampling, etc.) and its application & interpretation
  • Hands-on data mining and predictive modeling experience (Linear Regression, Clustering (K-means, DBSCAN, etc.), Classification (Logistic regression, Decision trees/Random Forest/Boosted Trees), Timeseries (SARIMAX/Prophet)etc.
  • Strong experience in at least one of the prominent cloud providers (Azure, AWS, GCP) and working knowledge of auto ML solutions (Sage Maker, Azure ML etc.)
  • Price optimization, promotion effectiveness, stock optimization and resource allocation, Personalization/Loyalty programs, Labor Optimization
  • CLM and revenue enhancement (segmentation, cross-sell/up-sell, next product to buy, offer recommendation, loyalty, LTV maximization, and churn prevention)
  • At least one tool in each category;
  • Programming language - Python (Must have), (R Or SAS OR PySpark), SQL (Must have)
  • Data Visualization (Tableau, QlikView, Power BI, Streamlit) ,
  • Data management (using Alteryx, MS Access, or any RDBMS)
  • ML Deployment tools (Airflow, MLflow Luigi, Docker etc.)
  • Big data technologies ( Hadoop ecosystem, Spark)
  • Data warehouse solutions (Teradata, Azure SQL DW/Synapse, Redshift, BigQuery etc,)
  • Version Control (Git/Github/Git Lab)
  • MS Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)
  • Coding IDE (VS Code/PyCharm)
  • GenAI tools (OpenAI, Google PaLM/BERT, Hugging Face, etc.)
  • Gurobi or Cplex Syntax
  • Experience in fine-tuning OSS LLMs is a big plus
  • Delivering analytical insights to case teams through sophisticated synthesis and packaging of results (including PowerPoint, dashboards and charts), collecting, synthesizing, analyzing team learnings and inputs into new best practices and methodologies.
  • Thinking Analytically: You should be strong in analytical solutions with hands-on experience in advanced analytics delivery through the entire life cycle of analytics. Strong analytics skills with the ability to develop and codify knowledge and provide analytical advice where required.
  • Communicating with confidence and ease, delivering messages in a concise manner with strong and effective written and verbal communication

What You'll Bring (Experience & Qualifications)
  • Bachelor/Master's degree in a field linked to business analytics, statistics or economics, operations research, applied mathematics, computer science, engineering, or related field required; advanced degree preferred
  • At least 2-4 years within relevant experience as a Data Analyst with the specified skillsets, providing analytics solutions in a commercial setting.
  • Consulting or professional services experience will be considered a plus.
  • Experience in deploying end-to-end analytics projects with hands-on understanding of data mining, data visualization, data management, along with proficiency in statistical modelling and forecasting on real-world sue cases
  • Ability to travel based on client and business needs. Expect 70%+
