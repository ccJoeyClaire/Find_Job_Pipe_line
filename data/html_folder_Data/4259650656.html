职位简介

Location: Shenzhen Flexible - This role allows the flexibility to work from home, within proximity to an office location.

The Role

As a Senior Product Quality Engineer for GoPro cameras and accessories you will be responsible for helping to ensure all hardware meets the GoPro standards for quality and reliability.

In this role you will have a direct impact on driving the highest quality standards to ensure GoPro continues to deliver the most rugged cameras in the market. You will lead the planning and execution of HW QA related tests including Mechanical, Electrical and Thermal.

What You Will Do

  • Serve as Quality & Reliability Engineering lead for products throughout the product development life cycle.
  • Define product validation plans, test methods and criteria in collaboration with cross functional teams.
  • Participate in post product launch efforts and early field failure analysis to establish root cause and implement corrective and preventive actions.
  • Facilitate failure analysis during NPI stages, validate/analyze test data, suggest solutions and make recommendations toward product release.
  • Develop and present management reports highlighting product risks, actions and recommendations.
  • Work independently and with project team members globally to develop and deliver.
  • Product Risk Analysis (DFMEA) and Product Quality Plans (PQP), review manufacturing assessment and qualification, support development of inspection documentation and test methods as they will impact product quality and performance.
  • Achieve GoPro quality standards by utilizing tools and techniques including statistical analysis, SPC, DOE, DFx, and project tracking software (Jira).
  • Evangelize the continuous improvement efforts related to NPI and sustaining processes.
  • Manage and execute EE QA testing.
  • Assist in the planning and execution of thermal related tests.


Skills We’re Stoked About
  • Ability and will to travel to APAC to work with partners, suppliers and CMs.
  • BSME, MSME preferred.
  • Six sigma experience and is highly desirable.
  • 3-4+ years’ experience in consumer electronics or relevant industries.
  • 2-3+ years’ experience in New Product Introduction environment.
  • Excellent communication skills both written and verbal.
  • Professional working proficiency in written and spoken English.
  • Previous knowledge or experience with design tools / CAD systems.
  • Exposure and working knowledge of key commodities such as LCD’s, Optical components, batteries, PCBs, molded and machined parts.
  • Effective presentation, project and resource management skills.
  • Demonstrated problem solving and root cause analysis skills.
