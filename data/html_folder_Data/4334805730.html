Duties

The brand business development expert, takes the responsibility to achieve the targets for the number of brand clients developed and the volume of business generated. Major duties will be:

探索品牌客户、其所在行业和其价值链在产品测试、认证、性能评估、质量保证等方面的潜在业务需求

与各产品线和项目组进行协调沟通，并在必要时加速项目的进度
  • Support management team to analyze market trends and achieve target set by electrical management team

协助电子电器管理团队进行分析市场趋势分析，实现电子电器管理团队设定的目标
