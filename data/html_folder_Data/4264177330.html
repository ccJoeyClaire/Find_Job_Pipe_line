职位简介

As the Country Manager for one of our key growth markets in Europe, you will be responsible for driving the company’s long-term strategic planning and daily operations in the region, the core includes user growth, category planning, guide marketing, user experience and P&L. You will lead both user growth and user operations initiatives, working closely with stakeholders across the global headquarters — including User Growth, Brand & Marketing, Categories, Product&Tech, Public Relations, Government Affairs, Logistics, and more.

You will play a pivotal role in shaping localized strategies that align with global objectives while ensuring sustainable business growth and an exceptional user experience tailored to the local market landscape.



Job Descriptions
  • Be responsible for the overall business strategy and performance in the assigned European market, driving sustainable growth in GMV, user base, and revenue.
  • Develop and execute localized business plans aligned with global objectives, with a strong focus on market entry, brand positioning, and customer acquisition strategies.
  • Lead full-funnel user growth initiatives — from awareness to conversion and retention — leveraging data insights, local marketing channels, and product localization.
  • Lead the category planning in the market, identify industry opportunities combine the user demands, competitors, market trends, and other dimensions, and collaborate with the category team to promote the optimization and promotion of the business structure.
  • Collaborate closely with global functions (marketing, product, logistics, government affairs) to ensure seamless execution of key initiatives and rapid iteration based on market feedback.
  • Monitor business KPIs, identify growth levers and risks, and drive continuous optimization across operations, pricing, promotions, and channel partnerships.



Job Qualifications
  • 8+ years of professional experience in marketing, e-commerce, or the internet industry, with a background in user growth, integrated marketing, category operations; prior experience in cross-border e-commerce is preferred.
  • In-depth understanding of European market conditions, consumer behavior, and regulatory frameworks, with the ability to develop effective business strategies tailored to local markets.
  • Strong strategic thinking and business acumen, capable of driving data-informed decisions, sustaining business growth, and solving complex operational challenges.
  • Proven experience managing multicultural teams, with strengths in talent development and organizational building, and the ability to collaborate and lead effectively in diverse cultural environments.
  • Fluent in English with strong business communication skills; proficiency in a European language is preferred; MBA or Master’s degree is a plus.
