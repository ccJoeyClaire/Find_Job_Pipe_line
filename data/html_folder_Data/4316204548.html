Company Description

Louis Dreyfus Company is a leading merchant and processor of agricultural goods. Our activities span the entire value chain from farm to fork, across a broad range of business lines, we leverage our global reach and extensive asset network to serve our customers and consumers around the world. Structured as a matrix organization of six geographical regions and ten platforms, Louis Dreyfus Company is active in over 100 countries and employs approximately 18,000 people globally.

Job Description

Industrial Reporting:

  • Manage industrial-related data inclusive process data & product data, etc.
  • Track & analyze relevant data to provide opportunities for improvement
  • Develop crush and refinery full cost model together with functional team ( commercial, finance)
  • Provide guidance and coaching to industrial team on data statistic methodology to make sure the reports from plants are in line with LDC Standards
  • Deeply involved in the production data visualizations by Microsoft Power BI process, coordinate and provide support between plants and IT.

Lean & Six sigma：
  • Facilitate region CI manager to implement six sigma training with industrial field
  • Carry six sigma projects to support plant KPI improvement

Experience

Education：Bachelor’s degree or above, major in industry engineering, or related is preferred.

Experience：5-7 years experience in Oilseed procced industry or fast-selling products industry. Preferred experience in CI & six sigma.

Competency：
  • Proficient in data analysis capability
  • Able to lead data-related improvement
  • Be able to work under pressure, have good communication and interpersonal skills;
  • Proficient in Microsoft software (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Power BI); Good presentation and moderation skills;
  • Nice to have: six sigma Green Belt certification;

Languages Skills: Good English verbal and Written
