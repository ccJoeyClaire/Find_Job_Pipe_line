职位简介

Algorithmic Developers design and implement the trading algorithms that drive our strategies. This role bridges quantitative insight and high-performance engineering, requiring both strong problem-solving and rigorous software development. Developers work closely with traders, researchers, and infrastructure teams in a fast-paced, collaborative environment. The ideal candidate combines analytical depth with creativity and precision.


Job requirements:
  • Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD in Computer Science or a related quantitative discipline;
  • Advanced proficiency in C++ and a commitment to robust, high-quality code;
  • Solid understanding of probability and statistics.
