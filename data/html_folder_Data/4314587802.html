职位简介

Responsibilities

• Understand the business and conduct business analysis by combining internal and external data to provide analysis support for company strategy, tactical decisions, and operational decisions

• Design a business analysis evaluation system from the perspective of business model health, including KPIs and core process monitoring indicators, and complete all tracking and analysis. Provide objective and professional evaluations of business results and give suggestions and opinions for business development, and assist with implementation

• Obtain market/industry/competitor information through various channels, establish and continually improve the analysis systems for market, industry trends, and competitor tracking. Analyze all aspects, from macro trends to micro individual strategies, core competencies, product features and experiences, users, marketing, etc. Identify opportunities and provide professional business suggestions, and assist with implementation


Qualifications

• One to five years experience in data analysis and business decision-making, with backgrounds in industries such as payment, e-commerce, internet finance, and data consulting

• Familiar with cutting-edge data solutions in the industry

• Proficient in independently writing business data analysis reports and able to communicate efficiently with business and operations teams

• Has a successful experience of data-driven business implementation, solving practical problems, creating real value, and collaborating as one of the main drivers to achieve business KPIs

• Bachelor's degree or above in relevant fields such as mathematics, statistics, computer science, strong English language skills as a working language, proficient in using SQL, Excel, and PPT

• Familiar with data analysis tools (SAS, R, Python), visualization tools (Tableau), data tools (ETL, Hive, HQL), and priority given to experience in A/B test design and testing
