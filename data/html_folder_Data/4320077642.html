WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR

We’re looking for a passionate and visionary leader in propelling Finance transformation through the orchestration of digital innovation, analytics, and intelligent automation


WHAT YOU WILL WORK ON
  • Translate Corporate and Finance strategies into actionable roadmaps, ensuring alignment with business priorities and long-term growth objectives.
  • Lead the end-to-end lifecycle of digital products (including BI, Automation, AI, etc) by applying product management principles and user-centric design, delivering scalable, data-driven finance solutions that create measurable business value.
  • Serve as a trusted partner to Finance teams and IT stakeholders, fostering seamless collaboration and shared success across functions.
  • Inspire, mentor, and develop a high-performing team, driving career growth, skill enhancement, and continuous improvement through constructive feedback.
  • Cultivate a culture of accountability, collaboration, and innovation


WHO YOU WILL WORK WITH
  • Greater China Finance and Controlling
  • Finance Data Product and Data Governance teams
  • Data Visualization / BI team
  • Technology Data & AI (D&AI) team


WHAT YOU BRING
  • Experience working in a multinational company
  • Background in financial or IT project management
  • Experience delivering digital solutions resulting in measurable reduction in effort and increase speed to insights at enterprise scale and identify and shape new business opportunities

General Skills:
  • Deep passion for driving digital transformation and relentless curiosity for emerging technologies and innovative solutions.
  • Outstanding organizational and prioritization capabilities, ensuring focus on high-impact initiatives.
  • Proven ability to simplify complexity, designing practical and scalable solutions for intricate business challenges.
  • Strong influencing and communication skills, effectively bridging technical and business stakeholders to achieve shared objectives.

Functional Skills:

Deep understanding of financial statements and finance business processes

Technical Skills:
  • Solid Understanding of Financial Principles and Analytical Techniques
  • Expert in Digital product management and development project management
  • Expertise in Requirement Discovery, Solution Evaluation, and Effort Estimation
  • Extensive Knowledge of Digital Technologies, including Databricks, Snowflake, Tableau, Cognos, RPA, AIML, etc.
