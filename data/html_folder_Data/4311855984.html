职位简介

Job Responsibilities:

1. Complete the development and testing of IOS/Android App according to the product requirements;

2. Responsible for high-quality technical design and coding, design code structure, and continuously improvements;

3. Responsible for the key and difficult technical breakthroughs of the project, as well as the pre-research of new technologies for mobile phone products.

4. Cooperate with product operation to complete the launch of major application.


Job Requirements:

1. Computer science and related majors, Bachelor Degree or above, five years or more development work experience, participated in more than 2 complete mobile Internet projects;

2. Familiar with IOS or Android development, have in-depth practice on UI, components, threads, performance and memory optimization;

3. Familiar with common protocols such as TCP, HTTP, and WebSocket;

4. Master the Flutter full-stack development framework and passionate in cross-end development;

5. Familiar with the tools and methods of mobile app performance optimization;

6. Good development foundation, understanding of common design patterns, and good practice in actual work;

7. Have a strong sense of responsibility and initiative, good communication skills and teamwork spirit;

8. Good English reading ability and new technology learning ability.


Added Advantages:

1. Has its own independent online IOS application;

2. Experience in chart and securities development is preferred.


岗位职责：

1、按产品的需求完成 IOS/Android客户端App的开发与测试；

2、负责高质量的技术设计和编码，设计良好的代码结构，不断迭代重构；

3、负责项目重点、难点的技术攻坚以及手机移动产品新技术预研等。

4、配合产品运营完成海内外各大应用市场的上架。


任职要求：

1. 计算机及相关专业，本科以上学历，五年及以上全职开发工作经验，参与过2个以上完整的移动互联网项目；

2. 熟悉IOS或Android开发，对UI、组件、线程、性能和内存优化等有深入实践；

3. 熟悉TCP、HTTP、WebSocket等常见协议；

4. 掌握 Flutter 全栈开发框架，并对跨端开发充满热情；

5. 熟练掌握移动端的App性能优化的工具和方法；

6. 良好的开发基础，理解常见设计模式，在实际工作中有很好的实践；

7. 具备较强的责任感和主动性，良好的沟通能力和团队协作精神；

8. 具备良好的英文阅读能力和新技术学习能力。


加分项：

1、有自己独立上线的IOS应用；

2、有图表、证券客户端开发经验优先。
