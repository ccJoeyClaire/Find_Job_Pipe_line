Job Description

Position Summary

We are seeking a highly experienced and strategic Principal \Senior Principal Data Scientist to join our team. The ideal candidate will bring 8+ years of hands-on experience in data science, with a proven track record in AI/ML applications within healthcare industry. This role will lead end-to-end AI/ML solution development, collaborate with KOLs, support regulatory submissions (NMPA/FDA), and collaborate cross-functionally to bring impactful AI-driven products to market.

Key Responsibilities
  • Lead the AI innovation from 0 to 1 involving design, development, validation, and deployment of AI/ML models focused on Vantive solutions (e.g. prescription, diagnostics, decision support, patient monitoring, etc).
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including regulatory, clinical, engineering, product, customer service and quality to ensure AI models meet performance, business and compliance requirements.
  • Work closely with local cross-functional teams, global teams, KOLs and 3rd party vendors to deliver AI Solutions.
  • Own AI components in regulatory documentation and submissions, including algorithm description, validation studies, risk assessments, etc.
  • Translate complex healthcare problems into AI-driven solutions with measurable clinical impact.
  • Drive data acquisition, curation, preprocessing, and pipeline optimization for training and validation datasets.
  • Stay current with latest advances in AI/ML, regulatory trends, and healthcare standards.
  • Act as a strategic partner in defining AI product roadmap and innovation strategy.
  • Ability to clean and preprocess healthcare data, including missing data imputation, outlier detection, and feature engineering






Required Qualifications
  • MSc or PhD in Computer Science, Biomedical Engineering, Data Science, Statistics, or related field.
  • 8+ years of professional experience as a AI/ML, including at least 4+ years in healthcare/medical industry.
  • Strong track record of leading or co-leading AI product development from concept to launch.
  • Experience in working on regulatory submissions involving software as a medical device (SaMD), including interaction with regulatory bodies (e.g., NMPA/FDA).
  • Deep understanding of AI/ML methodologies (supervised/unsupervised learning, deep learning, time-series, NLP, etc.).
  • Experience with advanced statistical methods, such as survival analysis, time-series forecasting, Bayesian statistics, and causal inference.
  • Strong programming skills in Python (e.g., scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch, etc.).
  • Familiarity with healthcare data standards (e.g., HL7, DICOM) and real-world data (EHR, claims, registries).
  • Solid understanding of model validation techniques, cross-validation, and hyperparameter tuning.
  • Advanced experience with SQL and database management for querying and handling large-scale datasets.
  • Ability to deploy models in production environments, using technologies like Flask, FastAPI, or other RESTful API frameworks.
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate complex technical content to cross-functional stakeholders.
  • Experience in AI for diagnostics, remote monitoring, digital biomarkers, or clinical decision support.
  • Publications or patents in applied healthcare AI.
  • Experience in GMLP (Good Machine Learning Practices) and model lifecycle management.
  • Knowledge of data privacy and compliance frameworks (e.g. China Data Privacy, HIPAA, GDPR) and best practices in managing patient data.
