职位简介

About Us

We’re a fast-moving, AI-leveraged rapid prototyping lab creating innovative products in martech, fintech, web apps, websites, crypto, and more. Our team thrives on versatility, speed, and creativity, delivering solutions that blend cutting-edge tech with real-world impact.


Job Summary

As an AI Leveraged Product Generalist, you’ll contribute across multiple areas—such as coding, design, multimedia, marketing, or project management—to build and launch prototypes and MVPs. You’ll collaborate with specialists and leverage AI tools to deliver solutions in a rapid development environment, ideally spanning several of our domains (martech, fintech, web, crypto), with the ability to cover all being a bonus but not Required.


Key Responsibilities
  • Leverage AI-driven tools (e.g., Cursor for coding, Figma AI for single domain design, Jasper for marketing and etc.) to accelerate product development across multiple domains where applicable.
  • Contribute to web apps, websites, multimedia or backend systems using modern frameworks as needed.
  • Design user interfaces, wireframes, or prototypes for intuitive products when required.
  • Create multimedia assets (e.g., videos, graphics) or marketing content to support launches as part of your skill set.
  • Manage project timelines, coordinate tasks, or define requirements in areas where you excel.
  • Deliver functional prototypes or MVPs in at least two or more domains (e.g., martech, fintech, web, crypto), with the potential to expand across all.


Qualifications
  • 2+ years of experience in at least two of: software development, UX/UI design, multimedia production, marketing, or project management. Fresh Graduates with relevant experience leveraging AI tooling will be considered.
  • Proficiency in relevant tools for your strongest areas (e.g., React, Figma, Adobe Suite, Hootsuite, Jira).
  • Familiarity with AI-driven tools (e.g., code generation, design automation, content creation) is a plus.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and a willingness to adapt across multiple domains.
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, prototype-driven setting with shifting priorities.
  • Bonus: Experience or interest in martech (e.g., adtech), fintech (e.g., payments), or crypto (e.g., blockchain).
