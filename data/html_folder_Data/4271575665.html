职位简介

Role Description

At Allegis Group | TEKsystems, marketing is about understanding the customer (clients, candidates, and technical consultants all included)—and building awareness about how our services can satisfy their needs. We’re looking for a versatile Digital Marketing professional who understands the importance of sharing valuable content to different audiences through digital platforms and driving omnichannel campaigns.


The Digital Marketing professional will play a crucial role in telling the Allegis Group | TEKsystems story and improving customer experience and engagement. Their main role will be to develop content, execute social marketing campaigns, spark meaningful discussions and conversations, supervise channel accounts, ensure compliance with brand and company standards, and work cross-functionally to deploy a strategic content calendar. This role will also help support our larger digital strategy.

Essential Responsibilities
  • Work with the China Marketing & Communications Lead and the APAC Digital Marketing team to execute digital strategy for China.
  • Manage all our social media channels including establishing new ones, lead email marketing campaigns and deliver digital content and strategies to maximse the company’s reach through social media and other online communities.
  • Understand our different customers and how each social media platform can be leveraged differently for specific results.
  • Maintain accuracy and the “unique voice” for all social media channels.
  • Develop and edit content for a variety of mediums, including articles, case studies, event materials, advertisements and web copy.
  • Update webpages to support business growth through our content management system (CMS), Sitecore.
  • Work with vendors and a variety of marketing technology tools.
  • Stay up to date on current industry trends.
  • Collaborate with the APAC marketing team as needed.
  • Take on other tasks as needed.

Minimum Education/Skills
  • Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communications or a related field.
  • In-depth knowledge and understanding of social media platforms, their respective participants (WeChat, Red Note, LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.) and how each platform can be deployed in different scenarios.
  • Knowledge in the principles of SEO and website management (CMS, Sitecore).
  • Effective interpersonal skills. Ability to establish and maintain relationship with external and internal audiences.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, having experience in graphic design and video editing favourable.
  • Exceptional communication and writing skills in near-native English and native Mandarin.
  • Prioritisation, organisation and project management skills with great attention to detail.
  • International mindset, demonstrating sensitivity to cross-cultural differences and enthusiasm for collaboration with diverse teams.
  • Proactive and results-driven mindset, with a passion for driving success in a dynamic and growing team.

Core Competencies
  • Alignment to the Allegis APAC values and beliefs. Character, Spirit, Relationships, and Serving others.
  • Effective people skills. Values and understands how to nurture professional relationships and build bridges to expand organisational network.
  • Self-direction. Has self-motivation and self-initiates new assignments and challenges, seeking out influencers and the knowledge they need to achieve their goals and progress to the next level.
  • Open mindset. Cultivates an open mindset by actively embracing new ideas, concepts and knowledge, adapting swiftly to change, and taking decisive action to achieve tangible outcomes.
  • Skills development. Has a strong desire to consistently improve/expand skills to become even better. Grows personally to help the team and company grow.
  • Desire for input. Seeks to understand what’s expected, secures regular feedback for motivation and develops new ideas/ways of working.
  • Cool under pressure. Has confidence to calmly analyze situations and solve problems, even when the deadline is quickly approaching. Knows when to compromise and when to stand firm.
