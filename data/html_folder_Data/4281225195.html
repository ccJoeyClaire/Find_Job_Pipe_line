职位简介

Littelfuse is one of America’s Best Mid-Sized Companies (Forbes) and has been named one of the Best Places to Work in Illinois (Best Companies Group) for 11 consecutive years. With its global headquarters in Chicago, Illinois, USA, Littelfuse is a leading, global manufacturer of electronic components serving more than 100,000 end customers across industrial, transportation, and electronics end markets. We have more than 17,000 employees with operations in 15 countries. From semiconductors to sensors… switches to fuses and more… we produce billions of electronic components that help our customers empower a sustainable, connected, and safer world. In 2021, Littelfuse had net sales of $2.1 billion.

Reporting to the Sr. MDE Manager, you as a Sr. Automation Engineer will be responsible for new automation machine design and existing machine modification and upgrade.

About The Job

  • New automation machine/process concept design to set up automatic process for new product & manufacture requirement
  • Existing process modification or upgrade to meet new automation manufacturing process needs
  • Automation Machine Specification set up after work with internal customer and make sure meet quality/cost requirement
  • Supplier capability review and machine specification confirm
  • Communicate with internal function team and supplier for machine design review
  • Automation project management, timeline set up, update and control to meet the delivery date
  • Work with operation and finance team to set up financial justification document for the new automation equipment
  • Support on other cost reduction/quality improvement/efficiency improvement project



About You
  • Bachelor's degree in Mechanical, automation Engineering or a related field
  • 8+ years working experience on automation machine design or automation project
  • Be familiar with all kinds of automation standard parts.
  • Strong logic analysis ability and failure diagnosis ability
  • Skillful in using the CAD software or other 3D design software is preferred.
  • Skillful in PLC program, vision system development is preferred
  • Proficient in MS Office suite
  • Basic knowledge on ISO9000 / IATF16949, automotive experience and exposure is added advantage
