Compensation Consultant

美世 · 中国 上海市 (混合办公)

职位简介

We are seeking a talented individual to join our Account Management team at Mercer China. This role will be based in Shanghai Office. This is a hybrid role that has a requirement of working at least three days a week in the office.

We will count on you to:

  • Owner of assigned industry/location survey projects
  • Achieve client satisfaction by ensuring high quality survey project delivery. Provide support to key clients. This requires the incumbent to have strong knowledge in the assigned industry/location.
  • Conduct client presentations and complex training sessions (e.g. on compensation planning and budgeting).
  • Project deliverables e.g. job matching, IPE etc.
  • Mentor/ provide coaching to less experienced consultants
  • With guidance from more experienced consultants and/or Industry/Location Leaders, develop business plan for the assigned industry/location. Establish connections with the clients’ C&B Senior Managers/Directors. Build and maintain strong client relationships in the assigned client portfolio (locations/ industries) to achieve business targets.
  • Interpret survey findings. Provide industry/location specific insights and advice. Tailor solutions to address clients’ business needs as appropriate.
  • Manage compensation and benefits related consulting projects. Leverage support from the project team.

What you need to have:

  • Bachelor degree in a relevant discipline. Master’s degree preferable.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work proactively/motivationally as an independent contributor and as part of a broader team
  • Proven interpersonal skills and ability to build successful business relationships with people at all levels
  • Highly organized and efficient project management skills
  • Solid computer skills, including Excel, Word and PowerPoint
  • Willingness to travel to different cities

What makes you stand out?
  • HR consulting or corporate HR experience at an appropriate level within a consulting/professional services environment
  • Sound client focus underpinned by strong C&B professional knowledge

Why join our team:
  • We help you be your best through professional development opportunities, interesting work and supportive leaders.
  • We foster a vibrant and inclusive culture where you can work with talented colleagues to create new solutions and have impact for colleagues, clients and communities.
  • Our scale enables us to provide a range of career opportunities, as well as benefits and rewards to enhance your well-being.

Mercer, a business of Marsh McLennan (NYSE: MMC), is a global leader in helping clients realize their investment objectives, shape the future of work and enhance health and retirement outcomes for their people. Marsh McLennan is a global leader in risk, strategy and people, advising clients in 130 countries across four businesses: Marsh, Guy Carpenter, Mercer and Oliver Wyman.
