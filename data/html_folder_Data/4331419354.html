职位简介

The team is responsible for Airstar Bank's business, covering everything from the bank's frontend customer-facing capabilities to back-end system construction.

Key Responsibilities

  • Build the financial product capabilities of Airstar bank, including capabilities in areas such as account opening, transfer, bank card, investment and wealth management, etc.;
  • Build the client and desktop products of Airstar bank and be responsible for customer experience;
  • Construction of the banking back-office system, targeting internal business colleagues, to improve the accuracy and efficiency of business processing;
  • Collect requirements from system users, promote the implementation of requirements, and continuously follow up on relevant data performance and user feedback
  • Pay attention to banking business operation data and contribute your own ideas to business growth

    • Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or above, with majors in computer science, software engineering, financial economics, mathematics preferred
  • Good communication and expression skills, rigorous logical thinking, and systematic thinking ability
  • Love product work and long-term aspiration for the technology and finance industry
  • Bonus points: Having investment experience or paying attention to the Financial Market
  • Open to candidates graduating between January 2025 and August 2026.
  • Fluent in both English & Chinese

    • *Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
