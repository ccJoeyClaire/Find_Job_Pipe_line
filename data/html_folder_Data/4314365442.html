You’ll be working with an Account Manager on day-to-day client service tasks.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Track foodservice industry market intelligence and economic indicators from multiple sources, including marketing activities, all channel market information, macro economy information, menu changes, industry reports etc.
  • Media monitoring
  • Assist the production of client deliverables
  • 3 – 5 days a week depend on workload allocation

Requirements:

  • Good command of written English at least
  • Proficient in MS Office: Excel formulas, charting, Word and PPT

  • Strong organizational skills
