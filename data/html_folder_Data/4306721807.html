职位简介

Job Description

Exciting opportunity to join our Equity Research team.

As an Analyst within the research team, you will play a pivotal role in developing models and analytical frameworks to interpret key facets of financial markets and the sector your covering. You will be an integral part of a vibrant environment, where your inputs will have a substantial impact on results and promote your career growth. Your responsibilities will include establishing strong relationships with clients and internal stakeholders, promoting a culture of innovation and excellence. This role offers a unique opportunity to hone your skills in economic analysis, econometric modeling, and market understanding, all within the context of a leading financial services firm.

Corporate and Investment Banking - Research

As one of the world’s most highly respected advisory franchises, J.P. Morgan Global Research provides thoughtful and differentiated fundamental analyses on sectors and companies, actionable ideas and thematic insights that empower our clients to make well-informed strategic and investment decisions.

Job Responsibilities

  • Contribute to research reports on a specific China sector;
  • Handle day to day client requests and needs
  • Communicate your team's ideas with a persuasive curve to internal and external parties; tailor your communications by client need/type;
  • Organize fact-finding trips and channel-check meetings upon clients’ bespoke requests or for investors tours;
  • Understands firm's approach and policies for managing risks in relevant business and applicable legal and regulatory rules, and follows policies.

Required Qualifications, Capabilities, And Skills
  • Strongly prefer working experience in research;
  • Ability to independently conduct channel checks;
  • Entrepreneurial drive with strong analytical and quantitative skills
  • Ability to complete a robust model (base and sensitivity cases) from start to finish with little guidance; mastery of the levers in the model.
  • Ability to discuss in detail you understanding of the fundamentals/drivers (competitive landscape, risks) of your industry/product
  • Ability to take disparate pieces of information, synthesize them and articulate a compelling thesis or argument in a clear and cogent manner.
  • Demonstrates creativity in solving problems. Always looks for new ways of doing things
  • Thoroughly understands drivers of relevant markets and can explain and anticipate market movements
  • Ability to thrive on the challenge of competitive pressures and remain focused on client needs
  • Ability to interact comfortably with business clients and management
  • Attainment of all necessary the China Securities Regulatory Commission (CSRC) licenses (or any other licenses / qualifications as required) for carrying out securities and other regulated activities
