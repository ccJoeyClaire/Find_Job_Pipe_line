职位简介

The Role

Monitor related cost evolution by regular checks and analysis, make it transparency. Expose cost situation, any deviation and risk to manager to support on cost control.

According to the monthly closing timeline to complete month end report of responsible part in quality and on time.

Meeting to communicate with operation departments, to help them have good understanding and actions on operation efficiency, working capital improvement etc.

Educational /work Experience Requirements

    • University graduated, bachelor’s degree.
    • At least 2 years work experience on finance or business analysis.
Knowledge Requirements
    • Experienced on SAP system FI, logistic module would be preferred
    • Experience on logistic would be preferred
    • CET 4 at least. Good command of both spoken and written English.
    • Good at MS office. (Excel analysis, scorecard, presentation skills etc.)
Other Requirements
  • Optimistic, positive. Self discipline
  • Willing be cooperated with other colleagues and good teamwork
