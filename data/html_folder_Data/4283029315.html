Job Responsibilities
  • Assist front-end engineers in page development by writing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code based on design mockups, ensuring page compatibility and aesthetic quality.
  • Participate in the daily maintenance of front-end projects, including bug fixes and code optimization, while learning the project development process and standards.
  • Assist in the development and testing of front-end components, understand component-based development concepts, and gain experience in creating reusable components.
  • Learn and apply front-end frameworks (e.g., React, Next.js) to contribute to the development of simple functional modules, gradually improving development skills.
  • Collect and report issues encountered during development, participate in team technical discussions, and share ideas and suggestions.

2. Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree or higher in computer science or a related field, with at least 3 days of availability per week and a minimum internship duration of 3 months.
  • Solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with familiarity in ES6+ syntax features.
  • Strong self-motivation, capable of proactively planning learning goals and tasks, and advancing work with minimal supervision.
  • Excellent learning ability and a keen interest in front-end development, willing to actively acquire new knowledge and skills, quickly grasp new technical concepts and business logic, and apply them effectively in development.
  • Familiarity with at least one front-end framework (e.g., React, Vue) is preferred. Candidates with personal projects (e.g., blogs, small applications) are advantageous.
  • Strong team spirit, responsible and detail-oriented attitude, with patience and perseverance.
  • A strong interest in Web3 and blockchain is a plus.
