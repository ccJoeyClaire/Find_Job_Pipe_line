职位简介

About Dida:

As the No.1 B2B outbound player in China and a leader across APAC markets, Dida has grown to become a global travel leader and the world’s fastest-growing B2B travel distributor. Our expert teams provide our partners with unparalleled access to retail travel agencies, tour operators, airlines, closed user groups and other high-value channels in some of the fastest growing, most relevant yet hard-to-reach source markets on earth.


Responsibilities

Provide professional financial support for business operations, including but not limited to:
  • Fully participate in the business operation process, understand all links of the business, and promptly provide reasonable financial solutions for financial-related issues encountered in the business process.
  • Optimize and sort out business-financial processes in the course of business operations, and put forward reasonable suggestions and plans from a financial perspective.
  • Dynamically track the operating performance of each business line, provide corresponding financial analysis reports, and support business decision-making.
  • Assist business departments in formulating budgets based on business development plans, monitor the use of budgets in real-time, and issue relevant reports.
  • Control the rationality of business expenses, review key commercial terms of contracts, sort out and standardize business processes to ensure efficient and reliable support for business operations.
  • Be responsible for responding to the daily financial needs of business departments, including but not limited to participating in discussions on business models, building and evaluating financial models, and optimizing business-related approval processes.
  • Regularly provide financial analysis reports to management, and offer relevant management suggestions and decision support.


Requirements
  • Full-time bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related fields; Intermediate Accountant qualification or CPA is preferred.
  • 3-7 years of working experience in internet companies, with relevant experience in the airline ticket industry preferred.
  • Advanced Excel skills, including the ability to create complex spreadsheets and financial models.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities, with a strong sense of attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Outstanding English written and verbal communication skills, with English as a working language.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office software (e.g., PPT, Word, etc.); experience in PowerBI and Oracle implementation is preferred.
