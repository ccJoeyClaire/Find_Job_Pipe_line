职位简介

  • Architecture Design: Quickly learn and master securities-related business knowledge and user experience design methodologies. Participate in user research and design for app navigation and global features. Combine business understanding with systematic thinking to deliver forward-thinking design solutions
  • Product Planning: Own the entire cycle for architecture-focused products, including requirements gathering, analysis, roadmap planning, and design. Produce comprehensive Product Requirements Documents (PRD) and interactive prototypes
  • Project Execution: Collaborate closely with R&D, Design, QA, and Operations teams. Coordinate resources effectively to ensure the timely and high-quality launch of products
  • Analysis & Iteration: Monitor post-launch product data and user feedback. Conduct analysis and drive continuous optimization based on insights gathered


Requirements

  • Graduating with a Bachelor's degree or higher in Finance, Interaction Design, Industrial Design, Psychology, or a related field.
  • Must be proficient in English and Chinese for professional communication
  • Demonstrable interest in securities and investing. Practical trading experience, relevant financial certifications, or prior internship experience in the securities or internet industry is a strong plus
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills, coupled with strong logical and analytical thinking abilities
  • Highly motivated to learn, with a strong sense of ownership and responsibility. Ability to work effectively under pressure and thrive in a challenging, fast-paced environment
  • Open to candidates graduating between January 2025 and August 2026.
