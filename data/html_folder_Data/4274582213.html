职位简介

Brighter futures start here

Anko Sourcing is the direct sourcing arm for the iconic Australian retail brands, Kmart and Target. As a team of highly experienced retail sourcing professionals, we strive to bring globally compelling, low-cost, on-trend products to our customers in Australia and New Zealand. We follow the highest standards to source ethically and operate with a clear focus on the welfare of our planet and our people. Anko Sourcing operations span the largest sourcing markets across Asia, including China, Hong Kong, India, Bangladesh, Singapore, Indonesia, and Vietnam.

Join The Team

What you’ll be doing:

Factory Management

To be successful in this role, you’ll have:

  • Conduct onsite technical factory audit and provide advice to the factory working out improvement plan for the audit.
  • Assist factories in developing Corrective Action Plans (CAP) and oversees implementation.
  • Assesses & improves factory Quality Management Systems.
  • Seasonal Data analysis for vendors performance based on sample performance, inspection result etc.

Product Management
  • Manage the development of the shoe product category (including QA and PP sample approval) produced in Asia, ensuring timelines, fitting (measurement for accessories) and quality standards are met.
  • Lead the Risk assessment, Line review meeting and Pre-Production Meeting with follow-up as necessary.
  • Generate standard testing procedures, coordinate on all testing schedules and results.
  • Handle customer's complaint and issues. Perform investigations and CAPs as required.
  • Provide support and technical resources /advice to merchandiser / buyer team concerning supplier corrective actions and approval.
  • Liaise with Compliance Team and 3rd party for new regulation update about product safety and compliance and organize training to all stakeholders.
  • Work with the QC team to ensure high-quality and timely delivery of bulk products each season.

Others
  • Good command of both written and spoken English. ( suggest that we should put the requirements in Traits and Abilities )
  • Frequent travel (China, Southeast Asia countries …) is required
  • Strong communication with overseas vendors / factories / Merchandiser team / Australia team.
