【About Anton】

Anton Oilfield Services Group is a globally leading independent integrated oilfield technology services company, operating across major oil and gas production regions worldwide. The business covers oilfield technical services, oilfield management services, oil and gas development operations, natural gas utilization, AI-enabled solutions, and platform collaboration. Anton Group employs approximately 7,000 staff from over 30 countries and regions. Anton’s long-term goal is to become a world-leading green energy technical services company, growing together with its clients and talent to promote high-quality development in the energy industry.


Position：Geology Expert

Location: Cheng du -China

Department: Reservoir Performance Enhancement Technology Group


Key Responsibilities:
  • Responsible for comprehensive geological studies related to oil and gas reservoir development.
  • Responsible for geological research and analysis in geology-engineering integration projects.
  • Responsible for research and analysis of technical requirements in oil and gas fields.


Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree or above in Petroleum Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Geological Sciences, or related fields.
  • At least 8 years of experience in the petroleum industry, including a minimum of 4 years in technical support roles.
  • Proficient in or familiar with various production enhancement technologies used in oil and gas field development, including acidizing and fracturing.
  • Strong cross-cultural communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with team members from different countries and regions.
  • Proficient in English; Arabic language skills are a plus.
  • Willing and able to travel internationally on a frequent basis.
