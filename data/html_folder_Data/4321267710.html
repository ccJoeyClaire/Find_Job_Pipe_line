Job Title: Product Manager, Data and AI Solutions, China, Mongolia, and South Korea

Location: Beijing, China

About Springer Nature

Springer Nature is one of the leading publishers of research in the world. We publish the largest number of journals and books and are a pioneer in open research. Through our leading brands, trusted for more than 180 years, we provide technology-enabled products, platforms and services that help researchers to uncover new ideas and share their discoveries, health professionals to stay at the forefront of medical science, and educators to advance learning. We are proud to be part of progress, working together with the communities we serve to share knowledge and bring greater understanding to the world. For more information, please visit about.springernature.com and @SpringerNature.

About The Role

As AI continues to transform the landscape of R&D, Springer Nature is dedicated to empowering researchers, scientists and engineers with a comprehensive portfolio of Data and AI solutions. These offerings enable users to unlock the full potential of data and information in the pursuit of innovation.

We are looking for a Product Manager to represent the data and AI product portfolio across China, Mongolia, and South Korea. This customer-facing role is crucial in driving business development, product management, and providing technical support to ensure sustainable revenue growth. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to innovate GTM and ensure successful product adoption and lifecycle management.

Role Responsibilities

Commercial Development:

  • Work with commercial teams on growing AI licensing revenue, such as product demo, prospect identification, solution proposal, training, usage report, user technical support, etc.
  • Support leaders in structuring and negotiating complex data licensing and strategic partnership agreements
  • Develop and validate value proposition and use cases to facilitate commercial offering in partnership with marketing and business development teams
  • Collaborate with legal, sales, content, technology, and operations teams to ensure successful implementation of licensing agreements and product launches.
  • Manage existing licensee customers, analyze performance and provide recommendations for sustainable partnership
  • Represent other products and solutions as line manager pointed

Product Strategy:
  • Conduct in-depth market and competitor analysis to identify opportunities, risks, and emerging trends in AI and data licensing
  • Build and manage relationships with Corporate R&D and AI Startups to stay on top of technical trends
  • Develop and execute portfolio strategies, aligning with current and future market needs

Product Development:
  • Build and maintain a localized roadmap for new business models and product innovation
  • Participate in product localization and development
  • Manage the full product lifecycle, from ideation to launch, evaluation, and iteration



Role Requirements
  • 3+ years of experience in product management with a commercial outlook in AI, technology, or SaaS sectors
  • Experience in content, data or IP licensing; a proven track record of sourcing, negotiating complex partnership is preferred
  • Strong technical and data science background
  • Experience with product management methodologies and tools, Agile, Aha, Jira, A/B testing, data analysis, etc.
  • Experience with GTM and new product launches
  • Bachelor’s degree is required
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills; Fluent in English
  • Self-motivated and strictly business-minded
  • Willingness to travel
  • Willingness to work with flexibility according to global hours. The overall working hour will comply to the Springer Nature policy and local law
