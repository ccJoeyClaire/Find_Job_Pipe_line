About The Team

The bioanalytical laboratory consists of three analytical platforms: biopharmaceuticals (large molecules), chemical drugs (small molecules), and immunology/immunotoxicology. It supports the entire spectrum of drug development—from early-stage research and preclinical pharmacokinetic/pharmacodynamic (PK/PD) studies to comprehensive clinical PK/PD evaluations—and facilitates global regulatory submissions for both domestic and international pharmaceutical companies. The lab is certified under OECD, FDA, and NPMA GLP standards.
