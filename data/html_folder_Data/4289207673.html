职位简介

Research Associate_ XR, Smart Glasses and cIoT, China Team


Counterpoint is a fast-growing global analyst firm. Our analysts produce a significant body of research that needs to be optimally presented. We are seeking a talented and enthusiastic research analyst to assist our research efforts.


We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Research Associate to join our team. The ideal candidate will play a pivotal role in driving insights into the XR, smart glasses, consumer IoT (Internet of Things), and ecosystem landscape by researching, analyzing, and tracking key data and trends..


Key responsibilities:
  • Data Research and Analysis: Gather, analyze, and track relevant data and information to maintain and update our proprietary databases.
  • Insights Development: Build a deep understanding of the key drivers, challenges, and opportunities in the XR supply chain and ecosystem space, and assess their impacts on overall industry trends.
  • Support for Senior Analysts: Support senior analysts to develop high-quality research reports, strengthen client and industry relationships, and assist with client engagements and consulting projects.
  • Research Development and Delivery: Contribute to creating compelling, data-driven research insights and industry viewpoints through qualitative and quantitative analysis. Deliver content for custom research projects, client requests, research blogs, reports, and whitepapers in collaboration with internal teams.
  • Other support to senior analysts when needed.


Key Skills/Experience Required:

You hold a master's or bachelor's degree, with 2+ years of relevant work experience in technology, telecom, internet, media, or finance sectors. Fresh graduates with strong potential are also encouraged to apply. Candidates from all academic disciplines are welcome, though a background in Electronics Engineering or Finance is a plus. Preference will be given to those with experience in hardware, semiconductors, or the broader pan-TMT (Technology, Media, and Telecom) sector.

In addition, we also expect the ideal candidate to demonstrate the following traits:
  • A strong sense of self-motivation with a deep interest in analyzing trends within the technology sector.
  • The ability to analyze and synthesize the available data and bring out value-added insights.
  • Exceptional analytical and critical thinking skills, with the ability to evaluate complex information and present clear, logical insights.
  • Excellent communication skills with a proven ability to collaborate effectively within diverse, international teams.
  • Fluent in both spoken and written English, with the ability to collaborate with global colleagues and clients across regions including Europe, North America, Korea, Japan, India, and other key markets.
  • Strong Microsoft Office skills especially Excel and PPT.
  • Ability to work confidently as an individual contributor under the coach of Senior Analysts.


Location
  • Preferably in Shenzhen or Hong Kong (We do not provide IANG Visa sponsorship. You must be eligible to work legally in your service region.)


Benefits & Perks:
  • Collaborate with a global network of industry pioneers and experts.
  • Engage directly with tech powerhouses like Apple, Samsung, Sony and others.
  • Cultivate your expertise through continuous learning, training and coaching.
  • Support for wellness and personal growth.
  • Progressive sabbatical opportunities.
  • Fun, vibrant work atmosphere (Frequent contests and challenges with cash gifts around the year).
  • Annual training retreats to exotic destinations (previous locations: Bali, Kuala Lumpur, Bangkok, Ko Samui, Dubai, Hongkong etc).
  • Annual support for tech gadgets such iPhones and Android smartphones.


How to apply:
  • Apply via the link (please attach your CV in English)
  • We will accept applications on a continual basis.
