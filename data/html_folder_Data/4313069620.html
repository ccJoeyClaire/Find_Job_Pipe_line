In This Role You’ll Make An Impact By

  • Leading the annual financial planning, analysis, and key business processes.
  • Optimizing resource allocation across areas to serve the best interests of the overall group.
  • Engaging in performance dialogues to ensure actions are implemented and tracked, contributing directly to the achievement of business objectives.
  • Providing tailored financial support to meet specific business needs, with a strong focus on sustainable, profitable growth.
  • Driving integration initiatives, synergy tracking, and the execution of the strategic financial agenda and key projects.
  • Partnering closely with Finance Business Partner Leads and Commercial Leadership Teams to drive financial stewardship, enhance value creation, and provide insights for a holistic view of the Business Area.
  • Delivering cross-value-chain transparency and actionable business insights by clearly outlining key assumptions, implications, and risks to support sound decision-making.
  • Actively engaging in commercial processes—including contracts, partnerships, pricing decisions, business development, demand planning, sales incentives, and strategic planning—to ensure financial alignment and impact.

To succeed in this role, you should bring the following qualifications and key competencies:
  • A degree in Business Administration, Finance, Accounting, or Economics, additional relevant certifications (e.g., CPA, CMA) are preferred but not required.
  • A minimum of 5 years of relevant experience in financial planning, management accounting, auditing, compliance, or a similar role.
  • A self-starter with strong adaptability, drive, and energy.
  • Demonstrated business acumen and commercial insight, with compliance as a foundational principle.
  • Strong presentation and communication skills, combined with a collaborative mindset and a focus on results and practical solutions.
  • A keen eye for detail, paired with the ability to maintain a strategic, big-picture view.
  • High numerical proficiency and solid technical finance skills.
  • Strong problem-solving capabilities and a continuous improvement mindset.
  • Effective at influencing and negotiating across functions and levels.
  • Able to build strong networks and communicate effectively across all organizational levels.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite, with advanced skills in Excel, PowerPoint, and Power BI.
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills, including the ability to plan, prioritize, and delegate effectively.
  • Fluent in spoken and written English; proficiency in Chinese is required, while Korean is considered a plus.

At Novonesis, we believe solutions rooted in biology are key to tackling the challenges our world faces.

Enzymes, functional proteins and microorganisms are our planet's tiniest, yet mightiest agents of change. When we leverage their power with science, we create biosolutions that transform the way we all produce, consume and live.

Today, we are 10,000 people worldwide whose expertise spans more than 30 different industries. All around the world our biosolutions already create value for thousands of customers and benefit the planet. Our solutions enable feeding and fueling the world more sustainably, from healthy and sustainable nutrition to biofuel. Biosolutions also reduce the use of fossil-based resources, chemicals, energy and water. And this is just the beginning. The potential is almost endless.

The time for biosolutions is now. And by working closely with our partners and customers, we will continue to challenge conventional thinking and transform business with biology. Together, we will create the change our world needs.
