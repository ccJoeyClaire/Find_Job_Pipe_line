Senior Analyst

Mailman, an IMG Company · 中国 上海市 (现场办公)

职位简介

Who We Are:

Mailman is Asia’s leading sports marketing agency that specialises in working with sports rights holders and brands to grow awareness, engagement and conversion primarily through digital, experiential and paid marketing. We create original content and deliver unforgettable experiences that provide value to fans, brands and rights holders across APAC.


The Role and What You'll Do:

Based in Shanghai but with responsibility across APAC and the regional leadership team in US, the Senior Analyst position represents an unrivalled opportunity to build a career within the sport and entertainment industry, and to develop an understanding of the commercial use of research and insights within the sector. Part of TKO Analytics group but with a focus on delivering on projects for Mailman across APAC, you will build an understanding of the use and application of global best-in-class, market-leading research tools and data sources across APAC markets. You will be responsible for leading projects to drive growth for the business, including delivering best-in-class data strategy solutions, creating insights and reports that deliver value across our client base, and developing a detailed understanding of fans and audiences across APAC. There will be a particular requirement for understanding fans and audiences across different APAC markets, and showcasing the differences in these markets.


The role requires a familiarity with tools and technologies both in China and across APAC. Key China platforms include KAWO, Baidu, Weibo, Xiaohulu, Feigua, Chanmama, Moonfox, Analysys and iimedia. Across APAC Global Web Index, Emplifi, YouGov, Nielsen, Euromonitor, Similarweb, Statista, SportBusiness, social listening platforms and others will be key to understanding the market landscape and deliver on the role’s requirements.


Working with Analytics group colleagues, the Senior Analyst role will offer the opportunity to be involved in developing sales narratives for sponsorship and media rights pitches, understanding category and market trends, building solutions to track performance across social media, sharing and summarising reports pertinent to the relevant business area, and developing the role of research, insights and data across the IMG business.


You will build a deep understanding of the needs of rights holders and brand within sport and entertainment, identifying opportunities to expand the support of TKO Analytics group within and outside TKO.
  • Understand the current use of tools across APAC, working closely with colleagues across TKO Analytics group to identify uses for existing tools, and areas of opportunity for new platforms.
  • Audit the reporting processes across APAC teams and create new ways of reporting for our existing clients.
  • Work on the overall data strategy (alongside the regional team in Singapore) to develop a long-term strategy for how we use data effectively.
  • Produce monthly / quarterly / annual reports across APAC markets.
  • Support and lead ad-hoc data projects to deliver value for Mailman, as well as for our local and regional clients.
  • Support the teams on new business with regional and local insights.
  • Support the Analytics team on projects across the suite of TKO businesses as required.
  • Conduct market analysis, competitive intelligence, and industry trend assessments to identify growth opportunities, mitigate risks, and inform strategic decisions.
  • Support Mailman’s business development initiatives by collaborating with team members to deliver high-impact presentations, proposals, research, and reports in both English and Chinese, tailored to Western and Eastern business contexts.


You Have These:
  • A minimum of 3+ years’ experience in APAC.
  • Undergraduate degree in marketing, economics, business or a numerate subject, or experience relevant to the role.
  • Experience of providing research and insights to rights holders in the sport, media or entertainment industry, or broader research experience with a demonstrable desire to build a career within the sport industry.
  • Natural ability to synthesize large quantities of data, draw conclusions, and present insights in a clear and compelling narrative.
  • Comfort with both analysing data and reading, summarising and writing reports specific to industry trends and themes.
  • At least a basic understanding of the major revenue streams within the sport industry.
  • A demonstrable desire to build a career in the sport industry.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment.
  • A self-starting attitude and willingness to commit to new projects.
  • Good communication skills with internal stakeholders and judgment in ambiguous situations.
  • Growth mindset.
  • Proven ability to develop high-quality decks and proposals tailored for both Chinese and Western clients, with sensitivity to cultural and business context.
  • Strong Excel(pivot tables, lookups etc).
  • Awareness and some experience using a few of Analytics key data tools: Global Web Index, YouGov Profiles, Infegy, Audiense, WARC, WGSN.
  • High level of competence MS office suite – Excel, Word, and PowerPoint
