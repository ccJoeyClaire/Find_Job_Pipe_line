职位简介

Looking for a high-performance sales role with uncapped earning potential, fast progression, and international opportunities?


Gravitas Recruitment Group — one of the world’s fastest-growing specialist recruitment consultancies — is hiring driven, ambitious sales professionals to join our Finance, Technology, and Insurance recruitment teams in Shenzhen.


We’re looking for people who thrive on targets, competition, and success — people who want to build a business within a business and see direct financial rewards for their performance.


The Role


As a Business Development Specialist, you’ll combine sales, relationship management, and market expertise to drive commercial growth. You will:
  • Develop new client relationships and expand Gravitas’ presence in your specialist market.
  • Identify and engage high-calibre candidates using channels such as Mai Mai and Lie Pin.
  • Manage the full recruitment cycle — from market mapping to offer negotiation.
  • Become a trusted market expert, advising global companies and top talent alike.
  • Build and lead your own client portfolio and revenue stream, supported by Gravitas’ world-class training and infrastructure.


Your Rewards

We reward success — not seniority. Expect fast progression, strong earnings, and recognition:
  • Competitive base salary + uncapped commission
  • Fast-track promotion based on performance
  • 20 days annual leave per year
  • Work-from-home flexibility
  • 1:1 sales coaching and mentorship
  • Industry-leading training designed to take you from consultant to director
  • Quarterly incentives & Michelin-star “Lunch Clubs”
  • Luxury incentive trips (Goa, Lapland, South Africa, Iceland, Turkey)
  • A high-energy, collaborative, and driven team culture


Who You Are
  • Degree holder (any discipline)
  • Background or strong interest in sales, recruitment, or client-facing work
  • Competitive, motivated, and target-driven
  • Excellent communicator and negotiator
  • Resilient under pressure and eager to succeed
  • Native Mandarin speaker


Why Gravitas
  • Princess Royal Training Award – Winner 2017, 2020, 2023
  • FT Future 100 List – Featured
  • Global Recruiter Awards – Best Specialist Recruitment Business (multiple years)
  • HR Vendors of the Year 2019 – Best Recruitment Firm – Banking & Financial Services
