Role Definition

This position will serve as a Digitalization Expert to support enterprise-wide digitalization projects implementation at CCMC, with primary responsibilities including CBS and SAP ME deployment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead or Support Caterpillar Standard Systems Deployment

（1）Lead or support the end-to-end deployment of enterprise digital standard systems, including requirement confirmation, plan formulation, system configuration, test execution, and go-live support.

（2）Collaborate with cross-functional teams (e.g., BR, Operations, SC, HR) to align system deployment with business processes, ensuring 100% compliance with enterprise digital standards.

（3）Establish and optimize the system deployment SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) to improve deployment efficiency and reduce post-go-live failure rates.
  • Current Systems Deep Dive to Extract Value for Full Use:

（1）Conduct in-depth analysis of the current systems (e.g., EMS…) application status, identify pain points in business processes such as data silos and inefficient operations.

（2）Design and implement optimization solutions, including module function expansion, data integration with other systems, and custom report development to tap into potential value (e.g., reducing 20% of manual data entry workload).

（3）Provide system application training and technical support to internal users, promote the deep application of system functions across the enterprise.
  • Provides CBS and SAP guidance and support on advance features, customizing, implementing and integrating.

（1）CPD 28.04.02 CBS Access and PUR.004 SOX controller, run regularly SOX control audit

（2）Develop and align CBS key parameters R&R metrics to drive clear role and responsibility.

（3）Leads CCMC campus ERP-SAP related projects to extract its value for full use
  • Transactional Process Efficiency Improvement through Digitalization

（1）Lead the full lifecycle of RPA projects, from business process analysis (identifying automation scenarios such as invoice verification, data entry) to RPA robot development, testing, deployment, and maintenance.

（2）Use RPA tools (e.g., UiPath, Power Automate, Automation Anywhere) to develop stable and efficient robots and establish a robot operation monitoring mechanism to ensure 99%+ stable operation.

（3）Collaborate with business teams to evaluate the ROI (Return on Investment) of RPA projects and continuously optimize existing RPA robots to improve automation coverage and business efficiency.
  • Advanced Statistical Modeling & Algorithm Development

（1）Independently design and develop advanced analytical models based on business decision scenarios.

（2）Proficiently use modeling tools and programming languages such as C++, C#, Python, SQL, and Excel (Advanced), and conduct model validation to ensure model stability, with model prediction error controlled within 5% for core scenarios.

（3）Translate complex model results into clear, business-oriented insights and actionable recommendations.

（4）Create interactive data visualization reports/dashboards using tools like Power BI, enabling business leaders to intuitively grasp key indicators (KPIs) and model-driven conclusions.

（5）Track the implementation effect of model-driven decisions, summarize successful cases and improvement points, and form a closed-loop of "modeling-decision-implementation-optimization“.

（6）Document modeling methodologies, data sources, and decision logic, and compile best practices to promote the replication & application of data-driven decision-making in other departments.

Key Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Information Technology, or related field; advanced degree preferred.
  • Strong knowledge of SAP and CBS and 5+ years extensive experience in promoting and implementing SAP ME and CBS systems.
  • Demonstrate good coding proficiency and be skilled in Power Bi, SQL, Python, C++, C# or other tools for data processing and visualization.
  • Excellent strategic thinking ability and a broad, enterprise-wide outlook.
  • Demonstrate strong planning and organizational skills.
  • Good cross-functional communication and coordination skills to effectively promote team collaboration.

Additional Information

The position requires the candidate to work a 5-day-a-week schedule in the office.
