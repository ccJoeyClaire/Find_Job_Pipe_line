职位简介

Job Description:

We are seeking a technically adept and marketing minded Manager (Campaign Automation and Development) to join our CRM team. This role is ideal for a candidate with a strong foundation in software architecture, API development, and data engineering, who can translate business needs into scalable, maintainable, and efficient technical solutions.

The successful candidate will lead the design and implementation of campaign automation systems, integrating APIs for content management and marketing channels, orchestrating workflows, and optimizing data pipelines. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure that technical solutions align with business objectives and deliver measurable impact.


Key Responsibilities
  • Architect and implement scalable software solutions for campaign automation, with a focus on modularity, maintainability, and performance
  • Design and develop data services to support campaign workflows and integrate with internal and external systems
  • Analyze business requirements and translate them into robust technical designs and workflow logic
  • Develop, test, and deploy reusable components using Python and SQL, ensuring high reliability and performance
  • Configure and manage Airflow environments for orchestrating ETL processes and campaign logic
  • Build and maintain data pipelines that support real-time and batch processing for CRM campaigns
  • Collaborate with business stakeholders to align technical solutions with strategic goals
  • Monitor system performance and proactively optimize workflows and APIs for scalability and efficiency
  • Troubleshoot complex issues across the software stack and provide expert-level support
  • Maintain comprehensive documentation of architecture, APIs, and workflow processes


Required Skills and Experience:
  • Proven experience in software architecture and API development, especially in Python-based environments
  • Strong proficiency in Python, SQL, and workflow orchestration tools like Airflow
  • Experience designing and deploying RESTful APIs and integrating with third-party services
  • Familiarity with Linux environments, DevOps practices, and development tools such as VSCode
  • Solid understanding of ETL processes, data modeling, and pipeline optimization
  • Strong business acumen with the ability to align technical solutions with organizational goals
  • Flexibility and adaptability to handle changing priorities and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and a proactive mindset for continuous improvement
  • Effective communication and collaboration skills across technical and non-technical teams
  • Proficiency in English and Cantonese/ Mandarin


Preferred Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, software engineering, or a related field
  • Minimum 5 years of experience in software development, data engineering and data services, with a focus on business process optimization
  • Experience with marketing automation platforms, data visualization tools, and agile methodologies
  • Demonstrated success in driving process improvements and delivering business value through technology
  • Experience in the integrated resort or hospitality industry is a plus


***Less experienced will be considered as Assistant Manager
