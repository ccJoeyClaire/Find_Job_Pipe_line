Position Responsibilities

  • Work with Advanced Analytics team, support whole Manulife departments in different countries.
  • Strong ability to extract, transform and stage data for consumption.
  • Develops and implements ad-hoc business analytics and reporting on projects of moderate scope.
  • Works on problems of moderate scope / complexity where analysis of situations or data requires a review of a variety of factors.
  • Demonstrates the ability to generate relevant, actionable insights based on iterative data analysis.
  • Communicates business analytics of moderate complexity to more senior team members and junior business partners.

Required Qualifications
  • Minimum Bachelor's degree in quantitative discipline with 3 years of applicable experience or a combination of education and experience
  • Deep understanding of one programing language such as (Python / R / C ++)
  • Advanced skill on chart design
  • Knowledge of Machine Learning models / Deep Learning models
  • Knowledge of data visualization tools
  • Experience and deep understanding of relational databases ( SQL / HDFS )

Preferred Qualifications
  • Python
  • SQL&Hive
  • Machine Learning Algorithm
