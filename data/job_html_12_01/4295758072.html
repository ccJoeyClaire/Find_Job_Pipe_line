Financial Reporting Specialist

职位简介

JD

  • Responsible for the preparation and review of monthly/quarterly/annual consolidated financial statements and notes for the company's Europe-Africa sector, ensuring compliance with IFRS and group policies.
  • Responsible for the preparation and maintenance of consolidation working papers (trial balance mapping, elimination entries, intercompany reconciliations), ensuring traceability and accuracy.
  • Conduct quality control and verification of financial data submitted by Europe-Africa subsidiaries, promptly identifying and resolving data anomalies and reporting discrepancies.
  • Assist in the initial accounting treatment and consolidation of newly established subsidiaries/projects within the Europe-Africa region.
  • Provide financial analysis support required by management (expense structure, profit contribution, intercompany transactions, etc.).
  • Liaise with internal and external auditors.
  • Continuously optimize existing consolidation processes, improve Excel templates, internal checklists, and process documentation, and support the implementation of consolidation systems.



Profile
  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Finance, Accounting, Auditing, or a related field.
  • Experience in one of the Big Four accounting firms (Audit/FAAS/consolidation reporting support) or in group consolidation/reporting at a multinational corporation. In-house reporting experience is a plus.
  • Practical experience with ERP/SAP/Oracle is preferred. Experience in independently completing consolidation and accounting work in environments with underdeveloped systems is a plus.
  • Strong communication skills in both Chinese and English, with the ability to collaborate with headquarters and regional subsidiary finance personnel.
  • Willingness to accept short-to-medium-term business trips to Europe.

国轩高科股份有限公司创立于2006年，总部位于安徽合肥，是具有全球竞争力的新能源电池企业和绿色能源解决方案商。于2015年5月成功上市，股票代码SZ:002074.。

公司主营磷酸铁锂材料及电芯、三元材料及电芯、动力电池组、储能电池组及电池管理系统等，产品广泛应用于乘用车、商用车、专用车等新能源汽车领域，并为储能客户提供绿色能源系统解决方案。

国轩高科是重技术型公司。研发投入占比多年超10%，在全球布局八大研发中心、四大验证平台，研发人员突破7000人，创造了10000多项覆盖电池全产业链的全球专利技术，国轩高科标准电芯获大众汽车全球首家定点，“金石电池”进军固态电池领域，实现从0到1的创新。

国轩高科是高国际化电池企业。德国大众是国轩高科第一大股东，布局中国、亚太、美洲、欧非四大业务板块，建有覆盖电池材料、电芯制造、Pack产品的全球二十个制造基地，致力于让绿色能源服务人类。

国轩高科是全产业链电池企业。构建从电池原料的矿产开采、材料生产、电池制造、产品应用，再到电池回收利用的垂直一体化产业链，是全球极少数拥有全产业链制造能力的电池企业之一。

公司秉承“让绿色能源服务人类”的使命，坚持产品为王、人才为本、用户至上，积极构建以材料科学和数字科学为基础的能源科学体系。未来，公司将加速提升市场力、产品力、制造力和资本力，发展成为全球领先的绿色能源解决方案商。 …
