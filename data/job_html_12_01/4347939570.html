Fresh Grad Hire - Full Stack Developer (Data Platform)

Futu Holdings Limited · 中国 广东省 深圳 (现场办公)

职位简介

We are the Data Platform Tooling team, focused on developing internal platforms and tools that enhance the productivity of data R&D and operations. Our goal is to make data usage and management simpler and more reliable, providing a first-class tooling experience for data developers and analysts across the company.

Key Responsibilities

  • Participate in designing, developing, and maintaining core tools and systems for the data platform (e.g., Data Development IDE, Job Scheduling System, Metadata Management, Data Governance Platform)
  • Optimize the usability, performance, and stability of data tools to improve developer productivity and user satisfaction
  • Deeply understand data R&D workflows and pain points, automating and intelligentizing processes through technical solutions
  • Collaborate closely with data development and data warehouse teams to build an integrated data platform
  • Write high-quality technical documentation and participate in code reviews and best practice promotion


    • Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or above in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
  • Solid programming skills, proficiency in at least one backend development language and its related tech stack (e.g., Java/Golang/Python)
  • Familiarity with front-end technologies (React/Vue) or full-stack development experience is a plus
  • Understanding of basic big data concepts and common components; strong interest in improving developer tools
  • Excellent problem-solving and innovative thinking skills, capable of independently designing and developing modules
  • Good product sense and teamwork spirit, with a focus on user experience
  • Open to candidates graduating between January 2025 and August 2026
  • Fluent in both English & Chinese

    • *Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
