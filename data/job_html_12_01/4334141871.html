职位简介

Schaeffler is a dynamic global technology company and its success has been a result of its entrepreneurial spirit and long history of private ownership. Does that sound interesting to you? As a partner to all of the major automobile manufacturers, as well as key players in the aerospace and industrial sectors, we offer you many development opportunities.

Global Position Name

Data Scientist

Responsibilities

  • For this position, you will design and develop AI/ML solutions using a combination of traditional machine learning, deep learning, large language models (LLMs), generative AI, and intelligent agents to address various manufacturing challenges. Typical tasks include:
    • Developing predictive maintenance models using sensor data and time series forecasting techniques.
    • Implementing quality prediction and defect detection models leveraging computer vision and deep learning.
    • Applying large language models and generative AI for intelligent knowledge management, natural language understanding, and decision support.
    • Creating intelligent agents to optimize production scheduling and operational workflows.
    • Collaborating with plant engineers and domain experts to identify data-driven opportunities and validate AI models in real manufacturing settings.
  • Communication: You will visualize, explain, and present AI/ML results, from traditional models to advanced generative AI solutions, to ensure clear understanding by business stakeholders.
  • Implementation: You will work closely with software and automation teams to integrate AI solutions into manufacturing execution systems for real-time production support.
Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s degree or above in Computer Science, AI, Data Science, Applied Mathematics, Mechanical Engineering, or related disciplines.
  • 5+ years of professional experience in AI/ML, including traditional machine learning, deep learning, and modern large model/LLM technologies; manufacturing domain experience is preferred.
  • Proficient in Python and familiar with machine learning libraries such as scikit-learn, XGBoost, LightGBM, as well as deep learning frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow.
  • Experience with large language models (e.g., GPT, BERT), generative AI frameworks, and transfer learning techniques.
  • Hands-on with typical manufacturing datasets: time series sensor signals, image/video data for vision tasks, text data for NLP applications.
  • Ability to design, train and deploy AI models that integrate multi-source heterogeneous data for manufacturing problem-solving.
  • Strong communication skills in English, both written and oral.
  • Proactive, collaborative, analytical mindset with good problem-solving skills and ability to work under pressure.
