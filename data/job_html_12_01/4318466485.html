职位简介

Join EPAM China as a Data Analytics And Automation Consultant in Beijing.

You will leverage AI and ML technologies to analyze wireless testing data, generate insightful reports and develop automation solutions. This role offers the chance to work with global teams, shape regulatory standards and grow your skills in a dynamic, innovative environment.

Primary Skills:
  • Data Analysis Coding
  • Automation Development
  • Webpage Design And Reporting

Responsibilities
  • Conduct AI/ML studies on wireless testing reports, regulations and standards from various countries
  • Generate reports using AI/ML technologies including Large Language Models
  • Perform testing data process from various market devices for wireless performance
  • Work with test labs to optimize data summaries and reporting
  • Develop automation software for regulatory testing processes

Requirements
  • Strong coding experience for data analysis and automation tasks
  • Webpage design experience and skills in report generation
  • Basic knowledge of RF test instruments for Wifi, BT and Cellular technologies
  • Proven planning, interpersonal skills and ability to work in multi-functional teams across time zones
  • Self-motivated with proficient English speaking and writing abilities
