职位简介

Jump Trading Group is committed to world class research. We empower exceptional talents in Mathematics, Physics, and Computer Science to seek scientific boundaries, push through them, and apply cutting edge research to global financial markets. Our culture is unique. Constant innovation requires fearlessness, creativity, intellectual honesty, and a relentless competitive streak. We believe in winning together and unlocking unique individual talent by incenting collaboration and mutual respect. At Jump, research outcomes drive more than superior risk adjusted returns. We design, develop, and deploy technologies that change our world, fund start-ups across industries, and partner with leading global research organizations and universities to solve problems.

We are seeking to hire a candidate with strong financial operations, management and business analysis skills to join our global Clearing team based in Shanghai, China. You will be part of a group that ensures the reliable flow of our trading activity between our various trading venues and clearing firms across the globe and will assist with the development of new business and back office initiatives.

What You'll Do:

  • Learn and support critical daily Clearing functions, including trade capture, lifecycle management, reconciliation and break resolution across all products and venues
  • Assist with bringing new business (i.e. new products, markets, strategies, brokers, etc.) online
  • Assist with the continued development of Jump’s back-office infrastructure and finding value-add opportunities to further leverage this infrastructure
  • Assist with identifying and improving process deficiencies
  • Assist in creating and/or improving written processes and procedures
  • Work closely with traders, understand trading workflows, and build support tailored to their needs.
  • Strong collaboration with developers, including providing clear specifications and ensuring follow-through to meet business requirements.
  • Regular communication with counterparties, prime brokers, traders, and various internal operations support teams in multiple regions
  • Other duties as assigned or needed.
  • Able to work on public holidays.

Skills You’ll Need:
  • At least 7+ year’s work experience preferred in a related capacity. Experience managing an operations team or sub-team, a plus
  • Deep knowledge of post-trade lifecycles of Equities, Treasuries, Futures, FX, Options, and/or Swaps, etc.
  • Experience with external relationship management, including technology vendors, brokers, banks, etc.
  • Strong project management and business analysis experience within financial operations, with demonstrated examples improving processes, supporting bringing new business online and working with Technology to build back office infrastructure.
  • A strong attention to detail and desire to maintain accuracy while processing large volumes of data under strict time constraints
  • Excellent organizational skills with the ability to exercise good judgment and pro-active decision-making in a fast paced and dynamic environment
  • Accomplished communication skills, with a demonstrated ability to effectively communicate (written and verbal) in English and Mandarin to both internal and external contacts on a global basis
  • Proven ability to maintain composure during stressful situations
  • Minimum of Bachelors’ degree in Business, Economics, Finance or related fields
  • Reliable and predictable availability
