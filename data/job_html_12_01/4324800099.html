Work you'll do

  • Conduct in-depth qualitative and quantitative analyses of clients’ financial, operational, and user data by leveraging statistical methods and big data techniques. Identify key risk indicators (KRIs) and support audit teams in performing risk assessment procedures
  • Check data integrity by validating computational accuracy and completeness in both operational and financial data, while detecting diverse risks across internal and external data sources through multidimensional data verification processes
  • Independently prepare audit workpapers and reports aligned with Deloitte standards. Actively contribute to core tasks throughout the project lifecycle, including summarizing interview notes, collecting documentation, creating process flow diagrams, and executing multi-dimensional data analysis
  • Proactively identify and explore innovative applications of cutting-edge technologies within audit workflows. Provide valuable insights and professional support to technology innovation teams, driving advancements in intelligent auditing capabilities and fostering continuous improvement in audit processes
  • Produce a high-quality work products and collaborate with others across phases of the project lifecycle to deliver a superior client experience
  • Manage work in an ethical manner with continuous attention to quality standards and risk rules, and maintaining confidentiality

During your tenure with us, you will demonstrate and develop your leadership and professional capabilities in the following areas: Inspiring, Creating purpose, Driving agility, Building diverse capability, Influencing, Collaborating, Delivering value, Building the business, Analytical acumen, Effective communication, Engagement management/delivery excellence, Managing change, Managing quality & risk, Strategic thinking and problem solving, and Tech savviness.

We are looking for someone with

To be considered for this position, you must demonstrate skills and experience in the following areas:
  • Bachelor or equivalent degree in statistics, mathematics, computer science, or a related area
  • At least 3 years of experience in finance, accounting, external audit, data analysis or a related field (including finance systems)
  • Demonstrates understanding of their role in planning the audit, performing tests of controls, and/or performing substantive procedures
  • Leverages technology to capitalize on the power of analytics and choose the appropriate technique for the underlying data set
  • Well-developed relationship management skills including teamwork and collaboration
  • Understands the limitations in analysis software and required input data format and is able to tweak the dataset to derive an effective visual
  • Fluent in at least 1-2 programming languages
  • Proficient in SQL, Python, R, Microsoft Power BI, Word, Excel and PowerPoint etc.
