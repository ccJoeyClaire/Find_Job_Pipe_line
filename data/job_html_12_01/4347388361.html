职位简介

Markem-Imaje is a trusted world manufacturer of product identification and traceability solutions, offering a full range of reliable and innovative inkjet, thermal transfer, laser, print and label application systems. Markem-Imaje delivers fully integrated solutions that enable product quality and safety, regulatory and retailer compliance, better product recalls and improved manufacturing processes.

The Role

This This person will work with the business, coordinating with Global and Regional FP&A to provide deep finance support on a narrow set of operational decisions, requiring specific/technical expertise.

  • Deeper Work with the FP&A team and cross-functional stakeholders in building bottom-up budget development
  • Financial modeling for business cases and discount.
  • Variance analysis (OPEX)

  • Support commercial leader in the annual target setting review, preparation of local partners rebate payout
  • Service local productivity plan support productivity improvement plan execution
  • Continuously support work practices and processes enhancement across functions for operational efficiency

  • Bachelor's degree or MBA in Finance or Accounting, with at least 5 years' relevant experience.

  • Power BI experience, preferably at intermediate level.
  • Ability to communicate effectively in English and Mandarin (written and verbal) to support Mandarin-speaking countries and stakeholders.

  • Strong logical and analytical skills, with business acumen.
