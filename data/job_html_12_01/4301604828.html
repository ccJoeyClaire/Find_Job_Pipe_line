职位简介

Job Responsibilities:

  • Audit the company’s overseas store renovation and other engineering projects, covering key stages such as design, bidding, construction, acceptance, and settlement. Also, conduct audits of internal control systems and process compliance
  • Identify potential issues and risks during the audit and propose corrective actions. Continuously track the implementation of these corrective actions

Qualifications:

  • At least 5 years of experience in overseas engineering auditing or related fields such as cost engineering or project management. Ability to quickly adapt to new work environments, independently plan auditing strategies, and complete overseas engineering audits
  • Experience in auditing store renovation projects for well-known retail brands in Southeast Asia, Europe, or the United States. Familiarity with the key processes, points, and market trends of store renovation projects in major countries is preferred
  • Professional certifications such as Cost Engineer, Construction Supervision Engineer, or other relevant engineering qualifications are preferred
  • Proficiency in English as a working language
