Job Title

Operations Analysis Manager

Company

Prologis

Major Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with relevant business teams to perform specialized analysis on key business challenges, providing strategic analysis and recommendations over a broad range of management issues at corporate level
  • Understands the company’s priorities, business drivers, competitors and competitive strategy to help drive strategically aligned solutions, considering aspects of risk/reward.
  • Define analytical approach;
  • Brings structure and order to undefined problems and/or large scale problems, making them easier to address and solve
  • Articulates or translates complex information in clear, meaningful and structured way to suit audience

Requirements
  • Outstanding academic record with Master or Bachelor’s Degree, preferably in Real Estate, Business, Finance or adjacent study area, oversea study experience is preferred.
  • Management consulting or corporate internal consulting background would be a plus;
  • Superior analytical thinking, attention to detail, and problem-solving capability.
  • Demonstrated skill in navigating ambiguity and managing complexity.
  • Ability to grasp primary real estate market economics - market demand and supply, vacancy, absorption, micro and macro issues relating to properties.
  • Experience defining requirements and using data and metrics to draw business insights
  • Excellent English verbal and written skills.
  • Good communication skills
  • Good presentation and storytelling capability
  • Good team spirit and cross-team collaboration skills
