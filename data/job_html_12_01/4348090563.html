We are responsible for building the capabilities of financial securities platforms, providing secure, stable, highly specialized, and scalable financial securities systems for the company's international market expansion. Key Responsibilities

  • Participate in the design and development of financial systems, including trading systems (including algorithmic trading, advanced order management), risk control systems (including derivatives risk control), clearing and settlement systems, etc
  • Learn and apply the latest financial technologies and participate in innovation projects

  • Internship Experience: Prior internship experience in FinTech, investment banking, or related fields is advantageous
  • Personal Attributes: Strong learning ability and problem-solving mindset, responsible, and capable of adapting to a fast-paced work environment
  • Language Skills: Excellent English & Chinese communication skills (verbal and written), capable of fluent daily and technical communication
  • Open to candidates graduating between January 2025 and August 2026.
