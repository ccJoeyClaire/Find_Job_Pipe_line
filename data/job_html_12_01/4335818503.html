Your Role

Our Corporate Actuarial Service Life/Health Unit is seeking to appoint a dynamic individual as Actuarial Analyst to work in our Shanghai office. In this role, you will support the reserving activities as well as actuarial and financial reporting for Life/Health business in China.


Your Key Responsibilities
  • Support the actuarial reporting under local statutory/IFRS bases and solvency regime
  • Support the reserve calculation (e.g., Mathematical Reserve/IBNR) under German GAAP
  • Support AXIS modelling and maintenance of AXIS model for local statutory (CROSS) and IFRS 17 reporting
  • Support the IFRS 17 implementation in China
  • Conduct, review or validate experience studies to develop reserving bases and best estimate bases
  • Participate in regional and international initiatives and projects


Your Profile

  • A near qualified Actuary with 2 - 5 years of relevant work experience in a life insurance or reinsurance company in China
  • Working knowledge of statutory or local GAAP reporting requirements in China
  • Solid knowledge and prior experience in AXIS or other actuarial software (e.g., Prophet)
  • Proficient in coding (e.g., Python or R), database (e.g.MS Access, SQL) and reporting tools (e.g., Power BI)
  • Good command of Chinese and English, both spoken and written
  • Strong attention to detail and sense of accountability
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple priorities

Our direct business model ensures our clients have direct access to decision makers, and underwriting, actuarial and claims experts, who understand and support their business objectives.

Our professionals bring a combination of local knowledge and global strength to our clients. We work closely with our clients to understand their objectives so that we can provide relevant capacity and differentiated services to them.
