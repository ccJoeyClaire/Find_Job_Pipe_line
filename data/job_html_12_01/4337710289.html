Cost Analyst

Analyzes and reviews financial statements to calculate manufacturing costs. Identifies areas that are increasing manufacturing costs more than planned. Summarizes cost analysis findings into a presentation for monthly reporting.

About The Job

  • Analyzes financials and manufacturing data to calculate manufacturing costs of products
  • Records cost information and reviews audit reports pertaining to cost
  • Prepares reports comparing projected manufacturing costs vs actual manufacturing costs; recommends changes, where appropriate
  • Evaluates internal procedures and analytical statements to recommend changes to improve administration and analysis of data in a Littelfuse location



About You
  • Bachelor or university degree. Preferred in Finance, Accounting, or related field. MBA preferred
  • Two to five years of experience
  • Knowledge of manufacturing and manufacturing cost analysis
  • Experience with global manufacturing companies preferred
  • Advanced knowledge in MS Office
  • Strong analytical skills
  • Strong communication skills
  • High attention to detail
