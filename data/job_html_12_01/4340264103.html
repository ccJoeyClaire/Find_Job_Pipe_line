About The Role

The Senior Financial Analyst (SFA) will play a critical role in supporting the Business Unit (BU) Controlling team by delivering accurate financial reporting, insightful analysis, and actionable recommendations. This position partners closely with BU leaders and cross-functional teams to drive business performance and support strategic decision-making.

What You Will Do
  • Prepare and deliver monthly financial reports for executive management review.
  • Support BU leaders with monthly reporting, variance analysis, and performance insights.
  • Analyze and evaluate BU operating results using COPA and Darwin systems; identify root causes and drive resolution of issues.
  • Assess regional BU performance against Plan and Forecast, explaining key variances and trends.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional and cross-divisional teams to provide financial analysis and support decision-makin.
  • Conduct product line portfolio and profitability analysis to optimize business outcomes.
  • Assist in the preparation of BU budgets and forecasts; perform actual vs. plan variance analysis.
  • Monitor cost center performance against Plan and Forecast and conduct gap analysis.
  • Lead and support ad hoc projects as required.

What You Need To Be Successful
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business, Finance, or related field.
  • 5+ years of relevant experience.
  • Strong analytical, written, verbal, and organizational skills.
  • Advanced proficiency in Excel and PowerPoint.

Moderate levels of stress may occur at times, especially during budgeting season.
