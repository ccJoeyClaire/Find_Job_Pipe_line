职位简介

Position Overview（药企背景优先考虑）


This role is cross-disciplinary opportunity to work at the intersection of pharma/medical devices regulatory affairs, life sciences digital solutions, corporate strategy, and B2B marketing.


The successful candidate will provide analytical and operational support for China strategy and growth team, while also assist and support marketing, business development, and other related tasks.



Key Responsibilities:
  • Conduct in-depth primary and secondary market research focused on the China life science regulatory environment, identifying trends, challenges, and opportunities.
  • Develop detailed profiles of target client segments, including pharmaceutical, biotech, and MedTech companies, understanding their regulatory needs and pain points.
  • Support and assist the Director with research, data gathering, and financial analysis, synthesize complex data into clear, actionable intelligence reports, dashboards, and presentations to support decision-making across business, operational, and marketing topics.
  • Develop and execute integrated B2B marketing campaigns to generate leads and build brand awareness within the life science sector.
  • Support marketing and business development by providing intelligence for pitches and proposals, representing the company at industry conferences and events (as required).
  • Measure and report on the performance of marketing campaigns, leveraging insights to optimize future activities.


Qualifications:
  • 5-8 years of relevant experience in the pharmaceutical industry (pharmaceuticals/medical devices), with 1~3 years of market research & analysis experience. Understanding of drug research and development, clinical development, regulatory requirements, and the pharmaceutical market.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, communication and execution skills.
  • Direct experience in a regulatory affairs role in multinational corporation (MNC) is an advantage.
  • Bilingual proficiency in Mandarin Chinese and English required.
  • Ability to work effectively across cultures, time zones and functions.
  • Master's degree or above degree in life sciences, pharmacy, medicine, or related fields.
