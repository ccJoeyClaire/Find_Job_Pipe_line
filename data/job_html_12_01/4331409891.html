职位简介

Job Description – Marketplace Strategy, DiDi International Food Delivery

Team Mission

The International Food Delivery Marketplace Strategy team sets the company-wide playbook for delivery strategy, strategic decision support, and marketplace operations. We turn data into actionable insights that accelerate growth across all overseas markets.

What You’ll Do

Insight Generation

Build a deep understanding of our logistics network; combine internal data mining with external best-practice research to produce compelling business insights that shape mid- and long-term planning.

End-to-End Strategy Delivery

Lead or co-lead high-impact projects in smart pricing & incentive algorithms and fulfillment strategy. Partner with Operations, Product, R&D and Data Science from problem definition through experimentation to full rollout.

Capability Building

Codify marketplace know-how into playbooks and toolkits that help regional teams master supply-demand dynamics and improve cross-team collaboration at scale.

What You Bring

4+ years in business analytics, management consulting, investment banking or equivalent.

Demonstrated ability to frame problems, build analytical models (SQL, Excel mandatory; Python a plus), and turn findings into board-ready stories (PPT).

Self-starter with continuous-learning mindset; comfortable in fast-paced, ambiguous environments.

Truth-seeking, open-minded, resilient; excellent communication skills.

Language: fluent English is required; international study / work experience preferred.
