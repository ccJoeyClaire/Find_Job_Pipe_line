Overview

As a Specialist, Business Insights, you will collaborate closely with various teams within Commercial department to support their data needs. Your core responsibility is to transform commercial and customer data into actionable insights that drive business growth and enhance commercial performance. You will design, develop, and manage dashboards and reports, ensuring that data is presented clearly, accurately, and in a timely manner to empower informed decision-making across the organization.

Role Purpose

Dashboard Creation and Reporting

  • Develop and manage performance dashboards for the Commercial team, ensuring timely updates and accuracy.
  • Build tailored visualizations that enhance data interpretation and supports decision-making and performance accountability.
  • Develop reports and presentations that are concise, easy to understand, and effectively convey insights and recommendations to stakeholders.

Market Research, Competitive Analysis and Customer Insights
  • Analyse historical data to support decision making and assist to form recommendation on revenue and pricing strategy. Propose and evaluate actions to improve commercial performance.
  • Conduct market landscape research including competitive analysis, desktop research and benchmarking exercises.
  • Support to conduct in-depth research to identify emerging trends and consumer preferences.

Database Management and Maintenance
  • Work closely with Data Analytics team and support the formulation of data structure.
  • Support data extraction and ensure data integrity, accuracy, and security.
  • Propose improvements to database functionality, such as automation, data integration, and enhanced reporting capabilities.

Data Analysis and Insights Generation
  • Analyse data from direct channels and other data sources to identify patterns, trends, and opportunities. Identify anomalies, and flag potential areas of concern. Prepare regular reports with insights and recommendations for optimisation.
  • Utilize Customer Experience Analytics and Digital Analytics tools to analyse digital experience, track user sessions, identify pain points, and uncover opportunities.
  • Implement data-driven improvements: Collaborate with the Digital Experience team to implement or test changes based on insights from aiming to improve website and app performance and drive conversion.
  • Analyze data from journey experience surveys and brand health trackers to identify key trends and issues, enabling timely actions and guiding long-term improvements through regular reporting.

职位:商业数据与洞察专员

地点: 深圳

汇报对象:商业数据与洞察高级经理/经理

作为商业数据与洞察专员，您将与商务部团队密切协作，了解并满足他们的数据需求。您的核心职责是将商业与客户数据转化为可执行的洞察，推动业务增长并提升商业绩效。您将设计、开发和管理仪表板与报告，确保数据以清晰、准确、及时的方式呈现，以支持全组织的知情决策。

职责与工作内容:

Dashboard 仪表板创建与数据报告（商务洞察方向）

  • 负责商业团队的仪表板创建与管理，确保数据更新及时、准确性高
  • 根据业务需求定制可视化呈现，提升数据解读效率，支持决策与绩效追踪
  • 编制简明扼要的商业报告与简报，清晰传达洞察与建议，突出关键发现、影响与行动建议

市场研究、竞争分析与客户洞察
  • 通过市场研究、竞争对手分析和客户洞察，支持商业决策与策略制定，辅助决策并提出定价策略的优化建议
  • 进行市场景观研究，包括竞争分析、市场环境评估、桌面研究和基准测试
  • 支持开展深入研究，识别新兴趋势与消费者偏好

数据库管理与性能优化
  • 与数据分析团队密切合作，支持数据结构的制定与改进
  • 协助数据提取，确保数据完整性、准确性与安全性
  • 提出数据库功能改进建议，如自动化、数据集成和增强报告能力

数据分析与洞察报告撰写
  • 分析来自直接渠道及其他数据源的数据，识别模式、趋势与机会，标注异常并提出改进建议
  • 借助客户体验分析和数字分析工具，分析体验、数字足迹、识别痛点并挖掘机会
  • 与数字体验(客户体验)团队合作，基于洞察执行或测试改动，以提升网站和应用性能并推动转化。
  • 分析客户旅程体验、品牌健康度评估等数据，识别关键趋势，定期汇报并推动持续优化及长期改进
