职位简介

Company Description

In GfK’s Market Insights business, we help our clients understand the dynamics of their marketplace including pricing, promotions, distribution and market share. Up-to-date retail sales data reveal how products are performing in the market.

Job Description

  • Assessment and optimization of data sample quality; 2.Monitor the product data quality of various categories (processing the sales data of offline stores to check whether there are outliers and whether the data is reasonable)
  • Create and maintain data in the system for items required for reporting;
  • Complete the inspection, maintenance and update of the total data;
  • Report design and production.

Qualifications
  • Bachelor degree or above, major in statistics, information management, mathematics is preferred, good skill of Excel\SQL;

2.2-3 years of data processing experience, have a certain understanding of durable consumer goods market, including: IT, communication and 3C digital products, household appliances, kitchen supplies, automotive supplies, personal care products, etc.
  • Strong data sensitivity and logical thinking ability;
  • Good communication and collaboration skills, team spirit and awareness, patient and careful.
